<h2><span id="槪要(개요)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E5%BF%83%E5%81%9C%E6%AD%A2&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要(개요)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>心停止</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>심정지</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>란 <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%BF%83%E8%87%9F&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="心臟 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade32 unknown"><rb>心臟</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>심장</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>이 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>停止</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>정지</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하는것, <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>卽</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>즉</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>正常的</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>정상적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이며 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>有效</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>유효</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한<ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>循環</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>순환</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>維持</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>유지</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하지못하는 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>現象</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>현상</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 말한다. 빠른 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>時間內</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>시간내</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%BF%83%E8%82%BA%E8%98%87%E7%94%9F%E8%A1%93&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="心肺蘇生術 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade32 unknown"><rb>心肺蘇生術</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>심폐소생술</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>을 하지않으면 <a href="/wiki/%E6%AD%BB%E4%BA%A1" title="死亡"><ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>死亡</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사망</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>에 이를 수 있다.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/%E8%8B%B1%E8%AA%9E" title="英語"><ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>英語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>영어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>로는 cardiac arrest<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>라 하며, <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>日常的</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일상적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로는 <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%BF%83%E8%87%9F%E9%BA%BB%E7%97%BA&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="心臟麻痺 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade10 unknown"><rb>心臟麻痺</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>심장마비</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>라고도 한다.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>
</p>
