<h2><span id="槪要(개요)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E6%BC%A2%E5%AD%97%EC%9C%84%ED%82%A4&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要(개요)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>漢字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>위키는 <a href="/wiki/%E9%9F%93%E5%9C%8B%E8%AA%9E" title="韓國語"><ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>韓國語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한국어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> <a href="/wiki/%EC%9C%84%ED%82%A4%EC%9C%84%ED%82%A4" title="위키위키">위키위키</a> 사이트이다. <a href="/wiki/%E9%9F%93%E5%9C%8B%E8%AA%9E" title="韓國語"><ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>韓國語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한국어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>를 <a href="/wiki/%E5%9C%8B%E6%BC%A2%E6%96%87%E6%B7%B7%E7%94%A8" title="國漢文混用"><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>國漢文混用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>국한문혼용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>으로 적은, 누구나 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>參與</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>참여</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해서 <ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>編輯</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>편집</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 및 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>修正</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>수정</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 할 수 있는 <a href="/index.php?title=%E7%99%BE%E7%A7%91%E4%BA%8B%E5%85%B8&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="百科事典 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade52 unknown"><rb>百科事典</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>백과사전</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>을 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>志向</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>지향</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한다.
</p>
