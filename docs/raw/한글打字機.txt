<h4><span id="두벌式(식)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".EB.91.90.EB.B2.8C.E5.BC.8F.28.EC.8B.9D.29">두벌<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%ED%95%9C%EA%B8%80%E6%89%93%E5%AD%97%E6%A9%9F&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="부분 편집: 두벌式(식)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>두벌<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>配列</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>배열</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 한 네벌<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이나 세벌<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>打字機</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>타자기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>存在</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>존재</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하지만 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>眞正</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>진정</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>意味</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>의미</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 두벌<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>打字機</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>타자기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는 <a href="/wiki/%ED%92%80%EC%96%B4%EC%93%B0%EA%B8%B0" title="풀어쓰기">풀어쓰기</a>로밖에 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>具現</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>구현</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되지 않았다.
</p><p>풀어쓰기 두벌<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 타자기로는 네벌<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>打字機</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>타자기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>有名</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>유명</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%AE%8B%E5%9F%BA%E5%91%A8&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="宋基周 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade12 unknown"><rb>宋基周</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>송기주</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>가 <a href="/index.php?title=1927%E5%B9%B4&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="1927年 (없는 문서)">1927<ruby class="hanja grade80 unknown"><rb>年</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>년</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>에 만든것과, <a href="/index.php?title=%E9%87%91%E4%BF%8A%E6%98%9F&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="金俊星 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade30 unknown"><rb>金俊星</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>김준성</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>打字機</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>타자기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>, <a href="/index.php?title=%EB%8F%84%EB%8D%A9%EB%B3%B4&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="도덩보 (없는 문서)">도덩보</a><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>打字機</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>타자기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>等</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>등</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 있다. <sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>

<h4><span id="金東勳(김동훈)_다섯벌式(식)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E9.87.91.E6.9D.B1.E5.8B.B3.28.EA.B9.80.EB.8F.99.ED.9B.88.29_.EB.8B.A4.EC.84.AF.EB.B2.8C.E5.BC.8F.28.EC.8B.9D.29"><ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>金東勳</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>김동훈</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 다섯벌<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%ED%95%9C%EA%B8%80%E6%89%93%E5%AD%97%E6%A9%9F&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="부분 편집: 金東勳(김동훈) 다섯벌式(식)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>金東勳</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>김동훈</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>開發</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개발</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 모아쓰기 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>打字機</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>타자기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>活字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>활자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>初聲</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>초성</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>2벌, <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>中聲</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>중성</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>2벌, <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>終聲</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>종성</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>1벌로 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>構成</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>구성</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되어 있다. <a href="/index.php?title=1949%E5%B9%B4&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="1949年 (없는 문서)">1949<ruby class="hanja grade80 unknown"><rb>年</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>년</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> <ruby class="hanja grade0"><rb>朝鮮發明奬勵會</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>조선발명장려회</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에서 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>施行</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>시행</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 한글<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>打字機</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>타자기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>懸賞應募</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>현상응모</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에서 3<ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>等</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>등</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>優勝</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>우승</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>.
</p><p>벌<ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>數</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>수</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 많아 익히기 어렵고 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>打字速度</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>타자속도</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 느리지만<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%AD%97%E9%AB%94&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="字體 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade62 unknown"><rb>字體</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자체</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>가 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>美麗</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>미려</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하여 <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%AE%98%E5%85%AC%E7%BD%B2&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="官公署 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade32 unknown"><rb>官公署</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>관공서</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>等</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>등</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에서 많이 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되었다 한다. <a href="/index.php?title=%E6%A8%99%E6%BA%96&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="標準 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade40 unknown"><rb>標準</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>표준</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a><ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>字板</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자판</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>制定</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>제정</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되기 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>以前</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>이전</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>까지는 <a href="/wiki/%E5%85%AC%E7%82%B3%E7%A6%B9%E6%89%93%E5%AD%97%E6%A9%9F" title="公炳禹打字機"><ruby class="hanja grade12"><rb>公炳禹打字機</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>공병우타자기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>와 함께 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>市場</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>시장</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 90%를 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>占有</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>점유</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하고 있었다.
