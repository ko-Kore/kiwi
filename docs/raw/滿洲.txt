<p><b><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>滿洲</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>만주</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></b>는 <a href="/index.php?title=%EC%95%84%EB%AC%B4%EB%A5%B4%E6%B1%9F&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="아무르江 (없는 문서)">아무르<ruby class="hanja grade72 unknown"><rb>江</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>강</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>·<a href="/index.php?title=%E6%9D%BE%E8%8A%B1%E6%B1%9F&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="松花江 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade40 unknown"><rb>松花江</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>송화강</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>流域</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>유역</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>中心</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>중심</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 하는 <a href="/index.php?title=%EC%9C%A0%EB%9D%BC%EC%8B%9C%EC%95%84&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="유라시아 (없는 문서)">유라시아</a> <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>東北部</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>동북부</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>, <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>現在</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>현재</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>中國</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>중국</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>東北地方</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>동북지방</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에서 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>露西亞</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>노서아</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>沿海地方</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>연해지방</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>, <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>歷史的</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>역사적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 퉁구스<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>系</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>계</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>諸集團</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>제집단</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>活動空間</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>활동공간</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다.
