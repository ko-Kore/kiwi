<h2><span id="槪要(개요)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%F0%B1%81%AC&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要(개요)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>𱁬(たいと, 다이토)는 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>總</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>총</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>84<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>劃</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>획</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 가장<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>複雜</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>복잡</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>漢字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로, <a href="/index.php?title=%E6%97%A5%E6%9C%AC%E8%A3%BD%E6%BC%A2%E5%AD%97&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="日本製漢字 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade42 unknown"><rb>日本製漢字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일본제한자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>이다. ‘だいと’나 ‘おとど’라고 읽기도 한다.
</p>
<h2><span id="構成(구성)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.8B.E6.88.90.28.EA.B5.AC.EC.84.B1.29"><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>構成</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>구성</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%F0%B1%81%AC&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 構成(구성)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>

<p><a href="/index.php?title=%E4%A8%BA&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="䨺 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade0 unknown"><rb>䨺</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>퇴</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a><sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>와 <a href="/index.php?title=%E9%BE%98&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="龘 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade0 unknown"><rb>龘</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>답</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a><sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>이 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>上下</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>상하</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>結合</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>결합</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>된 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>形態</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>형태</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다. <ruby class="hanja grade0"><rb>䨺</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>퇴</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 세 <a href="/index.php?title=%E9%9B%B2&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="雲 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade52 unknown"><rb>雲</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>운</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> 사이에 <ruby class="hanja grade0"><rb>龘</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>답</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 첫<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>番</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>번</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>째 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>龍</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 들어간 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>字形</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자형</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>과 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>單純</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>단순</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>히 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>上下</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>상하</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>結合</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>결합</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>된 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>字形</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자형</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>存在</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>존재</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하며, <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>前者</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>전자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는 おとど(오토도)라고도 읽힌다.
</p>
