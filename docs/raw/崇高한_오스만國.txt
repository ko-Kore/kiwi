<h2><span id="槪要(개요)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E5%B4%87%E9%AB%98%ED%95%9C_%EC%98%A4%EC%8A%A4%EB%A7%8C%E5%9C%8B&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要(개요)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><b><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>崇高</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>숭고</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 오스만<ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>國</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>국</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></b>(دولت عليه عثمانيه) <ruby grade40"><rb>世紀</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>세기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>부터 20<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>世紀</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>세기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>初</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>초</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>까지 <a href="/wiki/%EC%9C%A0%EB%9F%BD" title="유럽">유럽</a> <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>東南部</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>동남부</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>, <ruby grade62"><rb>大部分</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대부분</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>統治</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>통치</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하던 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>廣大</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>광대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>帝國</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>제국</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다. 투르크<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>系</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>계</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>(나중에 <a href="/index.php?title=%ED%84%B0%ED%82%A4%E4%BA%BA&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="터키人 (없는 문서)">터키<ruby class="hanja grade80 unknown"><rb>人</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>인</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>이 됨) <a href="/index.php?title=%EC%98%A4%EC%8A%A4%EB%A7%8C%E5%AE%B6&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="오스만家 (없는 문서)">오스만<ruby class="hanja grade72 unknown"><rb>家</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>가</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>出身</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>출신</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>君主</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>군주</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>治下</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>치하</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja </p>
<h2><span id="歷史(역사)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.AD.B7.E5.8F.B2.28.EC.97.AD.EC.82.AC.29"><ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>歷史</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>역사</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E5%B4%87%E9%AB%98%ED%95%9C_%EC%98%A4%EC%8A%A4%EB%A7%8C%E5%9C%8B&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 歷史(역사)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>13<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>世紀</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>세기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>末</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>말</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 <a unknown"><rb>世</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>세</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>에 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>依</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>의</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하여 아나톨리아(<ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>小</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>소</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>아시아) <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>北西部</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>북서부</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에서 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>建國</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>건국</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되었으며, 1354<ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>年</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>년</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 유럽으로 건너가 발칸<ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>半島</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>반도</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>征服</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>정복</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하고 세 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>大陸</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대륙</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 걸친 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>巨大</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>거대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>帝國</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>제국</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 세웠다. 15<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>世紀</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>세기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에는 <a href="/index.php?title=%EB%A1%9C%EB%A7%88%E5%B8%9D%E5%9C%8B&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="로마帝國 (없는 문서)">로마<ruby class="hanja grade40 unknown"><rb>帝國</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>제국</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>을 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>滅</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>멸</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하고 그 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>首都</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>수도</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>였던 <a href="/index.php?title=%EC%BD%98%EC%8A%A4%ED%83%84%ED%8B%B0%EB%85%B8%ED%8F%B4%EB%A6%AC%EC%8A%A4&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="콘스탄티노폴리스 (없는 문서)">콘스탄티노폴리스</a>를 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>征服</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>정복</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하고, 이 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>都市</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>도시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 스스로의 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>首都</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>수도</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 했다. 17<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>世紀</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>세기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>最大版圖</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>최대판도</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는 <a href="/index.php?title=%E4%B8%AD%E6%9D%B1&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="中東 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade80 unknown"><rb>中東</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>중동</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>에서 아프리카·유럽으로 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>顯著</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>현저</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>히 <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>擴大</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>확대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되었고, <ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>東西</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>동서</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는 <a href="/index.php?title=%EC%95%84%EC%A0%9C%EB%A5%B4%EB%B0%94%EC%9D%B4%EC%9E%94&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="아제르바이잔 (없는 문서)">아제르바이잔</a>에서 <a href="/index.php?title=%EB%AA%A8%EB%A1%9C%EC%BD%94&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="모로코 (없는 문서)">모로코</a>에 이르고, <ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>南北</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>남북</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 <a href="/index.php?title=%EC%98%88%EB%A9%98&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="예멘 (없는 문서)">예멘</a>에서 <a <h2><span id="呼稱(호칭)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E5.91.BC.E7.A8.B1.28.ED.98.B8.EC.B9.AD.29"><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>呼稱</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>호칭</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E5%B4%87%E9%AB%98%ED%95%9C_%EC%98%A4%EC%8A%A4%EB%A7%8C%E5%9C%8B&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="부분 편집: 呼稱(호칭)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>오스만<ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>國</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>국</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>後繼</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>후계</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>國家</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>국가</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>인 터키<ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>共和國</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>공화국</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>公式的</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>공식적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>인 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>國號</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>국호</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 처음으로 “터키”(Türkiye)라는 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>單語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>단어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>採用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>채용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>했지만, 오스만<ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>國</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>국</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 가리키는 데 있어서는 “오스만<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>帝國</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>제국</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>”에 <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>該當</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>해당</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하는 “Osmanlı İmparatorluğu”이나 “오스만<ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>國家</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>국가</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>”에 <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>該當</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>해당</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하는 “Osmanlı Devleti”의 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>表記</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>표기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하는 것이 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>一般的</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일반적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다.
</p><p><ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>英語圈</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>영어권</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에서는 “Ottoman Empire”라고 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>表記</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>표기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>된다.
</p>
<h2><span id="言語(언어)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E8.A8.80.E8.AA.9E.28.EC.96.B8.EC.96.B4.29"><ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>言語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>언어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E5%B4%87%E9%AB%98%ED%95%9C_%EC%98%A4%EC%8A%A4%EB%A7%8C%E5%9C%8B&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="부분 편집: 言語(언어)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
