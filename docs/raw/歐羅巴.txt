<p><b><ruby class="hanja grade10"><rb>歐羅巴</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>구라파</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></b>(<ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>歐洲</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>구주</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>, 유럽)은 <a href="/wiki/%E6%AD%90%E4%BA%9E" title="歐亞"><ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>歐亞</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>구아</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>의 한 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>地域</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>지역</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>亞細亞</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>아세아</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>反對</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>반대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>認識</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>인식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>된다. <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%B8%8C%E8%87%98&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="希臘 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade10 unknown"><rb>希臘</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>희랍</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>의 문화인 <a href="/index.php?title=%ED%97%AC%EB%A0%88%EB%8B%88%EC%A6%98&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="헬레니즘 (없는 문서)">헬레니즘</a>의 <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>發祥地</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>발상지</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이며 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>近東</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>근동</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%A4%9A%E7%A5%9E%E6%95%8E&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="多神敎 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade60 unknown"><rb>多神敎</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>다신교</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>, <a href="/wiki/%E5%9F%BA%E7%9D%A3%E6%95%8E" title="基督敎"><ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>基督敎</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기독교</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>等</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>등</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 헤브라이즘을 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>受容</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>수용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하여 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>發展</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>발전</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>시켰다.
