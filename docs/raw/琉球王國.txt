<h2><span id="名稱(명칭)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E5.90.8D.E7.A8.B1.28.EB.AA.85.EC.B9.AD.29"><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>名稱</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>명칭</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E7%90%89%E7%90%83%E7%8E%8B%E5%9C%8B&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 名稱(명칭)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>政體</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>정체</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>나 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>國號</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>국호</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로서의 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>正式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>정식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>名稱</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>명칭</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 “<b><ruby class="hanja grade10"><rb>琉球國</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>유구국</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></b>”이다. 덧붙여 “<ruby class="hanja grade10"><rb>琉球王國</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>유구왕국</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>” <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>自體</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자체</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>近現代</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>근현대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에서 본 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>歷史的</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>역사적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>呼稱</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>호칭</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이며, <ruby class="hanja grade10"><rb>琉球王國</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>유구왕국</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>時代</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>시대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade10"><rb>琉球</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>유구</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>內外</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>내외</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>文獻</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>문헌</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에서는 “<a href="/wiki/%E7%90%89%E7%90%83%E5%9C%8B" title="琉球國"><ruby class="hanja grade10"><rb>琉球國</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>유구국</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>”이라고 하는 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>表記</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>표기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 많이 보인다.
</p>
