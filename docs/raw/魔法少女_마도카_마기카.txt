<h2><span id="槪要(개요)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E9%AD%94%E6%B3%95%E5%B0%91%E5%A5%B3_%EB%A7%88%EB%8F%84%EC%B9%B4_%EB%A7%88%EA%B8%B0%EC%B9%B4&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要(개요)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>魔法少女</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>마법소녀</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>물이자 <ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>魔法少女</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>마법소녀</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>물 클리셰를 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>破壞</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>파괴</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해버린 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>始發點</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>시발점</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>漫畵映畵</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>만화영화</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>다.
</p>
<h2><span id="特徵(특징)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E7.89.B9.E5.BE.B5.28.ED.8A.B9.EC.A7.95.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>特徵</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>특징</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E9%AD%94%E6%B3%95%E5%B0%91%E5%A5%B3_%EB%A7%88%EB%8F%84%EC%B9%B4_%EB%A7%88%EA%B8%B0%EC%B9%B4&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 特徵(특징)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>普通</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>보통</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>魔法少女</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>마법소녀</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>물이면 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>幸福</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>행복</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하고 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>日常</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일상</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이지만 이 <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>漫畵映畵</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>만화영화</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는 다크 판타지이며 죽고 죽고 죽는다.
</p>
<h2><span id="漢字(한자)_特徵(특징)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.BC.A2.E5.AD.97.28.ED.95.9C.EC.9E.90.29_.E7.89.B9.E5.BE.B5.28.ED.8A.B9.EC.A7.95.29"><ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>漢字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>特徵</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>특징</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E9%AD%94%E6%B3%95%E5%B0%91%E5%A5%B3_%EB%A7%88%EB%8F%84%EC%B9%B4_%EB%A7%88%EA%B8%B0%EC%B9%B4&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="부분 편집: 漢字(한자) 特徵(특징)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>魔法少女</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>마법소녀</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>할때 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>一般</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일반</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>魔</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>마</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 아닌 <a href="/wiki/%ED%8C%8C%EC%9D%BC:%E9%AD%94_%E7%95%A5%E5%AD%97.png" class="image"><img alt="魔 略字.png" src="/images/thumb/d/d7/%E9%AD%94_%E7%95%A5%E5%AD%97.png/17px-%E9%AD%94_%E7%95%A5%E5%AD%97.png" decoding="async" width="17" height="17" srcset="/images/thumb/d/d7/%E9%AD%94_%E7%95%A5%E5%AD%97.png/26px-%E9%AD%94_%E7%95%A5%E5%AD%97.png 1.5x, /images/thumb/d/d7/%E9%AD%94_%E7%95%A5%E5%AD%97.png/34px-%E9%AD%94_%E7%95%A5%E5%AD%97.png 2x"></a> 인 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>略字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>약자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하고 있다.
