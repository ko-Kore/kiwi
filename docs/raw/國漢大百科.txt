<h2><span id="槪要(개요)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E5%9C%8B%E6%BC%A2%E5%A4%A7%E7%99%BE%E7%A7%91&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要(개요)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>國漢大百科</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>국한대백과</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는 <a href="/wiki/%E9%9F%93%E5%9C%8B%E8%AA%9E" title="韓國語"><ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>韓國語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한국어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> <a href="/wiki/%EC%9C%84%ED%82%A4%EC%9C%84%ED%82%A4" title="위키위키">위키위키</a> 사이트이다.
</p>
<h2><span id="特徵(특징)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E7.89.B9.E5.BE.B5.28.ED.8A.B9.EC.A7.95.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>特徵</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>특징</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E5%9C%8B%E6%BC%A2%E5%A4%A7%E7%99%BE%E7%A7%91&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 特徵(특징)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>어느 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>程度</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>정도</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 유머<ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>性</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>성</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>記述</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기술</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>과 함께 즐기면서 볼 수 있는 <ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>綜合</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>종합</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>雜學</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>잡학</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 위키를 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>志向</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>지향</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한다. <a href="/wiki/%E5%9C%8B%E6%BC%A2%E6%96%87%E6%B7%B7%E7%94%A8" title="國漢文混用"><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>國漢文混用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>국한문혼용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>으로 적힌 읽을거리와 쓸 거리를 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>提供</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>제공</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한다는 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>目的</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>목적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다.
</p><p><ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>全體的</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>전체적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 <a href="/wiki/%E6%BC%A2%E9%9B%BB%E5%A4%A7%E7%99%BE%E7%A7%91" title="漢電大百科"><ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>漢電大百科</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한전대백과</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>의 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>特徵</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>특징</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>大部分</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대부분</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>繼承</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>계승</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하지만, <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>運營</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>운영</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하는 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>目的中</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>목적중</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에서 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>漢字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>文書化</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>문서화</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>爲</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>위</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한다는 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>部分</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>부분</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 빠졌다. <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>文書化</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>문서화</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>爲</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>위</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>導入</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>도입</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>된 <a href="/index.php?title=%EC%8B%9C%EB%A7%A8%ED%8B%B1_%EB%AF%B8%EB%94%94%EC%96%B4%EC%9C%84%ED%82%A4&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="시맨틱 미디어위키 (없는 문서)">시맨틱 미디어위키</a> <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>擴張機能</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>확장기능</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>도 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>除去</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>제거</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되었다.
</p>
<h2><span id="國漢文混用(국한문혼용)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E5.9C.8B.E6.BC.A2.E6.96.87.E6.B7.B7.E7.94.A8.28.EA.B5.AD.ED.95.9C.EB.AC.B8.ED.98.BC.EC.9A.A9.29"><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>國漢文混用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>국한문혼용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E5%9C%8B%E6%BC%A2%E5%A4%A7%E7%99%BE%E7%A7%91&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="부분 편집: 國漢文混用(국한문혼용)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/wiki/%E5%9C%8B%E6%BC%A2%E6%96%87%E6%B7%B7%E7%94%A8%E9%AB%94" class="mw-redirect" title="國漢文混用體"><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>國漢文混用體</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>국한문혼용체</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>를 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하여 <a href="/wiki/%E6%96%87%E6%9B%B8" title="文書"><ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>文書</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>문서</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>의 모든 <a href="/wiki/%E6%BC%A2%E5%AD%97%E8%AA%9E" title="漢字語"><ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>漢字語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한자어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>는 <a href="/wiki/%E6%BC%A2%E5%AD%97" title="漢字"><ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>漢字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>로 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>表記</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>표기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한다. 그러나 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>漢字語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한자어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>讀音</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>독음</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>表示</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>표시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해주는 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>變換機能</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>변환기능</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>提供</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>제공</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하며, <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>入力環境</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>입력환경</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>問題</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>문제</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>等</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>등</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>因</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>인</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해 한글<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>專用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>전용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>作成</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>작성</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>할 수도 있다. <s>하지만 곧 위키페어리에 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>依</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>의</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해 모두 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>漢字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>變換</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>변환</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>될 것이다</s>
</p><p><ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>基本</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기본</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>設定</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>설정</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로는 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>讀音</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>독음</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>機能</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기능</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>設定</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>설정</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되어 모든 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>漢字語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한자어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 옆에 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>讀音</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>독음</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 표시되어 <a href="/wiki/%E5%9C%8B%E6%BC%A2%E6%96%87%E5%80%82%E7%94%A8%E9%AB%94" class="mw-redirect" title="國漢文倂用體"><ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>國漢文倂用體</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>국한문병용체</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>같이 보이며, <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>會員加入</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>회원가입</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 하면 <a href="/index.php?title=%EB%A3%A8%EB%B9%84&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="루비 (없는 문서)">루비</a>의 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>位置</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>위치</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>나 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>讀音</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>독음</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>表示</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>표시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되는 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>漢字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>難易度</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>난이도</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>等</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>등</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 맞춤 설정할 수 있다.
</p>
