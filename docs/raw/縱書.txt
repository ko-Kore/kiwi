<h2><span id="槪要(개요)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E7%B8%B1%E6%9B%B8&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要(개요)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>글을 쓸 때 <a href="/index.php?title=%E6%96%87%E7%AB%A0&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="文章 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade60 unknown"><rb>文章</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>문장</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>의 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>進行方向</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>진행방향</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 위에서 아래로, <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>卽</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>즉</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 세로(<a href="/index.php?title=%E7%B8%B1&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="縱 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade32 unknown"><rb>縱</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>종</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>)로 하는 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>方式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>방식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>.
</p>
<h2><span id="方向(방향)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.96.B9.E5.90.91.28.EB.B0.A9.ED.96.A5.29"><ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>方向</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>방향</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E7%B8%B1%E6%9B%B8&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 方向(방향)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span id="右縱書(우종서)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E5.8F.B3.E7.B8.B1.E6.9B.B8.28.EC.9A.B0.EC.A2.85.EC.84.9C.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>右縱書</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>우종서</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E7%B8%B1%E6%9B%B8&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="부분 편집: 右縱書(우종서)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>文章</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>문장</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>들이 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>右</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>우</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에서 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>左</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>좌</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>順序</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>순서</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 흐르는 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>方向</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>방향</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다. <a href="/wiki/%E6%BC%A2%E5%AD%97%E6%96%87%E5%8C%96%E5%9C%88" title="漢字文化圈"><ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>漢字文化圈</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한자문화권</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>의 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>傳統的</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>전통적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>書字方向</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>서자방향</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다.
</p>
<h3><span id="左縱書(좌종서)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E5.B7.A6.E7.B8.B1.E6.9B.B8.28.EC.A2.8C.EC.A2.85.EC.84.9C.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>左縱書</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>좌종서</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E7%B8%B1%E6%9B%B8&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="부분 편집: 左縱書(좌종서)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>左</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>좌</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에서 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>右</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>우</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 쓰는 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>方式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>방식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로, <a href="/index.php?title=%E6%BB%BF%E6%B4%B2%E6%96%87%E5%AD%97&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="滿洲文字 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade32 unknown"><rb>滿洲文字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>만주문자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>나 <a href="/index.php?title=%E8%92%99%E5%8F%A4%E6%96%87%E5%AD%97&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="蒙古文字 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade32 unknown"><rb>蒙古文字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>몽고문자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>를 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>筆記</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>필기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>할 때 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>된다.
</p>
