<h2><span id=".E5.90.8D.E7.A8.B1.28.EB.AA.85.EC.B9.AD.29"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="名稱(명칭)"><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>名稱</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>명칭</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=IOS&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 名稱(명칭)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/wiki/%EC%95%84%EC%9D%B4%ED%8F%B0" title="아이폰">아이폰</a>이 처음 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>發表</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>발표</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>될 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>當時</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>당시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에는 <a href="/index.php?title=IPhone_OS&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="IPhone OS (없는 문서)">iPhone OS</a>라는 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>名稱</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>명칭</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이었다. <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>後</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>후</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 <a href="/index.php?title=%EC%95%84%EC%9D%B4%ED%8C%A8%EB%93%9C&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="아이패드 (없는 문서)">아이패드</a>가 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>出市</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>출시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>됨에 따라 iOS라고 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>改名</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개명</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되었으며, iOS 13에서는 아이패드 버전이 <a href="/index.php?title=IPadOS&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="IPadOS (없는 문서)">iPadOS</a>로 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>獨立</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>독립</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하여 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>現在</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>현재</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> iOS라는 이름으로는 아이폰에만 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>適用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>적용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>된다.
</p>
