<h2><span id=".E5.90.8D.E7.A8.B1.28.EB.AA.85.EC.B9.AD.29"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="名稱(명칭)"><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>名稱</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>명칭</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E4%BA%9E%E7%B4%B0%E4%BA%9E&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 名稱(명칭)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>過去</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>과거</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 <a href="/wiki/%E7%BE%85%E9%A6%AC" title="羅馬"><ruby class="hanja unknown"><rb>小亞細亞</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>소아세아</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>(<ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>只今</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>지금</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <a unknown"><rb>土耳其</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>토이기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>)를 부르는 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>名稱</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>명칭</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이었으나 <ruby class="hanja href="/index.php?title=%E6%AD%90%E4%BA%9E%E5%A4%A7%E9%99%B8&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="歐亞大陸 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade20 grade42"><rb>意味</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>의미</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한다. 이 <ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>外</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>외</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에도 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>多樣</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>다양</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하게 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>定義</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>정의</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>된다. <a href="/wiki/%E6%BC%A2%E5%AD%97" title="漢字"><ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>漢字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>로는 <b><a href="/index.php?title=%E4%BA%9E&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="亞 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade32 unknown"><rb>亞</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>아</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a></b>로 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>縮略</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>축략</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되며, <b><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>亞洲</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>아주</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></b> <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>等</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>등</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>表記</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>표기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한다.
