<h2><span id="槪要(개요)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E8%87%BA%E5%8C%97%E5%B8%82&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要(개요)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><b><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>臺北市</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대북시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></b>는 <a href="/wiki/%E8%87%BA%E7%81%A3" class="mw-redirect" title="臺灣"><ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>臺灣</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대만</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>北部</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>북부</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에있는 <a href="/wiki/%E4%B8%AD%E8%8F%AF%E6%B0%91%E5%9C%8B" title="中華民國"><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>中華民國</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>중화민국</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>의 <a href="/index.php?title=%E7%9B%B4%E8%BD%84%E5%B8%82&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="直轄市 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade10 unknown"><rb>直轄市</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>직할시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>로, <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>事實上</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사실상</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>&nbsp;<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>中華民國</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>중화민국</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의&nbsp;<a href="/wiki/%E9%A6%96%E9%83%BD" title="首都"><ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>首都</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>수도</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>로 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>機能</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기능</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하고 있다.
</p>
<h2><span id="名稱(명칭)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E5.90.8D.E7.A8.B1.28.EB.AA.85.EC.B9.AD.29"><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>名稱</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>명칭</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E8%87%BA%E5%8C%97%E5%B8%82&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 名稱(명칭)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>公式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>공식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>英語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>영어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>表記</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>표기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는 Taipei. <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>現地</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>현지</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에서는 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>臺北</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대북</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 또는 <ruby class="hanja grade12"><rb>台北</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>태북</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>表記</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>표기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한다.
</p>
<h2><span id="歷史(역사)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.AD.B7.E5.8F.B2.28.EC.97.AD.EC.82.AC.29"><ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>歷史</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>역사</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E8%87%BA%E5%8C%97%E5%B8%82&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="부분 편집: 歷史(역사)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h2><span id="地理(지리)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E5.9C.B0.E7.90.86.28.EC.A7.80.EB.A6.AC.29"><ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>地理</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>지리</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E8%87%BA%E5%8C%97%E5%B8%82&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="부분 편집: 地理(지리)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>臺北市</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대북시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는 <a href="/index.php?title=%E8%87%BA%E5%8C%97%E7%9B%86%E5%9C%B0&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="臺北盆地 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade10 unknown"><rb>臺北盆地</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대북분지</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>에 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>位置</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>위치</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하고, <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>大屯火山群</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대둔화산군</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>市北部</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>시북부</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>位置</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>위치</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하고 있으며, <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>市南部</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>시남부</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>圓山</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>원산</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>, <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>大直</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대직</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>, <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>內湖</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>내호</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>向</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>향</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해 <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>緩慢</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>완만</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>傾斜</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>경사</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 있다. <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>市東部</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>시동부</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>內湖</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>내호</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>, <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>南港</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>남항</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 및 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>南部</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>남부</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade10"><rb>木柵</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>목책</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에서는 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>丘陵地帶</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>구릉지대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 되어 있어 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>標高</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>표고</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>約</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>약</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 300m의 <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%8D%97%E6%B8%AF%E5%B1%B1&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="南港山 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade42 unknown"><rb>南港山</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>남항산</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>가 퍼지고 있다.
</p>
<h2><span id="政治(정치)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.94.BF.E6.B2.BB.28.EC.A0.95.EC.B9.98.29"><ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>政治</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>정치</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E8%87%BA%E5%8C%97%E5%B8%82&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="부분 편집: 政治(정치)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>臺北市政府</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대북시정부</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>臺北市</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대북시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>最高行政機關</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>최고행정기관</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다.
</p><p><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>臺北市議會</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대북시의회</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>臺北市</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대북시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja
