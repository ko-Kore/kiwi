<h2><span id="槪要(개요)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E5%A7%94%E7%8F%AD%E5%BE%A9%E7%94%9F%E6%BC%A2%E5%96%83%E8%B6%8A%E5%8D%97&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要(개요)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>

<h2><span id="名稱(명칭)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E5.90.8D.E7.A8.B1.28.EB.AA.85.EC.B9.AD.29"><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>名稱</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>명칭</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E5%A7%94%E7%8F%AD%E5%BE%A9%E7%94%9F%E6%BC%A2%E5%96%83%E8%B6%8A%E5%8D%97&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 名稱(명칭)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/wiki/%E8%B6%8A%E5%8D%97%E8%AA%9E" title="越南語"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>越南語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>월남어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>는 <a href="/index.php?title=%E4%BF%AE%E9%A3%BE%E8%AA%9E&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="修飾語 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade32 unknown"><rb>修飾語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>수식어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>가 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>被修飾語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>피수식어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 뒤에 놓이기 때문에 <a href="/wiki/%E6%BC%A2%E5%AD%97%E8%AA%9E" title="漢字語"><ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>漢字語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한자어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>의 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>構造</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>구조</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>亦是</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>역시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <a href="/wiki/%E9%9F%93%E5%9C%8B%E8%AA%9E" title="韓國語"><ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>韓國語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한국어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>等</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>등</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>과는 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>反對</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>반대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 된다. 따라서 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>越南語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>월남어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>名稱</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>명칭</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>委班</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>위반</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>(<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>委員會</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>위원회</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>)가 맨 앞에, <a href="/index.php?title=%E8%B6%8A%E5%8D%97&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="越南 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade32 unknown"><rb>越南</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>월남</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>이 맨 뒤에 온다.
</p>
<h2><span id="活動(활동)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.B4.BB.E5.8B.95.28.ED.99.9C.EB.8F.99.29"><ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>活動</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>활동</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E5%A7%94%E7%8F%AD%E5%BE%A9%E7%94%9F%E6%BC%A2%E5%96%83%E8%B6%8A%E5%8D%97&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="부분 편집: 活動(활동)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span id="漢喃(한남)_弘報(홍보)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.BC.A2.E5.96.83.28.ED.95.9C.EB.82.A8.29_.E5.BC.98.E5.A0.B1.28.ED.99.8D.EB.B3.B4.29"><ruby class="hanja grade0"><rb>漢喃</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한남</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>弘報</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>홍보</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E5%A7%94%E7%8F%AD%E5%BE%A9%E7%94%9F%E6%BC%A2%E5%96%83%E8%B6%8A%E5%8D%97&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="부분 편집: 漢喃(한남) 弘報(홍보)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://www.hannom-rcv.org/">웹 사이트</a>를 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>運營</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>운영</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하며 <a href="/wiki/%E8%B6%8A%E5%8D%97%E8%AA%9E" title="越南語"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>越南語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>월남어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>에 있어서의 <a href="/wiki/%F0%A1%A8%B8%E5%96%83" title="𡨸喃"><ruby class="hanja grade0"><rb>𡨸喃</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자남</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>과 <a href="/wiki/%E6%BC%A2%E5%AD%97" title="漢字"><ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>漢字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>를 <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>弘報</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>홍보</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한다. <a href="/index.php?title=%ED%99%88_%ED%8E%98%EC%9D%B4%EC%A7%80&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="홈 페이지 (없는 문서)">홈 페이지</a>의 '<ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>排曰</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>배왈</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>' <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>項目</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>항목</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 <a href="/wiki/%E9%9F%93%E5%9C%8B%E8%AA%9E" title="韓國語"><ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>韓國語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한국어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>로 된 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>說明</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>설명</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>도 있다.
</p>
<h3><span id="위키_運營(운영)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".EC.9C.84.ED.82.A4_.E9.81.8B.E7.87.9F.28.EC.9A.B4.EC.98.81.29">위키 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>運營</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>운영</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E5%A7%94%E7%8F%AD%E5%BE%A9%E7%94%9F%E6%BC%A2%E5%96%83%E8%B6%8A%E5%8D%97&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="부분 편집: 위키 運營(운영)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade0"><rb>漢喃</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한남</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 적는 <a href="/wiki/%EC%9C%84%ED%82%A4%EC%9C%84%ED%82%A4" title="위키위키">위키위키</a>인 <a href="/wiki/%E9%9F%8B%E9%82%A3%E5%A8%81%E7%AE%95" title="韋那威箕"><ruby class="hanja grade12"><rb>韋那威箕</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>위나위기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>를 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>運營中</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>운영중</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다.
</p>
<h3><span id="𡨸喃(자남)_標準化(표준화)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".F0.A1.A8.B8.E5.96.83.28.EC.9E.90.EB.82.A8.29_.E6.A8.99.E6.BA.96.E5.8C.96.28.ED.91.9C.EC.A4.80.ED.99.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade0"><rb>𡨸喃</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자남</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>標準化</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>표준화</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E5%A7%94%E7%8F%AD%E5%BE%A9%E7%94%9F%E6%BC%A2%E5%96%83%E8%B6%8A%E5%8D%97&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="부분 편집: 𡨸喃(자남) 標準化(표준화)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a href="/index.php?title=%E6%A8%99%E6%BA%96%E5%8C%96&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="標準化 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade40 unknown"><rb>標準化</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>표준화</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>될 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>機會</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기회</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 없이 여러 <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%AD%97%E9%AB%94&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="字體 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade62 unknown"><rb>字體</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자체</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>와 <a href="/index.php?title=%E6%9B%B8%E6%B3%95&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="書法 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade52 unknown"><rb>書法</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>서법</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>이 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>混在</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>혼재</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해 있던 <a href="/wiki/%F0%A1%A8%B8%E5%96%83" title="𡨸喃"><ruby class="hanja grade0"><rb>𡨸喃</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자남</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>을 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>自體的</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자체적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 <a href="/index.php?title=%E6%A8%99%E6%BA%96%E5%8C%96&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="標準化 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade40 unknown"><rb>標準化</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>표준화</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>하는 <a href="/index.php?title=%ED%94%84%EB%A1%9C%EC%A0%9D%ED%8A%B8&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="프로젝트 (없는 문서)">프로젝트</a>를 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>進行中</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>진행중</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다.
</p><p><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>標準化</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>표준화</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>先述</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>선술</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 <a href="/wiki/%E9%9F%8B%E9%82%A3%E5%A8%81%E7%AE%95" title="韋那威箕"><ruby class="hanja grade12"><rb>韋那威箕</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>위나위기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>에서 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>多數</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>다수</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>參與</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>참여</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>意見</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>의견</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 나누는 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>進行</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>진행</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>된다. <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>歷史的</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>역사적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用例</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용례</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 <a href="/index.php?title=%E6%A8%99%E6%BA%96%E5%AD%97%E5%BD%A2&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="標準字形 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade40 unknown"><rb>標準字形</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>표준자형</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>의 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>基準</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기준</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 되며, <a href="/wiki/%F0%A1%A8%B8%E5%96%83" title="𡨸喃"><ruby class="hanja grade0"><rb>𡨸喃</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자남</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>에 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>親熟</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>친숙</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하다면 누구나 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>參與</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>참여</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>할 수 있다.
</p>
