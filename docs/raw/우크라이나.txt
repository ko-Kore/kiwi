<h2><span id="槪要(개요)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%EC%9A%B0%ED%81%AC%EB%9D%BC%EC%9D%B4%EB%82%98&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要(개요)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><b>우크라이나</b>는 <a href="/index.php?title=%E6%9D%B1%EC%9C%A0%EB%9F%BD&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="東유럽 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade80 unknown"><rb>東</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>동</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>유럽</a>에 있는 나라이다. 유럽에서 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>面積</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>면적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 두 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>番</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>번</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>째로 큰 나라이다. <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>中世</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>중세</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에는 <ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>東</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>동</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>슬라브<ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>文化</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>문화</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>中心地</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>중심지</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이자 <a href="/index.php?title=%EB%A3%A8%EC%8A%A4%E5%9C%8B&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="루스國 (없는 문서)">루스<ruby class="hanja grade80 unknown"><rb>國</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>국</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>이라는 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>强力</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>강력</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>國家</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>국가</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 우크라이나의 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>國民意識</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>국민의식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>基礎</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기초</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>形成</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>형성</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>했다.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/2021%E5%B9%B4" title="2021年">2021<ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>年</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>년</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a><a href="/index.php?title=3%E6%9C%88&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="3月 (없는 문서)">3<ruby class="hanja grade80 unknown"><rb>月</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>월</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>부터 <a href="/index.php?title=%E9%9C%B2%E8%A5%BF%E4%BA%9E&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="露西亞 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade32 unknown"><rb>露西亞</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>노서아</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>와의 사이에서 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>危機</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>위기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 높아졌고, <a href="/index.php?title=2022%E5%B9%B4&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="2022年 (없는 문서)">2022<ruby class="hanja grade80 unknown"><rb>年</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>년</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a><a href="/index.php?title=2%E6%9C%8824%E6%97%A5&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="2月24日 (없는 문서)">2<ruby class="hanja grade80 unknown"><rb>月</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>월</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>24<ruby class="hanja grade80 unknown"><rb>日</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>에 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>露西亞</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>노서아</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <a href="/wiki/2022%E5%B9%B4_%EC%9A%B0%ED%81%AC%EB%9D%BC%EC%9D%B4%EB%82%98-%EB%9F%AC%EC%8B%9C%EC%95%84_%E6%88%B0%E7%88%AD" title="2022年 우크라이나-러시아 戰爭">우크라이나 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>侵攻</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>침공</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>始作</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>시작</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>되었다.
</p>
