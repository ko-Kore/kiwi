<h2><span id="게임플레이"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".EA.B2.8C.EC.9E.84.ED.94.8C.EB.A0.88.EC.9D.B4">게임플레이</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%ED%8C%A9%ED%86%A0%EB%A6%AC%EC%98%A4&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 게임플레이">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span id="自動化(자동화)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E8.87.AA.E5.8B.95.E5.8C.96.28.EC.9E.90.EB.8F.99.ED.99.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>自動化</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자동화</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%ED%8C%A9%ED%86%A0%EB%A6%AC%EC%98%A4&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 自動化(자동화)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>基本的</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기본적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>인 <ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>箱子</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>상자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>나 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>初盤</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>초반</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에서 쓰는 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>機械</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기계</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>들은 인벤토리에 있는 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>材料</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>재료</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>直接</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>직접</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>組合</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>조합</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>할수있다. 그러나 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>當然</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>당연</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>히 한<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>番</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>번</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 만들수있는 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>物件</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>물건</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 하나뿐인데다 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>材料</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>재료</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>加工</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>가공</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>中間部品</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>중간부품</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>부터 만들어야 할 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>境遇</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>경우</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에는 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>時間</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>시간</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 매우 오래 걸리므로 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>組立機械</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>조립기계</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하여 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>生産</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>생산</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>自動化</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자동화</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해나가며 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>效率</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>효율</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 올리는게 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>主</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>주</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>컨텐츠이다.
</p>
<h3><span id="採鑛(채광)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.8E.A1.E9.91.9B.28.EC.B1.84.EA.B4.91.29"><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>採鑛</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>채광</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%ED%8C%A9%ED%86%A0%EB%A6%AC%EC%98%A4&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="부분 편집: 採鑛(채광)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>採鑛</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>채광</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>亦是</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>역시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 맨손(도끼)로도 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>可能</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>가능</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하지만 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>極初盤部</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>극초반부</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 아니라면 그 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>時間</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>시간</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>採鑛</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>채광</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>드릴을 만들어서 돌리는 게 더 빠르고 플레이어도 손이 비기 때문에 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>效率</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>효율</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>도 좋다.

grade32"><rb>製鍊</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>제련</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하여 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>鐵板</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>철판</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>과 구리<ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>板</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>판</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 만든 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>後</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>후</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>利用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>이용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>할수있는데, 이 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>作業</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>작업</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 <a class="hanja grade40"><rb>境遇</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>경우</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는 흔히 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하는 아이템 <ruby class="hanja
