<html><head></head><body><div class="mw-parser-output"><h2><span id="槪要(개요)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E9%AB%98%E9%80%9F%E9%90%B5%E9%81%93&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要(개요)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>旣存</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기존</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 것보다 빠른 <a href="/index.php?title=%E9%90%B5%E9%81%93&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="鐵道 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade50 unknown"><rb>鐵道</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>철도</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a><ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>車輛</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>차량</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>과 그에 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>對應</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대응</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하는 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>施設</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>시설</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>들을 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>指稱</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>지칭</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한다. <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>一般的</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일반적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>旣存線路</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기존선로</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>改良</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개량</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해서 200km/h<ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>以上</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>이상</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 내거나 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>新線</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>신선</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에서 250km/h<ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>以上</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>이상</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 낼수있는 <ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>車輛</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>차량</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>과 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>施設</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>시설</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>高速鐵道</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>고속철도</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>라고 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>定義</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>정의</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한다.
</p>
<h2><span id="歷史(역사)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.AD.B7.E5.8F.B2.28.EC.97.AD.EC.82.AC.29"><ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>歷史</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>역사</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E9%AB%98%E9%80%9F%E9%90%B5%E9%81%93&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 歷史(역사)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/index.php?title=1964%E5%B9%B4&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="1964年 (없는 문서)">1964<ruby class="hanja grade80 unknown"><rb>年</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>년</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>에 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>開通</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개통</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하여 210km/h로 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>營業運轉</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>영업운전</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>施行</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>시행</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 <a href="/wiki/%E6%9D%B1%E6%B5%B7%E9%81%93%E6%96%B0%E5%B9%B9%E7%B7%9A" title="東海道新幹線"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>東海道新幹線</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>동해도신간선</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>이 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>世界最初</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>세계최초</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>商業用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>상업용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>高速鐵道</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>고속철도</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 여겨진다.
</p>
