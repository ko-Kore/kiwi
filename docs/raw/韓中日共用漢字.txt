<p><ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>韓中日共用漢字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한중일공용한자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>(<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>或</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>혹</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>中日韓共用漢字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>중일한공용한자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>)는 ‘<a href="/index.php?title=%E9%9F%93%E4%B8%AD%E6%97%A530%E4%BA%BA%E6%9C%83&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="韓中日30人會 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade80 unknown"><rb>韓中日</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한중일</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>30<ruby class="hanja grade62 unknown"><rb>人會</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>인회</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>’에서 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>制定</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>제정</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 <a href="/index.php?title=%E6%BC%A2%E5%AD%97%E8%A1%A8&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="漢字表 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade62 unknown"><rb>漢字表</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한자표</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>이다.
