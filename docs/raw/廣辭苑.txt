<h2><span id="槪要(개요)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E5%BB%A3%E8%BE%AD%E8%8B%91&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要(개요)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>『<ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>廣辭苑</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>광사원</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>』은<a href="/index.php?title=%E5%B7%96%E6%B3%A2%E6%9B%B8%E5%BA%97&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="巖波書店 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade32 unknown"><rb>巖波書店</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>암파서점</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>이 <a href="/index.php?title=1955%E5%B9%B4&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="1955年 (없는 문서)">1955<ruby class="hanja grade80 unknown"><rb>年</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>년</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>부터<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>發行</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>발행</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하고있는<ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>中型</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>중형</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <a href="/index.php?title=%E6%97%A5%E8%AA%9E%E8%BE%AD%E5%85%B8&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="日語辭典 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade40 unknown"><rb>日語辭典</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일어사전</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>이다. <a href="/index.php?title=%E7%99%BE%E7%A7%91%E4%BA%8B%E5%85%B8&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="百科事典 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade52 unknown"><rb>百科事典</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>백과사전</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>의<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>機能</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기능</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>도 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>兼</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>겸</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하고있다.
</p>
<h2><span id="情報(정보)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.83.85.E5.A0.B1.28.EC.A0.95.EB.B3.B4.29"><ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>情報</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>정보</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E5%BB%A3%E8%BE%AD%E8%8B%91&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 情報(정보)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>

unknown"><rb>新村猛</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>신촌맹</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>이며, <a href="/index.php?title=1935%E5%B9%B4&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="1935年 (없는 문서)">1935<ruby class="hanja
