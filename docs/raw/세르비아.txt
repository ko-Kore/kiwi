<h2><span id="槪要(개요)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%EC%84%B8%EB%A5%B4%EB%B9%84%EC%95%84&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要(개요)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><b>세르비아</b>(<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>塞耳維</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>새이유</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby><sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup>)는 <ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>南東</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>남동</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby><a href="/index.php?title=%EC%9C%A0%EB%9F%BD&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="유럽 (없는 문서)">유럽</a>의 <a href="/index.php?title=%EB%B0%9C%EC%B9%B8%E5%8D%8A%E5%B3%B6&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="발칸半島 (없는 문서)">발칸<ruby class="hanja grade50 unknown"><rb>半島</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>반도</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>의 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>中西部</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>중서부</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>內陸</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>내륙</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>位置</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>위치</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>國家</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>국가</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다.
</p>
<h2><span id="國家象徵(국가상징)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E5.9C.8B.E5.AE.B6.E8.B1.A1.E5.BE.B5.28.EA.B5.AD.EA.B0.80.EC.83.81.EC.A7.95.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>國家象徵</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>국가상징</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%EC%84%B8%EB%A5%B4%EB%B9%84%EC%95%84&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 國家象徵(국가상징)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span id="國名(국명)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E5.9C.8B.E5.90.8D.28.EA.B5.AD.EB.AA.85.29"><ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>國名</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>국명</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%EC%84%B8%EB%A5%B4%EB%B9%84%EC%95%84&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="부분 편집: 國名(국명)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a class="mw-selflink selflink">세르비아</a><ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>&nbsp;<ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>國名</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>국명</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 “스르비야”(Србија)이다.
</p><p>“세르비아”라는 말은 <a href="/wiki/%E8%8B%B1%E8%AA%9E" title="英語"><ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>英語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>영어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> “Serbia”에서 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>由來</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>유래</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하여 “Serbia”라는 이름은 세르비아의 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>主要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>주요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>民族</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>민족</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>인 <a href="/index.php?title=%EC%84%B8%EB%A5%B4%EB%B9%84%EC%95%84%E4%BA%BA&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="세르비아人 (없는 문서)">세르비아<ruby class="hanja grade80 unknown"><rb>人</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>인</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>(Срби)의 이름에서 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>由來</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>유래</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>했다. Срби이라는 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>民族名</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>민족명</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>對</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>語源</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>어원</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%8F%A4%E4%BB%A3%EC%8A%AC%EB%9D%BC%EB%B8%8C%E8%AA%9E&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="古代슬라브語 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade60 unknown"><rb>古代</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>고대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>슬라브<ruby class="hanja grade70 unknown"><rb>語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>인 *sьrbъ에서 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>由來</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>유래</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>된 것으로 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>推測</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>추측</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>할 뿐이다. <a href="/index.php?title=%E7%BE%85%E9%A6%AC%E4%BA%BA&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="羅馬人 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade42 unknown"><rb>羅馬人</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>나마인</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>, <a href="/index.php?title=%EC%8A%AC%EB%9D%BC%EB%B8%8C%E4%BA%BA&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="슬라브人 (없는 문서)">슬라브<ruby class="hanja grade80 unknown"><rb>人</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>인</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>, <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%B8%8C%E8%87%98%E4%BA%BA&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="希臘人 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade10 unknown"><rb>希臘人</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>희랍인</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>이 모두 비슷한 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>時期</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>시기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 이 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>地域</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>지역</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 세르비아와 비슷한 이름으로 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>稱</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>칭</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>했기에 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>語源</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>어원</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>明確</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>명확</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>意味</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>의미</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 알기가 힘들다.
</p><p><ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>日本</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일본</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>·<ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>中國</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>중국</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의&nbsp;<ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>漢字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>&nbsp;<ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>表記</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>표기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는&nbsp;<ruby class="hanja grade10"><rb>塞爾維亞</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>새이유아</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다.
</p>
