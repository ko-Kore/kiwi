<h2><span id="槪要(개요)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E4%BB%BB%E5%A4%A9%E5%A0%82&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要(개요)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><b><ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>任天堂</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>임천당</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></b>(にんてんどう, 닌텐도)은 <ruby class="hanja grade10"><rb>玩具</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>완구</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>와 <a href="/index.php?title=%EB%B9%84%EB%94%94%EC%98%A4_%EA%B2%8C%EC%9E%84&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="비디오 게임 (없는 문서)">비디오 게임</a>을 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>開發</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개발</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>·<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>製造</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>제조</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>·<ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>販賣</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>판매</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하는 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>日本</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일본</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>企業</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기업</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다.
</p>
<h2><span id="歷史(역사)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.AD.B7.E5.8F.B2.28.EC.97.AD.EC.82.AC.29"><ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>歷史</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>역사</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E4%BB%BB%E5%A4%A9%E5%A0%82&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 歷史(역사)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>草創期</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>초창기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에는 <a href="/index.php?title=%E8%8A%B1%E9%AC%AA%E5%BC%B5&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="花鬪張 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade40 unknown"><rb>花鬪張</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>화투장</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>이나 <a href="/index.php?title=%ED%8A%B8%EB%9F%BC%ED%94%84_%EC%B9%B4%EB%93%9C&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="트럼프 카드 (없는 문서)">트럼프 카드</a><sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>를 만들던 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>會社</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>회사</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>였다가 <a href="/index.php?title=%EA%B2%8C%EC%9E%84%E6%A9%9F&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="게임機 (없는 문서)">게임<ruby class="hanja grade40 unknown"><rb>機</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>開發</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개발</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>始作</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>시작</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하고, <a href="/index.php?title=%EA%B2%8C%EC%9E%84%26%EC%9B%8C%EC%B9%98&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="게임&amp;워치 (없는 문서)">게임&amp;워치</a>와 <a href="/index.php?title=%ED%8C%A8%EB%B0%80%EB%A6%AC%EC%BB%B4%ED%93%A8%ED%84%B0&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="패밀리컴퓨터 (없는 문서)">패밀리컴퓨터</a>로 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>本格的</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>본격적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 게임<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>機</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>開發</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개발</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 힘쓰게 된다.
</p>
