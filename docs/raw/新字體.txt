<h2><span id="特徴(특징)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E7.89.B9.E5.BE.B4.28.ED.8A.B9.EC.A7.95.29"><ruby class="hanja grade0"><rb>特徴</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>특징</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E6%96%B0%E5%AD%97%E9%AB%94&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 特徴(특징)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>民間</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>민간</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에서 쓰이던 여러 <a href="/wiki/%E7%95%A5%E5%AD%97" title="略字"><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>略字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>약자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>를 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>採用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>채용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하여 <ruby class="hanja grade0"><rb>画数</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>화수</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 적고 쓰기 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>簡便</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>간편</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 것 <ruby class="hanja grade0"><rb>為主</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>위주</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>標準</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>표준</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>字形</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자형</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>正</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>정</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하였다. 또, <ruby class="hanja grade0"><rb>点</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>점</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 한두 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>個</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>差異</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>차이</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>等</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>등</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 <ruby class="hanja grade10"><rb>曖昧</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>애매</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하게 나뉜 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>字形</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자형</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>도 쓰기 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>便</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>편</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 쪽으로 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>統合</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>통합</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>했다.
</p>
