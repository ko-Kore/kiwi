<h2><span id=".EB.8B.A4.EB.A5.B8_.EC.9C.84.ED.82.A4.EC.99.80.EC.9D.98_.E5.B7.AE.E7.95.B0.E9.BB.9E.28.EC.B0.A8.EC.9D.B4.EC.A0.90.29"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="다른_위키와의_差異點(차이점)">다른 위키와의 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>差異點</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>차이점</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E5%9C%8B%E6%BC%A2%E5%A4%A7%E7%99%BE%E7%A7%91:%E7%B4%B9%E4%BB%8B&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 다른 위키와의 差異點(차이점)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>

<p>위키를 읽거나 <ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>編輯</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>편집</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하기 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>前</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>전</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>會員加入</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>회원가입</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 하시는 것을 <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>推薦</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>추천</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>합니다. <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>加入</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>가입</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하시면 <ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>編輯</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>편집</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>할 때 <a href="/index.php?title=IP%E4%BD%8F%E6%89%80&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="IP住所 (없는 문서)">IP<ruby class="hanja grade70 unknown"><rb>住所</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>주소</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>를 숨길 수 있을 뿐만 아니라, <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>文書</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>문서</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 읽을 때에도 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>讀音</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>독음</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>表示</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>표시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>할 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>漢字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>範圍</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>범위</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>指定</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>지정</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>할 수 있는 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>等</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>등</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>便利</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>편리</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>機能</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기능</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하실 수 있습니다.
</p><p>그래도 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>加入</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>가입</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하는 것이 꺼려진다면 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>非會員</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>비회원</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>狀態</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>상태</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 바로 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>始作</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>시작</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하셔도 됩니다!
