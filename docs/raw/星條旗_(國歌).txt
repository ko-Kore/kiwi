<h2><span id="歌詞(가사)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.AD.8C.E8.A9.9E.28.EA.B0.80.EC.82.AC.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>歌詞</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>가사</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E6%98%9F%E6%A2%9D%E6%97%97_(%E5%9C%8B%E6%AD%8C)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 歌詞(가사)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>上述</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>상술</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한대로 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>國歌</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>국가</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>基盤</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기반</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이되는<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>詩</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>戰時</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>전시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에<ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>作成</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>작성</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되었으며, <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>歌詞亦是</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>가사역시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>當時</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>당시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>戰狀</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>전상</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>反映</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>반영</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하고있다. 또한, 이러한<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>歌詞</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>가사</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>通</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>통</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>英</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>영</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby><a href="/index.php?title=%E8%BB%8D%E8%89%A6&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="軍艦 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade20 unknown"><rb>軍艦</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>군함</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>의<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>徹夜砲擊後</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>철야포격후</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에도 <ruby class="hanja grade10"><rb>聳然</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>용연</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>히 서있었던 <a href="/index.php?title=%EB%A7%A5%ED%95%B8%EB%A6%AC%E8%A6%81%E5%A1%9E&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="맥핸리要塞 (없는 문서)">맥핸리<ruby class="hanja grade32 unknown"><rb>要塞</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>요새</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>위 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>星條旗</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>성조기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의모습을 <ruby class="hanja grade10"><rb>窺知</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>규지</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>할수있다.
</p><p>O say can you see, by the dawn’s early light,<br>
What so proudly we hailed at the twilight’s last gleaming,<br>
Whose broad stripes and bright stars through the perilous fight,<br>
O’er the ramparts we watched, were so gallantly streaming?<br>
And the rocket’s red glare, the bombs bursting in air,<br>
Gave proof through the night that our flag was still there;<br>
O say does that star-spangled banner yet wave<br>
O’er the land of the free and the home of the brave?<br>
<br>
On the shore dimly seen through the mists of the deep,<br>
Where the foe’s haughty host in dread silence reposes,<br>
What is that which the breeze, o’er the towering steep,<br>
As it fitfully blows, half conceals, half discloses?<br>
Now it catches the gleam of the morning’s first beam,<br>
In full glory reflected now shines in the stream:<br>
’Tis the star-spangled banner, O long may it wave<br>
O’er the land of the free and the home of the brave.<br>
<br>
And where is that band who so vauntingly swore<br>
That the havoc of war and the battle’s confusion,<br>
A home and a country, should leave us no more?<br>
Their blood has washed out their foul footsteps’ pollution.<br>
No refuge could save the hireling and slave<br>
From the terror of flight, or the gloom of the grave:<br>
And the star-spangled banner in triumph doth wave,<br>
O’er the land of the free and the home of the brave.<br>
<br>
O thus be it ever, when freemen shall stand<br>
Between their loved homes and the war’s desolation.<br>
Blest with vict’ry and peace, may the Heav’n rescued land<br>
Praise the Power that hath made and preserved us a nation!<br>
Then conquer we must, when our cause it is just,<br>
And this be our motto: ‘In God is our trust.’<br>
And the star-spangled banner in triumph shall wave<br>
O’er the land of the free and the home of the brave!
