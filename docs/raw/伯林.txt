<h2><span id="槪要(개요)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E4%BC%AF%E6%9E%97&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要(개요)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><b><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>伯林</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>백림</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></b>은&nbsp;<a href="/wiki/%E7%8D%A8%E9%80%B8" title="獨逸"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>獨逸</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>독일</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>의&nbsp;<a href="/wiki/%E9%A6%96%E9%83%BD" title="首都"><ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>首都</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>수도</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>이다. <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>都市</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>도시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>全體</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>전체</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 <a href="/index.php?title=%EB%B8%8C%EB%9E%80%EB%8D%B4%EB%B6%80%EB%A5%B4%ED%81%AC%E5%B7%9E&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="브란덴부르크州 (없는 문서)">브란덴부르크<ruby class="hanja grade52 unknown"><rb>州</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>주</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>에 둘러싸여 있으며 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>伯林</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>백림</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>自體</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자체</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>도 <a href="/index.php?title=%E8%81%AF%E9%82%A6%E5%B7%9E&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="聯邦州 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade30 unknown"><rb>聯邦州</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>연방주</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>이다.
</p>
