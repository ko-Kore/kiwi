<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Temasek">Temasek</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E6%96%B0%E5%8A%A0%E5%9D%A1&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="部分 編輯: Temasek">編輯</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
