<h2><span id="槪要(개요)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E8%8A%B1%E5%9C%92%E6%98%8E%E6%9C%9D&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要(개요)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>花園明朝</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>화원명조</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>(はなぞのみんちょう, Hanazono Mincho)는 <a href="/wiki/%E6%98%8E%E6%9C%9D%E9%AB%94" title="明朝體"><ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>明朝體</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>명조체</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>基盤</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기반</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <a href="/index.php?title=%E9%9F%93%E4%B8%AD%E6%97%A5&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="韓中日 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade80 unknown"><rb>韓中日</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한중일</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>漢字書體</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한자서체</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다. <a href="/index.php?title=%EC%9C%A0%EB%8B%88%EC%BD%94%EB%93%9C&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="유니코드 (없는 문서)">유니코드</a>의 거의 모든 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>漢字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>支援</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>지원</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하며, <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>非漢字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>비한자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <a href="/wiki/%E6%96%87%E5%AD%97" title="文字"><ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>文字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>문자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>도 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>一部</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일부</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>包含</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>포함</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한다.
</p>
