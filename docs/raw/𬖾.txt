<h2><span id="槪要(개요)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%F0%AC%96%BE&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要(개요)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>𬖾(phở, 퍼)는 쌀국수 <a href="/index.php?title=%F0%A5%B9%98%F0%AC%96%BE&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="𥹘𬖾 (없는 문서)">𥹘𬖾</a>를 쇠고기나 닭고기 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>肉水</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>육수</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 말아먹는 <a href="/index.php?title=%E8%B6%8A%E5%8D%97&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="越南 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade32 unknown"><rb>越南</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>월남</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>의 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>國民飮食</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>국민음식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다.
</p>
<h2><span id="種類(종류)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E7.A8.AE.E9.A1.9E.28.EC.A2.85.EB.A5.98.29"><ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>種類</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>종류</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%F0%AC%96%BE&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 種類(종류)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span id="肉水別(육수별)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E8.82.89.E6.B0.B4.E5.88.A5.28.EC.9C.A1.EC.88.98.EB.B3.84.29"><ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>肉水別</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>육수별</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%F0%AC%96%BE&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="부분 편집: 肉水別(육수별)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>一般的</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일반적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로는 쇠고기나 닭고기 <a href="/index.php?title=%E8%82%89%E6%B0%B4&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="肉水 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade42 unknown"><rb>肉水</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>육수</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>를 쓴다.
</p>
<ul><li>𬖾𪃿(phở gà, 퍼 가) — 닭고기 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>肉水</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>육수</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></li>
<li>𬖾𩵽(phở tôm, 퍼 똠) — 새우<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>肉水</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>육수</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></li>
<li>𬖾𤙭(phở bò, 퍼 보) — 쇠고기 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>肉水</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>육수</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></li>
<li>𬖾<ruby class="hanja grade0"><rb>螠</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>의</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>(phở ếch, 퍼 엑) — 개구리 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>肉水</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>육수</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></li>
<li>𬖾<ruby class="hanja grade0"><rb>㺧</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>효</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>(phở heo, 퍼 해오) — 돼지고기 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>肉水</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>육수</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></li></ul>
