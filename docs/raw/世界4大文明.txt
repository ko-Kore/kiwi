<p><ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>世界四大文明</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>세계사대문명</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이란 <ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>中國</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>중국</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>, <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>印度</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>인도</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>, 이집트(<ruby class="hanja grade12"><rb>埃及</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>애급</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>), 메소포타미아 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>地域</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>지역</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>發興</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>발흥</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>했던 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>古代</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>고대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>文明</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>문명</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>意味</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>의미</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한다.
</p><p>네 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>文明</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>문명</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 모두 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>農耕</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>농경</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>有利</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>유리</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>溫和</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>온화</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>氣候</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기후</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>地域</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>지역</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>과 큰 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>江</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>강</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>流域</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>유역</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에서 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>發生</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>발생</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>했다는 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>共通點</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>공통점</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 있다.
</p><p><ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>參考</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>참고</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 네 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>文明</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>문명</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 모두 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>靑銅器</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>청동기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>時代</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>시대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>該當</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>해당</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하며 또한 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>歷史時代</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>역사시대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>該當</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>해당</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한다. 그러나 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>韓國史</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한국사</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>境遇</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>경우</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>鐵器</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>철기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>時代</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>시대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>부터 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>歷史時代</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>역사시대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>進入</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>진입</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한다.
</p>

<h3><span id="이집트_文明(문명)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".EC.9D.B4.EC.A7.91.ED.8A.B8_.E6.96.87.E6.98.8E.28.EB.AC.B8.EB.AA.85.29">이집트 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>文明</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>문명</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E4%B8%96%E7%95%8C4%E5%A4%A7%E6%96%87%E6%98%8E&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="부분 편집: 이집트 文明(문명)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
