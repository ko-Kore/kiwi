grade70"><rb>入力</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>입력</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하려면 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>中間</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>중간</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>暫</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>잠</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>깐 기다리거나 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>分離</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>분리</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>爲</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>위</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 키를 누른다. <a href="/index.php?title=%E6%BF%81%E9%BB%9E&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="濁點 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade30 unknown"><rb>濁點</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>탁점</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>과 <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%8D%8A%E6%BF%81%E9%BB%9E&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="半濁點 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade30 unknown"><rb>半濁點</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>반탁점</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>은 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>別途</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>별도</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 키로 붙인다.
</p>
<h3><span id=".ED.94.8C.EB.A6.AD.E5.85.A5.E5.8A.9B.28.EC.9E.85.EB.A0.A5.29"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="플릭入力(입력)">플릭<ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>入力</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>입력</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E5%81%87%E5%90%8D%E5%85%A5%E5%8A%9B&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="부분 편집: 플릭入力(입력)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="thumb tleft"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:202px;"><a href="/wiki/%ED%8C%8C%EC%9D%BC:Flick_keyboard_kayout.svg" class="image"><img alt="" src="https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/7/7b/Flick_keyboard_kayout.svg/langko-200px-Flick_keyboard_kayout.svg.png" decoding="async" width="200" height="271" class="thumbimage" srcset="https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/7/7b/Flick_keyboard_kayout.svg/langko-300px-Flick_keyboard_kayout.svg.png 1.5x, https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/7/7b/Flick_keyboard_kayout.svg/langko-400px-Flick_keyboard_kayout.svg.png 2x"></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/%ED%8C%8C%EC%9D%BC:Flick_keyboard_kayout.svg" class="internal" title="實際 크기로"></a></div>플릭入力의 各 方向에 對應되는 文字의 說明</div></div></div>
<p>토글<ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>入力</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>입력</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에서 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>發展</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>발전</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>시켜 <a href="/index.php?title=%ED%84%B0%EC%B9%98%EC%8A%A4%ED%81%AC%EB%A6%B0&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="터치스크린 (없는 문서)">터치스크린</a>에 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>特化</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>특화</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>된 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>形態</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>형태</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로, 한 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>行</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>행</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>文字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>문자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>連打</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>연타</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>選擇</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>선택</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하는 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>代身</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대신</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 터치+<ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>上下左右</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>상하좌우</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 각 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>方向</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>방향</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>段</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>단</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>配定</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>배정</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되어 슥슥 미는 것으로 빠르게 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>入力</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>입력</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>할 수 있게 한 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>方式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>방식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다.
</p><p>이걸 더욱 <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>擴張</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>확장</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해서 플릭<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>途中</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>도중</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>方向</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>방향</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 바꾸는 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 <a href="/index.php?title=%E6%8B%97%E9%9F%B3&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="拗音 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade10 unknown"><rb>拗音</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>요음</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>이나 <a href="/index.php?title=%E9%95%B7%E9%9F%B3&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="長音 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade62 unknown"><rb>長音</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>장음</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>까지 터치 한<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>番</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>번</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>入力</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>입력</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>할수있게한 <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="https://prtimes.jp/main/html/rd/p/000000016.000024473.html">턴 플릭</a>이란 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>物件</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>물건</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>도 있다.
</p>
