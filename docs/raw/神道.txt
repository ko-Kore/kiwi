<html><head></head><body><div class="mw-parser-output"><p><b><ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>神道</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>신도</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></b>는 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>日本</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일본</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%AE%97%E6%95%8E&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="宗敎 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade42 unknown"><rb>宗敎</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>종교</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>이다. <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>惟神道</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>유신도</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>라고도합니다. <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>敎典</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>교전</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이나 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>具體的</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>구체적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>인 가르침은 없으며 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>開祖</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개조</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>도 없다. <a href="/index.php?title=%E7%A5%9E%E8%A9%B1&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="神話 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade62 unknown"><rb>神話</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>신화</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>, <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>八百萬</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>팔백만</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>神</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>신</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>, <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>自然</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자연</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>과 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>自然現象</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자연현상</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>等</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>등</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 기초한 <a href="/index.php?title=%E6%B1%8E%E9%9D%88%E8%AB%96&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="汎靈論 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade20 unknown"><rb>汎靈論的</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>범령론적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>·<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>祖靈崇拜的</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>조령숭배적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>인 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>民族宗敎</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>민족종교</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다.
</p><p>또한 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>神道</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>신도</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>宗敎</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>종교</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 아니라는 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>見解</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>견해</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>도 있다(<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>神社非宗敎論</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>신사비종교론</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>).
</p><p><ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>自然</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자연</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>과 하느님은 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>一體</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일체</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>認識</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>인식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되어 하느님과 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>人間</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>인간</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 잇는 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>具體的</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>구체적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>인 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>作法</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>작법</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>祭祀</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>제사</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이며, 그 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>祭祀</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>제사</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 하는 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>場所</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>장소</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>神社</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>신사</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이며 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>聖域</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>성역</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 여겨졌다.
</p>
<h2><span id="이름"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".EC.9D.B4.EB.A6.84">이름</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E7%A5%9E%E9%81%93&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 이름">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>宗敎</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>종교</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 이름의 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>大部分</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대부분</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 “○○<ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>敎</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>교</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>”라고 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>呼稱</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>호칭</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하지만, 이 이름은 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>神道敎</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>신도교</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 아니라 “<ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>神道</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>신도</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>”이다.
</p><p>“<ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>神道</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>신도</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>”라는 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>名稱</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>명칭</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>對</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해서는 “오직 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>神</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>신</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 뜻 그대로의 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>道</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>도</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>”이란 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>意味</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>의미</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다. “<a href="/index.php?title=%E6%98%93%E7%B6%93&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="易經 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade40 unknown"><rb>易經</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>역경</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>”<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>이나 “<a href="/index.php?title=%E6%99%89%E6%9B%B8&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="晉書 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade20 unknown"><rb>晉書</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>진서</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>”<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup> 속에 보이는 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>神道</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>신도</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는 “<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>神奇</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>신기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>道</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>도</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>”라는 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>意味</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>의미</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이며, 이는 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>日本</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일본</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>神道</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>신도</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>觀念</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>관념</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>과는 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>性質</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>성질</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 다른 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>別個</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>별개</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 것이다.
</p><p><ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>日本</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일본</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에서의 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>神道</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>신도</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>라는 말의 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>初出</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>초출</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 “<a href="/index.php?title=%E6%97%A5%E6%9C%AC%E6%9B%B8%E7%B4%80&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="日本書紀 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade40 unknown"><rb>日本書紀</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일본서기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>”의 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>用明天皇</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>용명천황</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>卽位前紀</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>즉위전기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 있는 “<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>天皇</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>천황</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>佛法</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>불법</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 믿고 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>神道</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>신도</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>尊崇</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>존숭</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>했다”<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup>이지만, 이와 같이 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>外來</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>외래</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>宗敎</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>종교</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>인 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>佛敎</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>불교</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>와 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>雙</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>쌍</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 이루는 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>日本</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일본</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>固有</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>고유</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>信仰</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>신앙</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 가리킨 것 이었다.
</p><p>19<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>世紀</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>세기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>末期</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>말기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 되면 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>西洋</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>서양</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>近代的</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>근대적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>인 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>宗敎</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>종교</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪念</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개념</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>日本</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일본</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에서도 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>輸入</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>수입</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되어 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>宗敎</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>종교</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로서의 “<ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>神道</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>신도</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>”라는 말도 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>定着</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>정착</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하기 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>始作</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>시작</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>했다.
</p>
