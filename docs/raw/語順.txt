<h2><span id="槪要(개요)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E8%AA%9E%E9%A0%86&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要(개요)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><b><ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>語順</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>어순</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></b>은 <a href="/wiki/%E8%A8%80%E8%AA%9E" title="言語"><ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>言語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>언어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>에 있어서 한 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>文章</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>문장</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 안에 <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%96%AE%E8%AA%9E&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="單語 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade42 unknown"><rb>單語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>단어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>나 <a href="/index.php?title=%E8%AA%9E%E7%AF%80&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="語節 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade52 unknown"><rb>語節</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>어절</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>이 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>整列</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>정렬</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되는 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>順序</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>순서</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 말한다.
</p>
<h2><span id="基本語順(기본어순)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E5.9F.BA.E6.9C.AC.E8.AA.9E.E9.A0.86.28.EA.B8.B0.EB.B3.B8.EC.96.B4.EC.88.9C.29"><ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>基本語順</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기본어순</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E8%AA%9E%E9%A0%86&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 基本語順(기본어순)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>言語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>언어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 따라 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>語順</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>어순</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>柔軟</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>유연</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하여 한 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>意味</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>의미</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 나타내는 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>文章</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>문장</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>多樣</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>다양</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>語順</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>어순</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 나타낼 수 있는 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>境遇</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>경우</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>도 있는데, 이럴 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>境遇</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>경우</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에는 그 <ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>中</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>중</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 하나가 <b><ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>基本語順</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기본어순</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></b> <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>或</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>혹</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 <b><ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>支配的語順</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>지배적어순</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></b>이 된다.
</p><p>그 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>例</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>예</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 <a href="/index.php?title=%EB%9D%BC%ED%8B%B4%E8%AA%9E&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="라틴語 (없는 문서)">라틴<ruby class="hanja grade70 unknown"><rb>語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>나 <a href="/index.php?title=%ED%95%80%EB%9E%80%EB%93%9C%E8%AA%9E&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="핀란드語 (없는 문서)">핀란드<ruby class="hanja grade70 unknown"><rb>語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>는 <ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>定型的</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>정형적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>인 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>語順</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>어순</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 따로 없지만 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>傾向上</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>경향상</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>前者</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>전자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 SOV<ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>型</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>형</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>, <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>後者</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>후자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 SVO<ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>型</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>형</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 본다.
</p>
