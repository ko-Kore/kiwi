<h2><span id="呼稱(호칭)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E5.91.BC.E7.A8.B1.28.ED.98.B8.EC.B9.AD.29"><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>呼稱</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>호칭</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E5%B0%B9%E9%8C%AB%E6%82%85&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 呼稱(호칭)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>이름의 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>發音法</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>발음법</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>對</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>論難</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>논란</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 있었다. <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%9C%8B%E7%AB%8B%E5%9C%8B%E8%AA%9E%E9%99%A2&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="國立國語院 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade50 unknown"><rb>國立國語院</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>국립국어원</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>의 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>發音原則</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>발음원칙</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>대로라면 ‘윤서결’이 맞지만, <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>本人</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>본인</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>選好度</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>선호도</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>와 <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%9B%BA%E6%9C%89%E5%90%8D%E8%A9%9E&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="固有名詞 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade32 unknown"><rb>固有名詞</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>고유명사</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>라는 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>點</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>점</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>考慮</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>고려</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해서 ‘윤성녈’도 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>慣用的</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>관용적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>許容</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>허용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>된다. <a rel="nofollow" class="external autonumber" href="https://www.news1.kr/articles/?4614759">[1]</a>
</p>
