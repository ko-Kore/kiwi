<h2><span id="槪要(개요)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E6%BC%A2%E5%AD%97%E8%B7%AF_%ED%95%9C%EA%B8%80%C2%B7%E6%BC%A2%E5%AD%97_%E8%87%AA%E5%8B%95%E8%AE%8A%E6%8F%9B%E6%A9%9F&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要(개요)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>漢字路</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한자로</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는 <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%82%B3%E7%B5%B1%E6%96%87%E5%8C%96%E7%A1%8F%E7%A9%B6%E6%9C%83&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="傳統文化硏究會 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade42 unknown"><rb>傳統文化硏究會</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>전통문화연구회</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>에서 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>開發</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개발</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>·<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>運榮</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>운영</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하는 <a href="/index.php?title=%EC%9B%B9_%EC%82%AC%EC%9D%B4%ED%8A%B8&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="웹 사이트 (없는 문서)">웹 사이트</a>이다. '한글·<ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>漢字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>自動變換機</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자동변환기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>'라는 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>道具</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>도구</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>內藏</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>내장</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되어 <a href="/index.php?title=%EC%9B%B9_%EC%82%AC%EC%9D%B4%ED%8A%B8&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="웹 사이트 (없는 문서)">웹 사이트</a>를 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>國漢文混用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>국한문혼용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>變換</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>변환</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해서 볼 수 있다.
</p>
<h2><span id="機能(기능)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A9.9F.E8.83.BD.28.EA.B8.B0.EB.8A.A5.29"><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>機能</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기능</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E6%BC%A2%E5%AD%97%E8%B7%AF_%ED%95%9C%EA%B8%80%C2%B7%E6%BC%A2%E5%AD%97_%E8%87%AA%E5%8B%95%E8%AE%8A%E6%8F%9B%E6%A9%9F&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 機能(기능)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/wiki/%ED%95%9C%EA%B8%80%E5%B0%88%E7%94%A8" title="한글專用">한글<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>專用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>전용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>으로 된 <a href="/wiki/%E9%9F%93%E5%9C%8B%E8%AA%9E" title="韓國語"><ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>韓國語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한국어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> <a href="/index.php?title=%EC%9B%B9_%EC%82%AC%EC%9D%B4%ED%8A%B8&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="웹 사이트 (없는 문서)">웹 사이트</a>를 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>國漢文混用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>국한문혼용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>變換</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>변환</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해주는 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>機能</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기능</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>提供</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>제공</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한다. 메인 페이지의 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>住所欄</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>주소란</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>願</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>원</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하는 사이트의 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>住所</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>주소</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 입력하면 <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>該當</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>해당</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 사이트의 <a href="/wiki/%E6%BC%A2%E5%AD%97%E8%AA%9E" title="漢字語"><ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>漢字語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한자어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>를 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>漢字表記</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한자표기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 변환해준다. 텍스트 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>變換機能</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>변환기능</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>도 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>別途</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>별도</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>提供</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>제공</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한다.
</p><p>옵션을 바꿔 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>變換結果</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>변환결과</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 <a href="/wiki/%E5%9C%8B%E6%BC%A2%E6%96%87%E5%80%82%E7%94%A8" title="國漢文倂用">한글과 <ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>倂記</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>병기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>할지, <a href="/wiki/%E5%9C%8B%E6%BC%A2%E6%96%87%E6%B7%B7%E7%94%A8" title="國漢文混用"><ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>漢字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>만 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>表示</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>표시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>할지</a> <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>選擇</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>선택</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>할 수 있으며, <a href="/index.php?title=%E6%BC%A2%E5%AD%97%E6%AA%A2%E5%AE%9A%E8%A9%A6%E9%A9%97&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="漢字檢定試驗 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade42 unknown"><rb>漢字檢定試驗</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한자검정시험</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>의 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>級數別</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>급수별</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>變換</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>변환</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>할 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>漢字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>區分</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>구분</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>選擇</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>선택</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>할 수도 있다.
</p>
<h2><span id="短點(단점)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E7.9F.AD.E9.BB.9E.28.EB.8B.A8.EC.A0.90.29"><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>短點</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>단점</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E6%BC%A2%E5%AD%97%E8%B7%AF_%ED%95%9C%EA%B8%80%C2%B7%E6%BC%A2%E5%AD%97_%E8%87%AA%E5%8B%95%E8%AE%8A%E6%8F%9B%E6%A9%9F&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="부분 편집: 短點(단점)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/index.php?title=HTTPS&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="HTTPS (없는 문서)">HTTPS</a>를 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>支援</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>지원</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하지 않는지, https로 로드되는 이미지 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>等</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>등</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <a href="/index.php?title=%EB%A6%AC%EC%86%8C%EC%8A%A4&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="리소스 (없는 문서)">리소스</a>가 로드·<ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>表示</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>표시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되지 않거나 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>一部</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일부</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <a href="/index.php?title=%EC%82%AC%EC%9D%B4%ED%8A%B8&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="사이트 (없는 문서)">사이트</a>는 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>表示</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>표시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>조차 되지 않기도 한다.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/%ED%95%9C%EA%B8%80%E5%B0%88%E7%94%A8" title="한글專用">한글<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>專用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>전용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>文章</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>문장</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <a href="/wiki/%E6%BC%A2%E5%AD%97%E8%AA%9E" title="漢字語"><ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>漢字語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한자어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>를 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>人間</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>인간</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>檢修</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>검수</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 따위를 하지 않고 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>漢字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>變換</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>변환</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하는 것이기 때문에<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>, <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>誤變換</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>오변환</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>可能性</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>가능성</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 있으며 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>資料</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자료</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>等</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>등</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하려면 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>事前</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사전</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>確認</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>확인</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 거쳐야 한다.
</p>
<h2><span id="其他(기타)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E5.85.B6.E4.BB.96.28.EA.B8.B0.ED.83.80.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>其他</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기타</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E6%BC%A2%E5%AD%97%E8%B7%AF_%ED%95%9C%EA%B8%80%C2%B7%E6%BC%A2%E5%AD%97_%E8%87%AA%E5%8B%95%E8%AE%8A%E6%8F%9B%E6%A9%9F&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="부분 편집: 其他(기타)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/index.php?title=%E8%94%9A%E5%B1%B1%E5%A4%A7%E5%AD%B8%E6%A0%A1&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="蔚山大學校 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade12 unknown"><rb>蔚山大學校</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>울산대학교</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>韓國語處理硏究室</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한국어처리연구실</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에서 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>開發</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개발</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 ‘한글<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>漢字自動變換機</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한자자동변환기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>’을 바탕으로 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>開發</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개발</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되었다 하며, <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>該當</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>해당</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>技術</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기술</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://nlplab.ulsan.ac.kr/doku.php?id=utagger">UTagger</a>에 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>基盤</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기반</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 듯하다.
</p>
