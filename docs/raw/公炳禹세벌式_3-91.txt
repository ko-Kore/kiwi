<h2><span id=".E5.90.8D.E7.A8.B1.28.EB.AA.85.EC.B9.AD.29"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="名稱(명칭)"><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>名稱</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>명칭</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E5%85%AC%E7%82%B3%E7%A6%B9%EC%84%B8%EB%B2%8C%E5%BC%8F_3-91&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 名稱(명칭)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/index.php?title=MacOS&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="MacOS (없는 문서)">macOS</a>에는 '세벌식'으로, <a href="/index.php?title=%EC%9C%88%EB%8F%84%EC%9A%B0&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="윈도우 (없는 문서)">윈도우</a>에서는 '세벌식 최종'이라는 이름으로 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>提供</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>제공</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되지만, <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>名稱</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>명칭</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>不適切</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>부적절</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하다는 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>意見</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>의견</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 있다. '세벌식 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>最終</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>최종</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>'이라는 이름은 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>原來</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>원래</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> '세벌식 <ruby class="hanja grade12"><rb>公炳禹</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>공병우</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>最終</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>최종</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>', <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>卽</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>즉</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> "<a href="/index.php?title=%E5%85%AC%E7%82%B3%E7%A6%B9&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="公炳禹 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade12 unknown"><rb>公炳禹</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>공병우</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>가 만든 마지막 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>字板</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자판</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>" 이라는 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>意圖</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>의도</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 지어진듯 하나, <a href="/index.php?title=%EB%A7%88%EC%9D%B4%ED%81%AC%EB%A1%9C%EC%86%8C%ED%94%84%ED%8A%B8&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="마이크로소프트 (없는 문서)">마이크로소프트</a><ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>社</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <a href="/index.php?title=%EC%9C%88%EB%8F%84%EC%9A%B0%E9%81%8B%E7%87%9F%E9%AB%94%E5%88%B6&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="윈도우運營體制 (없는 문서)">윈도우<ruby class="hanja grade40 unknown"><rb>運營體制</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>운영체제</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>의 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>字板配列</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자판배열</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>選擇欄</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>선택란</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 '세벌식 최종'으로 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>表示</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>표시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>된걸 보고 많은 사람이 이 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>配列</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>배열</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 세벌<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>完成本</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>완성본</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이라고 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>錯覺</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>착각</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>狀態</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>상태</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>入門</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>입문</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한다는 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>問題</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>문제</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 있다.
</p><p>더더욱이 이 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>配列</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>배열</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>文章用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>문장용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>配列</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>배열</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>屬</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>속</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하여, <a href="/wiki/%EB%A1%9C%EB%A7%88%E5%AD%97" title="로마字">로마<ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>나 <a href="/wiki/%EB%91%90%EB%B2%8C%E5%BC%8F" title="두벌式">두벌<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>과는 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>數字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>숫자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>나 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>記號</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기호</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>位置</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>위치</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>들이 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>全</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>전</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>혀 달라, <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>入門難易度</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>입문난이도</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 어렵게 하거나 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>定着</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>정착</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>失敗</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>실패</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>原因</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>원인</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 되기도 한다. <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>反面</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>반면</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>, 두OS에서 함께 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>提供</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>제공</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되는 <a href="/index.php?title=%EC%84%B8%EB%B2%8C%E5%BC%8F_3-90&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="세벌式 3-90 (없는 문서)">세벌<ruby class="hanja grade60 unknown"><rb>式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 3-90</a>은 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>數字配列</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>숫자배열</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 <a href="/index.php?title=%ED%85%90%ED%82%A4&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="텐키 (없는 문서)">텐키</a>와 비슷한 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>配置</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>배치</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 맞춰져 있는데다 로마<ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 키보드의 모든 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>記號</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기호</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>配定</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>배정</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되어 있고 그 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>位置</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>위치</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>도 크게 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>差異</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>차이</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>나지 않는다.
</p><p>이런 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>理由</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>이유</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로, <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>前述</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>전술</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>問題點</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>문제점</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>意識</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>의식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하는 세벌<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用者</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는 '세벌식 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>最終</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>최종</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>' <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>代身</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대신</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> '세벌<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 3-91'<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>이라 부르기도 한다.
</p><p><a href="/index.php?title=%E5%85%AC%E7%82%B3%E7%A6%B9%EC%84%B8%EB%B2%8C%E5%BC%8F_3-90&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="公炳禹세벌式 3-90 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade12 unknown"><rb>公炳禹</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>공병우</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>세벌<ruby class="hanja grade60 unknown"><rb>式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 3-90</a>이 'IBM 3-90'이라 불린것과 같은 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>理由</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>이유</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 이 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>配列</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>배열</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 '<a href="/index.php?title=%EB%A7%A4%ED%82%A8%ED%86%A0%EC%8B%9C&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="매킨토시 (없는 문서)">매킨토시</a> 세벌<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>'이라 불리기도 했다.
</p>

<p><ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>文章用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>문장용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>配列</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>배열</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>基盤</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기반</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 하는 3-91은 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>事務室</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사무실</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>보다는 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>文人</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>문인</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>들이 <a href="/wiki/%E9%9F%93%E5%9C%8B%E8%AA%9E" title="韓國語"><ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>韓國語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한국어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>文章</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>문장</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 치는데 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하기에 좋게 되어 있다.
</p>

<dt><ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>韓國語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한국어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>文章用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>문장용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>記號</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기호</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>一部</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일부</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>包含</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>포함</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>됨</dt>
