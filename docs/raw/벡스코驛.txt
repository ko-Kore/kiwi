<h2><span id="槪要(개요)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%EB%B2%A1%EC%8A%A4%EC%BD%94%E9%A9%9B&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要(개요)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>벡스코<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>驛</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>역</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 <a href="/wiki/%E9%87%9C%E5%B1%B1%E5%BB%A3%E5%9F%9F%E5%B8%82" title="釜山廣域市"><ruby class="hanja grade12"><rb>釜山廣域市</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>부산광역시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> <a href="/wiki/%E6%B5%B7%E9%9B%B2%E8%87%BA%E5%8D%80" title="海雲臺區"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>海雲臺區</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>해운대구</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> <ruby class="hanja grade12"><rb>佑洞</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>우동</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>位置</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>위치</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 <a href="/wiki/%E9%87%9C%E5%B1%B1%E9%83%BD%E5%B8%82%E9%90%B5%E9%81%93_2%E8%99%9F%E7%B7%9A" title="釜山都市鐵道 2號線"><ruby class="hanja grade12"><rb>釜山都市鐵道</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>부산도시철도</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 2<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>號線</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>호선</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>과 <a href="/index.php?title=%E6%9D%B1%E6%B5%B7%E7%B7%9A&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="東海線 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade62 unknown"><rb>東海線</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>동해선</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>의 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>換乘驛</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>환승역</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다.
</p>
<h2><span id="名稱(명칭)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E5.90.8D.E7.A8.B1.28.EB.AA.85.EC.B9.AD.29"><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>名稱</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>명칭</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%EB%B2%A1%EC%8A%A4%EC%BD%94%E9%A9%9B&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 名稱(명칭)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>元來</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>원래</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>名稱</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>명칭</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 <b><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>市立美術館驛</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>시립미술관역</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></b>이었지만 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>東海線</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>동해선</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 벡스코<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>驛</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>역</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>開通</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개통</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하면서 <b>벡스코<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>驛</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>역</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></b>으로 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>改稱</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개칭</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되었다. 이름은 벡스코<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>驛</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>역</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이지만 <a href="/wiki/%EB%B2%A1%EC%8A%A4%EC%BD%94" title="벡스코">벡스코</a>와는 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>距離</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>거리</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 있어서 벡스코에 갈 때는 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>正門</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>정문</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>近處</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>근처</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>出口</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>출구</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 난 <a href="/index.php?title=%EC%84%BC%ED%85%80%EC%8B%9C%ED%8B%B0%E9%A9%9B&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="센텀시티驛 (없는 문서)">센텀시티<ruby class="hanja grade32 unknown"><rb>驛</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>역</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>을 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>利用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>이용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하는 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>境遇</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>경우</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 많다.
</p>

<h3><span id="2號線(호선)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="2.E8.99.9F.E7.B7.9A.28.ED.98.B8.EC.84.A0.29">2<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>號線</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>호선</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%EB%B2%A1%EC%8A%A4%EC%BD%94%E9%A9%9B&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="부분 편집: 2號線(호선)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a href="/index.php?title=%EC%98%AC%EB%A6%BC%ED%94%BD%E4%BA%A4%E5%8F%89%E8%B7%AF&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="올림픽交叉路 (없는 문서)">올림픽<ruby class="hanja grade10 unknown"><rb>交叉路</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>교차로</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>地下</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>지하</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>位置</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>위치</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하여 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>市內</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>시내</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>버스 <a href="/index.php?title=%EC%98%AC%EB%A6%BC%ED%94%BD%E4%BA%A4%E5%8F%89%E8%B7%AF_%E6%8F%9B%E4%B9%98%EC%84%BC%ED%84%B0&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="올림픽交叉路 換乘센터 (없는 문서)">올림픽<ruby class="hanja grade10 unknown"><rb>交叉路</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>교차로</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade32 unknown"><rb>換乘</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>환승</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>센터</a>와 <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>連繫</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>연계</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>된다.

<h2><span id="換乘地獄(환승지옥)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.8F.9B.E4.B9.98.E5.9C.B0.E7.8D.84.28.ED.99.98.EC.8A.B9.EC.A7.80.EC.98.A5.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>換乘地獄</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>환승지옥</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%EB%B2%A1%EC%8A%A4%EC%BD%94%E9%A9%9B&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="부분 편집: 換乘地獄(환승지옥)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>

</p>
