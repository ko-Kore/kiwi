<p><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>一般的</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일반적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%8F%8D%E8%8A%BB%E5%8B%95%E7%89%A9&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="反芻動物 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade10 unknown"><rb>反芻動物</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>반추동물</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>의 살점을 저민 뒤 <ruby class="hanja grade10"><rb>鹽醬</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>염장</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>, <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>乾燥</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>건조</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 및 <ruby class="hanja grade12"><rb>熏燃</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>훈연</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>過程</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>과정</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>製作</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>제작</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되는 <a href="/index.php?title=%E4%BF%9D%E5%AD%98%E9%A3%9F&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="保存食 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade40 unknown"><rb>保存食</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>보존식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>이다.
</p>
<h2><span id="詳細(상세)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E8.A9.B3.E7.B4.B0.28.EC.83.81.EC.84.B8.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>詳細</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>상세</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E8%82%89%E8%84%AF&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 詳細(상세)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>대부분이 <ruby class="hanja grade10"><rb>蛋白質</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>단백질</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 이루어져 있고, <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>製作</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>제작</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>과정으로 인해 압축된 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>食糧</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>식량</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이기 때문에 부피 대비 열량이 높다.
</p><p>이러한 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>特性</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>특성</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>利用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>이용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하여 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>過去</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>과거</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에는 <a href="/index.php?title=%E6%88%B0%E9%AC%AA%E9%A3%9F%E7%B3%A7&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="戰鬪食糧 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade40 unknown"><rb>戰鬪食糧</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>전투식량</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>으로 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되기도 하였고 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>一般</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일반</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>家庭</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>가정</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>保存食</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>보존식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로도 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되었다.
</p>
