<h2><span id="機能(기능)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A9.9F.E8.83.BD.28.EA.B8.B0.EB.8A.A5.29"><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>機能</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기능</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%EB%94%94%EC%8A%A4%EC%BD%94%EB%93%9C&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 機能(기능)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span id="音聲(음성)·映像通話(영상통화)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E9.9F.B3.E8.81.B2.28.EC.9D.8C.EC.84.B1.29.C2.B7.E6.98.A0.E5.83.8F.E9.80.9A.E8.A9.B1.28.EC.98.81.EC.83.81.ED.86.B5.ED.99.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>音聲</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>음성</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>·<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>映像通話</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>영상통화</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%EB%94%94%EC%8A%A4%EC%BD%94%EB%93%9C&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 音聲(음성)·映像通話(영상통화)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>登錄</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>등록</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>親舊</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>친구</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>와의 다이렉트 메시지(DM)와 <a href="/index.php?title=%E9%9F%B3%E8%81%B2%E9%80%9A%E8%A9%B1&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="音聲通話 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade42 unknown"><rb>音聲通話</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>음성통화</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>, <a href="/index.php?title=%E6%98%A0%E5%83%8F%E9%80%9A%E8%A9%B1&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="映像通話 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade32 unknown"><rb>映像通話</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>영상통화</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>, 컴퓨터 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>畵面共有</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>화면공유</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>支援</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>지원</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>된다. 그룹DM도 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>支援</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>지원</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하는데, <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>人員數</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>인원수</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 10<ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>名</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>명</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>까지로 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>制限</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>제한</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되지만 그 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>以上</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>이상</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>人員</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>인원</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 많아지면 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>後述</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>후술</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>할 ‘서버’<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>機能</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기능</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>利用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>이용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하는게 더 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>便</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>편</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하기 때문에 큰 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>意味</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>의미</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는 없다.
</p>
<h3><span id="서버"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".EC.84.9C.EB.B2.84">서버</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%EB%94%94%EC%8A%A4%EC%BD%94%EB%93%9C&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="부분 편집: 서버">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>‘서버’라 불리는 채팅 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>基盤</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기반</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>小規模</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>소규모</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 커뮤니티를 만들 수 있는데, <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>一般的</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일반적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>인 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>意味</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>의미</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <a href="/index.php?title=%EC%84%9C%EB%B2%84&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="서버 (없는 문서)">서버</a>와는 달리 호스팅을 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>直接</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>직접</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 할<ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>必要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>필요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는 없다.
</p><p>서버<ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>內</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>내</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에는 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>主題別</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>주제별</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 채팅채널이나 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>音聲</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>음성</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>채널을 둘 수 있고, 유저<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>別</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>별</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>役割</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>역할</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>分離</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>분리</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하여 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>役割</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>역할</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 따라 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>權限</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>권한</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 관리하거나 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>參與可能</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>참여가능</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 채널을 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>制限</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>제한</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>할수도 있다.
</p>
<h3><span id="봇"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".EB.B4.87">봇</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%EB%94%94%EC%8A%A4%EC%BD%94%EB%93%9C&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="부분 편집: 봇">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>公式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>공식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 봇<a href="/index.php?title=API&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="API (없는 문서)">API</a>를 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>提供</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>제공</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하고있어 봇을 만들 수 있다. 이 봇들은 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>個人對話</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개인대화</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>比</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>비</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해 서버에서 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>重要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>중요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>役割</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>역할</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 하는데, 봇에게도 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>役割</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>역할</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>割當</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>할당</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해 <a href="/index.php?title=%E7%AE%A1%E7%90%86%E8%80%85&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="管理者 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade40 unknown"><rb>管理者</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>관리자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a><ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>權限</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>권한</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>附與</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>부여</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>할 수도 있기 때문이다. <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>卽</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>즉</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>, <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>基本的</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기본적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>인 스팸이나 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>惡性</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>악성</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>유저의 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>接近</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>접근</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>自動</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자동</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 막을 수도 있고, 서버<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>管理</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>관리</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>必要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>필요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>行動</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>행동</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>自動化</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자동화</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>할수도 있는 것이다.
</p><p><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>管理者</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>관리자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 아닌 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>一般使用者</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일반사용자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>立場</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>입장</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에서의 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>活用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>활용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>도 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>無窮無盡</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>무궁무진</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한데, 서버<ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>活動</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>활동</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>動機</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>동기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가되도록 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>出席</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>출석</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이나 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>活動時間</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>활동시간</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>順位</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>순위</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>表示</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>표시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하는 봇부터 해서 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>音聲通話</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>음성통화</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>參加</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>참가</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해서 채팅 채널의 메시지를 읽어주거나, <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>多國籍</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>다국적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>서버의 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>境遇</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>경우</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>簡便</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>간편</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하게 <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>飜譯</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>번역</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 도와주는 봇도 있다. <a href="/wiki/%EB%A7%88%EC%9D%B8%ED%81%AC%EB%9E%98%ED%94%84%ED%8A%B8" title="마인크래프트">마인크래프트</a> 서버와 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>聯動</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>연동</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하여 서로 채팅을 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>傳達</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>전달</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해주는 봇도 쓰인다.
