<p>Battle for Dream Island <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>系列</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>계열</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>(<ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>韓</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>: 꿈의 섬을 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>爲</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>위</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>戰鬪</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>전투</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>)은 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>動畵師</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>동화사</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <a href="/index.php?title=Janknjellify&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Janknjellify (없는 문서)">janknjellify</a>가 만든 <a href="/index.php?title=%EC%98%A4%EB%B8%8C%EC%A0%9D%ED%8A%B8_%EC%95%A0%EB%8B%88%EB%A9%94%EC%9D%B4%EC%85%98&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="오브젝트 애니메이션 (없는 문서)">오브젝트 애니메이션</a>(<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>英</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>영</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>: Object show)이다. <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>總</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>총</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 5<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>個</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 시즌이 있다.
</p><p>모든 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>話</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>화</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에서 <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>挑戰</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>도전</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>主催者</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>주최자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>依</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>의</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>開催</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개최</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>된다. <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>敗北</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>패배</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>隊</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>/<ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>競爭者</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>경쟁자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>들은 Cake at Stake(케이크 앳 스테이크) 있을 것이다. 
</p>
