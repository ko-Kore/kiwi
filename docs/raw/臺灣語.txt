<h2><span id="槪要(개요)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E8%87%BA%E7%81%A3%E8%AA%9E&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要(개요)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><b><ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>臺灣語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대만어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></b>는 <a href="/index.php?title=%E8%87%BA%E7%81%A3%E5%B3%B6&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="臺灣島 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade20 unknown"><rb>臺灣島</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대만도</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>와 <a href="/index.php?title=%E6%BE%8E%E6%B9%96%E8%AB%B8%E5%B3%B6&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="澎湖諸島 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade10 unknown"><rb>澎湖諸島</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>팽호제도</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>에서 말하는 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>現地語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>현지어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 <a href="/index.php?title=%E9%96%A9%E8%AA%9E&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="閩語 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade0 unknown"><rb>閩語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>민어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>의 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>一部</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일부</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이며 <a href="/wiki/%E6%97%A5%E6%9C%AC%E8%AA%9E" title="日本語"><ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>日本語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일본어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>와 <ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>臺灣原住民</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대만원주민</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>諸言語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>제언어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>影響</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>영향</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>强</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>강</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하게 받고 있다. <ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>臺灣語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대만어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>母國語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>모국어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 하는 <ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>福建系臺灣人</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>복건계대만인</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 <ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>臺灣</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대만</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에서 가장 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>人口</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>인구</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 많은 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>族群</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>족군</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다.
</p>
<h2><span id="呼稱(호칭)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E5.91.BC.E7.A8.B1.28.ED.98.B8.EC.B9.AD.29"><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>呼稱</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>호칭</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E8%87%BA%E7%81%A3%E8%AA%9E&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 呼稱(호칭)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>現地</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>현지</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에서는 “<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>臺語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>”라고 불리는 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>境遇</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>경우</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 많다.
</p>
