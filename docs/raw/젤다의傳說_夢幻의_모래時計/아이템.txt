<h2><span id=".EC.8B.9C.EC.99.95.EC.9D.98_.E5.8A.8D.28.EA.B2.80.29"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="시왕의_劍(검)">시왕의 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>劍</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>검</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%EC%A0%A4%EB%8B%A4%EC%9D%98%E5%82%B3%E8%AA%AA_%E5%A4%A2%E5%B9%BB%EC%9D%98_%EB%AA%A8%EB%9E%98%E6%99%82%E8%A8%88/%EC%95%84%EC%9D%B4%ED%85%9C&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 시왕의 劍(검)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>게임 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>初盤</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>초반</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 첫번째 수수께끼를 풀면 시왕의 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>倉庫</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>창고</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>門</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>문</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 열리고 안쪽 <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%AF%B6%E7%89%A9%E7%AE%B1%E5%AD%90&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="寶物箱子 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade20 unknown"><rb>寶物箱子</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>보물상자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>에서 얻을 수 있다. <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>劍</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>검</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 가지고 나가면 시왕이 앞을 가로막고 <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%8A%8D%E8%A1%93&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="劍術 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade32 unknown"><rb>劍術</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>검술</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>訓練</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>훈련</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 마치면 보내주며 첫 <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>冒險</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>모험</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>始作</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>시작</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>된다.
</p><p>게임 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>極後半部</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>극후반부</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 <ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>夢幻</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>몽환</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>劍</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>검</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>獲得</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>획득</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하기 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>前</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>전</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>까지는 업그레이드가 없어 이 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>劍</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>검</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>繼續</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>계속</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하게 된다. <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>代身</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대신</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>精靈</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>정령</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 <ruby class="hanja grade10"><rb>覺醒</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>각성</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>시켜 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>威力</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>위력</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 올리거나 <a href="/index.php?title=%EC%86%8C%EB%93%9C%EB%B9%94&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="소드빔 (없는 문서)">소드빔</a>을 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>發射</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>발사</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>할수는 있다.
</p>
<h2><span id=".E9.98.B2.E7.89.8C.28.EB.B0.A9.ED.8C.A8.29"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="防牌(방패)"><ruby class="hanja grade10"><rb>防牌</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>방패</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%EC%A0%A4%EB%8B%A4%EC%9D%98%E5%82%B3%E8%AA%AA_%E5%A4%A2%E5%B9%BB%EC%9D%98_%EB%AA%A8%EB%9E%98%E6%99%82%E8%A8%88/%EC%95%84%EC%9D%B4%ED%85%9C&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 防牌(방패)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/index.php?title=%E8%90%AC%E7%89%A9%E5%95%86&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="萬物商 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade52 unknown"><rb>萬物商</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>만물상</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>에서 <ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>購入</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>구입</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>할 수 있는 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>基本的</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기본적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>인 <a href="/index.php?title=%E9%98%B2%E7%89%8C&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="防牌 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade10 unknown"><rb>防牌</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>방패</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>. <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>劍</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>검</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 꺼내들고 있으면 앞에서 오는 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>攻擊</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>공격</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>自動</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자동</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 막아준다. <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>劍</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>검</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>보다도 더한게, 이건 업그레이드가 아예 없다(...). <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>亦是</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>역시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>精靈</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>정령</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>强化</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>강화</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>할 수 있긴 하다.
</p>
<h2><span id=".E9.81.93.E5.85.B7.28.EB.8F.84.EA.B5.AC.29_.28.EC.95.84.EC.9D.B4.ED.85.9C.29"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="道具(도구)_(아이템)"><ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>道具</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>도구</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> (아이템)</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%EC%A0%A4%EB%8B%A4%EC%9D%98%E5%82%B3%E8%AA%AA_%E5%A4%A2%E5%B9%BB%EC%9D%98_%EB%AA%A8%EB%9E%98%E6%99%82%E8%A8%88/%EC%95%84%EC%9D%B4%ED%85%9C&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="부분 편집: 道具(도구) (아이템)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>畵面</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>화면</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>右下端</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>우하단</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 아이템 메뉴를 펼친 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>後</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>후</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>願</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>원</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하는 아이템을 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>選擇</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>선택</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하여 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>裝備</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>장비</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하고, <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>右上端</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>우상단</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 아이콘을 터치해서 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한다. 아이템 메뉴는 <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%8D%81%E5%AD%97%EB%B2%84%ED%8A%BC&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="十字버튼 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade70 unknown"><rb>十字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>십자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>버튼</a> 오른쪽이나 A버튼을 눌러서 펼칠수도 있고, <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>裝備</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>장비</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 아이템은 L 또는 R 트리거를 누르는 동안 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>할수도 있다. 보스<ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>戰</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>전</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>같이 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>强力</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>강력</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>攻擊</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>공격</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>繼續</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>계속</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 들어오는<ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>等</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>등</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 빠른 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>操作</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>조작</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>必要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>필요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>할때 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하면 편리하다.
</p>
