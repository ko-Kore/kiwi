<h2><span id="槪要(개요)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E6%B7%B8%E6%89%93%E6%A9%9F&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要(개요)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>淸打機</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>청타기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>活字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>활자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가<ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>平板</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>평판</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>配列</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>배열</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>된 <a href="/wiki/%E6%89%93%E5%AD%97%E6%A9%9F" title="打字機"><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>打字機</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>타자기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>의<ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>一種</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일종</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다. <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%AD%94%E7%89%88%E5%8D%B0%E5%88%B7&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="孔版印刷 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade32 unknown"><rb>孔版</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>공판</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>打字機</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>타자기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>라고도 불린다. <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>活字板</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>활자판</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을움직여 글<ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를찾아 하나씩<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>印字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>인자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하는<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>方式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>방식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한다.
</p>
<h2><span id="特徵(특징)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E7.89.B9.E5.BE.B5.28.ED.8A.B9.EC.A7.95.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>特徵</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>특징</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E6%B7%B8%E6%89%93%E6%A9%9F&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 特徵(특징)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>

<p><ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>日本</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일본</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에서 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>輸入</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>수입</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>된<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>製品</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>제품</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>活字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>활자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>만 <a href="/wiki/%ED%95%9C%EA%B8%80" title="한글">한글</a>로바꿔끼워 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>했다. <a href="/wiki/%ED%95%9C%EA%B8%80" title="한글">한글</a>과 <a href="/wiki/%E6%BC%A2%E5%AD%97" title="漢字"><ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>漢字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>가 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>音節別</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>음절별</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>, <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>頻度順</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>빈도순</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>配列</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>배열</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되어 있고, <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>必要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>필요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>할<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>境遇</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>경우</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>活字板</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>활자판</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에서 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>豫備活字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>예비활자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 뽑거나 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>活字板</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>활자판</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>自體</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자체</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>交換</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>교환</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해서 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한다.
