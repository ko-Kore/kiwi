<h2><span id="槪要(개요)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E5%A4%A7%E9%82%B1%E9%83%BD%E5%B8%82%E9%90%B5%E9%81%93&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要(개요)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade12"><rb>大邱都市鐵道</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대구도시철도</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는 <a href="/wiki/%E5%A4%A7%E9%82%B1%E5%BB%A3%E5%9F%9F%E5%B8%82" title="大邱廣域市"><ruby class="hanja grade12"><rb>大邱廣域市</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대구광역시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>의 <a href="/index.php?title=%E9%83%BD%E5%B8%82%E9%90%B5%E9%81%93&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="都市鐵道 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade50 unknown"><rb>都市鐵道</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>도시철도</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>體系</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>체계</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다. 1~2<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>號線</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>호선</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 <a href="/wiki/%E5%9C%B0%E4%B8%8B%E9%90%B5" title="地下鐵"><ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>地下鐵</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>지하철</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>이지만 3<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>號線</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>호선</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 <a href="/index.php?title=%EB%AA%A8%EB%85%B8%EB%A0%88%EC%9D%BC&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="모노레일 (없는 문서)">모노레일</a>이다.
</p>
<h2><span id="名稱(명칭)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E5.90.8D.E7.A8.B1.28.EB.AA.85.EC.B9.AD.29"><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>名稱</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>명칭</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E5%A4%A7%E9%82%B1%E9%83%BD%E5%B8%82%E9%90%B5%E9%81%93&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 名稱(명칭)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>市民</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>시민</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>들 사이에서는 1~2<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>號線</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>호선</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 <a href="/wiki/%E5%9C%B0%E4%B8%8B%E9%90%B5" title="地下鐵"><ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>地下鐵</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>지하철</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>이라고 불리며, <a href="/index.php?title=%EB%AA%A8%EB%85%B8%EB%A0%88%EC%9D%BC&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="모노레일 (없는 문서)">모노레일</a>인 3<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>號線</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>호선</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>地上鐵</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>지상철</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 이라고 불린다.
</p>
