<h2><span id="槪要(개요)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%EC%84%B8%EB%B2%8C%E5%BC%8F&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要(개요)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><b>세벌<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></b>(three-set type/Sebeolsik)은 한글 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>字板</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자판</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>中</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>중</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에서 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>配列</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>배열</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>初聲</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>초성</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>·<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>中聲</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>중성</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>·<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>終聲</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>종성</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 나뉜 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>方式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>방식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 말한다.
</p>
<h2><span id="特徵(특징)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E7.89.B9.E5.BE.B5.28.ED.8A.B9.EC.A7.95.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>特徵</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>특징</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%EC%84%B8%EB%B2%8C%E5%BC%8F&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 特徵(특징)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>子音</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자음</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>·<ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>母音</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>모음</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>區分</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>구분</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>된 <a href="/wiki/%EB%91%90%EB%B2%8C%E5%BC%8F" title="두벌式">두벌<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>과는 달리 <a href="/index.php?title=%EB%B0%9B%EC%B9%A8&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="받침 (없는 문서)">받침</a>이 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>別途</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>별도</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 키로 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>存在</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>존재</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한다. 때문에 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>一般的</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일반적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 두벌<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>比</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>비</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>配列</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>배열</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>된 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>字素</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자소</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>數</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>수</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 많으며, 그만큼 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>初期</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>초기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>學習</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>학습</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에도 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>時間</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>시간</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 더 든다.
</p><p>KS<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>標準</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>표준</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 두벌<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>非效率的</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>비효율적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>인 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>點</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>점</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>과 더불어 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>大體</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대체</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>打字時</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>타자시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>疲勞度</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>피로도</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 낮고 <a href="/index.php?title=%EB%8F%84%EA%B9%A8%EB%B9%84%EB%B6%88%E7%8F%BE%E5%83%8F&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="도깨비불現像 (없는 문서)">도깨비불<ruby class="hanja grade32 unknown"><rb>現像</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>현상</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>이 없다는 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>長點</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>장점</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>等</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>등</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>因</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>인</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>標準</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>표준</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>도 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>主流</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>주류</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>도 아니지만 어느<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>程度</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>정도</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <a href="/index.php?title=%EB%A7%88%EB%8B%88%EC%95%84&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="마니아 (없는 문서)">마니아</a><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>層</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>층</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 있으며, <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>字板</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자판</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>硏究</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>연구</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>·<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>開發</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개발</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 및 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>改善</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개선</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>도 <ruby class="hanja grade10"><rb>活潑</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>활발</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>히 이루어지고 있다.
</p>
