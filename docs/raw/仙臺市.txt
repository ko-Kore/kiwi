<h2><span id="名稱(명칭)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E5.90.8D.E7.A8.B1.28.EB.AA.85.EC.B9.AD.29"><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>名稱</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>명칭</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E4%BB%99%E8%87%BA%E5%B8%82&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 名稱(명칭)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>仙臺</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>선대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>地名由來</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>지명유래</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>對</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해서는 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>諸說</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>제설</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 있다. 『<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>仙臺事物起原考</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>선대사물기원고</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>』에 따르면 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>元來</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>원래</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>仙臺</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>선대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>서 모시고 있던 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>千體佛</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>천체불</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>關聯</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>관련</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>시켜 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>千體</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>천체</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>라고 불렸으나 그 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>後</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>후</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>雅名</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>아명</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>인 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>千代</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>천대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 쓰게 됐다. <a href="/index.php?title=%E4%BC%8A%E9%81%94%E6%94%BF%E5%AE%97&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="伊達政宗 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade12 unknown"><rb>伊達政宗</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>이달정종</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>이 <a href="/index.php?title=%E4%BB%99%E8%87%BA%E5%9F%8E&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="仙臺城 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade32 unknown"><rb>仙臺城</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>선대성</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>을 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>築城</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>축성</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>後</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>후</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>慶長</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>경장</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>5<ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>年</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>년</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> (1600<ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>年</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>년</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>) 12<ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>月</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>월</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>仙臺</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>선대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>改稱</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개칭</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>했다. 이 이름은 <a href="/wiki/%E4%B8%AD%E5%9C%8B" title="中國"><ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>中國</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>중국</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%94%90%EB%82%98%EB%9D%BC&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="唐나라 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade32 unknown"><rb>唐</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>당</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>나라</a> <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>詩人</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>시인</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>인 <a href="/index.php?title=%E9%9F%93%E7%BF%83&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="韓翃 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade0 unknown"><rb>韓翃</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한굉</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>이 적은 <a href="/index.php?title=%E4%B8%83%E8%A8%80&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="七言 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade60 unknown"><rb>七言</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>칠언</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a><a href="/index.php?title=%E5%BE%8B%E8%A9%A9&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="律詩 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade42 unknown"><rb>律詩</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>율시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> 『<a href="/index.php?title=%E5%90%8C%E9%A1%8C%E4%BB%99%E9%81%8A%E8%A7%80&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="同題仙遊觀 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade40
