</p>
<h2><span id=".E5.9C.B0.E5.9F.9F.28.EC.A7.80.EC.97.AD.29"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="地域(지역)"><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>地域</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>지역</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E5%8E%9F%E7%A5%9E&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 地域(지역)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span id=".EB.AA.AC.EB.93.9C"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="몬드">몬드</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E5%8E%9F%E7%A5%9E&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="부분 편집: 몬드">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>自由</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자유</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>와 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>風</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>풍</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>國家</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>국가</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다. 모티브는 <a href="/wiki/%EC%9C%A0%EB%9F%BD" class="mw-redirect" title="유럽">유럽</a>이다.
</p>
<h3><span id=".E7.92.83.E6.9C.88.28.EC.9D.B4.EC.9B.94.29"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="璃月(이월)"><ruby class="hanja grade0"><rb>璃月</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>이월</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E5%8E%9F%E7%A5%9E&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="부분 편집: 璃月(이월)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>

</p>
<h3><span id=".E7.A8.BB.E5.A6.BB.28.EB.8F.84.EC.B2.98.29"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="稻妻(도처)"><ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>稻妻</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>도처</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E5%8E%9F%E7%A5%9E&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="부분 편집: 稻妻(도처)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>永遠</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>영원</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>과 번개의 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>國家</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>국가</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>다. 모티브는 <a href="/wiki/%E6%97%A5%E6%9C%AC" title="日本"><ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>日本</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일본</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>이다.
