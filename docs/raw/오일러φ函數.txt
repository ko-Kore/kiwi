<p><b>오일러φ<ruby class="hanja grade10"><rb>函數</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>함수</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></b>(오일러 피 함수)는 <a href="/index.php?title=%E9%99%BD%E6%95%B4%E6%95%B8&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="陽整數 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade40 unknown"><rb>陽整數</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>양정수</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>n</a>에<ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>對</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해 1<ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>以上</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>이상</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>n<ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>以下</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>이하</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>陽整數</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>양정수</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의<ruby class="hanja grade10"><rb>數爻</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>수효</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를값으로하는<a href="/index.php?title=%E5%87%BD%E6%95%B8&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="函數 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade10 unknown"><rb>函數</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>함수</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>이다。이는<a href="/index.php?title=%E6%95%B8%E8%AB%96%E7%9A%84%E5%87%BD%E6%95%B8&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="數論的函數 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade10 unknown"><rb>數論的函數</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>수론적함수</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>이며、<a href="/index.php?title=%E4%B9%98%E6%B3%95%E7%9A%84%E5%87%BD%E6%95%B8&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="乘法的函數 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade10 unknown"><rb>乘法的函數</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>승법적함수</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>이다。<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>卽</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>즉</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>、<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>定義域</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>정의역</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>陽整數</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>양정수</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이며、서로<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>素</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>소</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>인모든<ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>自然數</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자연수</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>雙</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>쌍</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>(m,n)에<ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>對</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하여 φ(mn)=φ(m)φ(n)이다。
</p><p><ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>最初</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>최초</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 이<ruby class="hanja grade10"><rb>函數</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>함수</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를<ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>考案</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>고안</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한것은 <a href="/index.php?title=%EB%A0%88%EC%98%A8%ED%95%98%EB%A5%B4%ED%8A%B8_%EC%98%A4%EC%9D%BC%EB%9F%AC&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="레온하르트 오일러 (없는 문서)">레온하르트 오일러</a>이나、그는 이를<a href="/index.php?title=%CE%A0&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Π (없는 문서)">π</a>로<ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>表記</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>표기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하였다。<a href="/index.php?title=%CE%A6&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="Φ (없는 문서)">φ</a>로써의<ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>表記</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>표기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를처음<ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>考案</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>고안</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한것은 <a href="/index.php?title=%EC%B9%BC_%ED%94%84%EB%A6%AC%EB%93%9C%EB%A6%AC%ED%9E%88_%EA%B0%80%EC%9A%B0%EC%8A%A4&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="칼 프리드리히 가우스 (없는 문서)">칼 프리드리히 가우스</a>이며 <a href="/index.php?title=1801%E5%B9%B4&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="1801年 (없는 문서)">1801<ruby class="hanja grade80 unknown"><rb>年</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>년</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>의<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>著書</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>저서</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby><i>Disquisitiones Arithmeticae</i>（<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>算術硏究</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>산술연구</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>）에<ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>登場</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>등장</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한다。
</p><p><a href="/index.php?title=%E7%B4%A0%E6%95%B8&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="素數 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade42 unknown"><rb>素數</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>소수</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>p</a>에<ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>對</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하여k를<ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>任意</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>임의</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의<a href="/index.php?title=%E8%87%AA%E7%84%B6%E6%95%B8&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="自然數 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade70 unknown"><rb>自然數</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자연수</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>라하면、<code>p<sup>k</sup></code><ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>以下</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>이하</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의p의<ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>倍數</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>배수</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>正確</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>정확</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>히<code>p<sup>k-1</sup></code><ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>個</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>있으니、<code>φ(p<sup>k</sup>)=p<sup>k</sup>-p<sup>k-1</sup>=p<sup>k</sup>(1-1/p)</code>가<ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>成立</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>성립</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한다。또한φ<ruby class="hanja grade10"><rb>函數</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>함수</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>乘法的性質</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>승법적성질</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로써<ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>任意</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>임의</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의<ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>自然數</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자연수</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에<ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>對</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하여그값이<ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>計算可能</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>계산가능</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하다。
