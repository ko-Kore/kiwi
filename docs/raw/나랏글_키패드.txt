<h2><span id="槪要(개요)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%EB%82%98%EB%9E%8F%EA%B8%80_%ED%82%A4%ED%8C%A8%EB%93%9C&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要(개요)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>나랏글은 <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>携帶電話用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>휴대전화용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <a href="/wiki/%E9%9F%93%E5%9C%8B%E8%AA%9E_%E5%85%A5%E5%8A%9B%E6%96%B9%E5%BC%8F" title="韓國語 入力方式"><ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>韓國語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한국어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>入力方式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>입력방식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>이다.
</p>
<h2><span id="特徵(특징)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E7.89.B9.E5.BE.B5.28.ED.8A.B9.EC.A7.95.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>特徵</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>특징</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%EB%82%98%EB%9E%8F%EA%B8%80_%ED%82%A4%ED%8C%A8%EB%93%9C&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 特徵(특징)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>子母</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자모</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>左右</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>좌우</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>分離</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>분리</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>된 <a href="/wiki/%EB%91%90%EB%B2%8C%E5%BC%8F" title="두벌式">두벌<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>의 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>形態</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>형태</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이며, <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>子音入力時</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자음입력시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에는 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>連打</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>연타</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 아닌 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>別途</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>별도</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 ‘<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>劃追加</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>획추가</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>’ 키를 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한다. <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>子音</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자음</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 키 하나에 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>基本</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기본</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 되는 한 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>씩만 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>配定</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>배정</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되어 있기 때문에 <a href="/wiki/%E5%A4%A9%E5%9C%B0%E4%BA%BA%ED%82%A4%ED%8C%A8%EB%93%9C" title="天地人키패드"><ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>天地人</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>천지인</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>에서같이 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>子音衝突</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자음충돌</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 일어나지 않는다.
</p><p><ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>基本子音</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기본자음</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 ㄱ,ㄴ,ㄹ,ㅁ,ㅅ,ㅇ으로 6<ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>配列</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>배열</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되었으며, ㄱ+(<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>劃追加</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>획추가</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>)로 ㅋ, ㄱ+(<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>雙子音</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>쌍자음</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>)으로 ㄲ와 같이 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>加劃</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>가획</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의<ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>原理</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>원리</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>反映</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>반영</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되었다.
</p><p><ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>母音</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>모음</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 ㅏㅓ,ㅗㅜ,ㅡ,ㅣ로 키 4<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>個</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 6<ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>配定</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>배정</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되었으며 ㅏㅓ+ㅏㅓ+(<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>劃追加</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>획추가</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>)로 ㅕ를 입력하는 것과 같이 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>連打</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>연타</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>와 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>劃追加</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>획추가</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>키를 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하여 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>入力</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>입력</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한다.
</p>
