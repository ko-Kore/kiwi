<h2><span id="槪要(개요)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E6%AD%B7%E5%8F%B2%E7%9A%84%E5%81%87%E5%90%8D%E8%A1%A8%E8%A8%98%E6%B3%95&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要(개요)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><b><ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>歷史的假名表記法</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>역사적가명표기법</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></b>(<span class="noruby"><ruby class="hanja grade0"><rb>歴史的仮名遣</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>역사적가명견</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>い</span>, <span class="noruby"><ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>歷史的假名遣</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>역사적가명견</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>ひ</span>)은 <a href="/wiki/%E7%AC%AC%E4%BA%8C%E6%AC%A1%E4%B8%96%E7%95%8C%E5%A4%A7%E6%88%B0" title="第二次世界大戰"><ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>第二次世界大戰</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>제이차세계대전</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>以前</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>이전</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>까지 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>主</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>주</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 쓰인 <a href="/wiki/%E6%97%A5%E6%9C%AC%E8%AA%9E" title="日本語"><ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>日本語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일본어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>의 <a href="/wiki/%E5%81%87%E5%90%8D" title="假名"><ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>假名</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>가명</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a><ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>表記法</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>표기법</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다.
</p>
<h2><span id="名稱(명칭)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E5.90.8D.E7.A8.B1.28.EB.AA.85.EC.B9.AD.29"><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>名稱</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>명칭</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E6%AD%B7%E5%8F%B2%E7%9A%84%E5%81%87%E5%90%8D%E8%A1%A8%E8%A8%98%E6%B3%95&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 名稱(명칭)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>一般的</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일반적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 쓰이는 ‘<span class="noruby"><ruby class="hanja grade0"><rb>歴史的仮名遣</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>역사적가명견</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>い</span>’라는 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>名稱</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>명칭</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>外</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>외</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에도 <a href="/index.php?title=%E7%8F%BE%E4%BB%A3%E5%81%87%E5%90%8D%E8%A1%A8%E8%A8%98%E6%B3%95&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="現代假名表記法 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade42 unknown"><rb>現代假名表記法</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>현대가명표기법</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>과 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>對比</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대비</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되는 ‘<span class="noruby"><ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>舊假名遣</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>구가명견</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>ひ</span>(<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>舊假名表記法</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>구가명표기법</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>)’나, <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>保守的</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>보수적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>인 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>名稱</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>명칭</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>인 ‘<span class="noruby"><ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>正假名遣</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>정가명견</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>ひ</span>(<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>正假名表記法</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>정가명표기법</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>)’라는 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>名稱</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>명칭</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되기도 한다.
</p><p><a href="/index.php?title=%E8%88%8A%E5%AD%97%E9%AB%94&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="舊字體 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade52 unknown"><rb>舊字體</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>구자체</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>와 더불어 이 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>表記法</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>표기법</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 다시 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>採用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>채용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하자고 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>主張</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>주장</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하는 사람들 <ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>中</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>중</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>一部</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일부</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는 ‘<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>正字正假名</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>정자정가명</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>’라고 묶어 부르기도 한다.
</p><p><ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>漢字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의<a href="/index.php?title=%E9%9F%B3%E8%AE%80&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="音讀 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade62 unknown"><rb>音讀</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>음독</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>을 나타내는 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>假名表記法</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>가명표기법</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에는 여기에<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>對應</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대응</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되는 <a href="/wiki/%E6%AD%B7%E5%8F%B2%E7%9A%84%E5%AD%97%E9%9F%B3%E5%81%87%E5%90%8D%E8%A1%A8%E8%A8%98%E6%B3%95" title="歷史的字音假名表記法"><ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>歷史的字音假名表記法</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>역사적자음가명표기법</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>이 있다.
</p>
<h2><span id="特徵(특징)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E7.89.B9.E5.BE.B5.28.ED.8A.B9.EC.A7.95.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>特徵</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>특징</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E6%AD%B7%E5%8F%B2%E7%9A%84%E5%81%87%E5%90%8D%E8%A1%A8%E8%A8%98%E6%B3%95&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="부분 편집: 特徵(특징)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>戰後</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>전후</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>國語改革</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>국어개혁</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 <a href="/index.php?title=%E7%8F%BE%E4%BB%A3%E5%81%87%E5%90%8D%E8%A1%A8%E8%A8%98%E6%B3%95&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="現代假名表記法 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade42 unknown"><rb>現代假名表記法</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>현대가명표기법</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>이 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>公式化</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>공식화</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되기 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>前</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>전</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>까지 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>主</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>주</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>된 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>表記法</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>표기법</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다. <a href="/index.php?title=%E8%AA%9E%E6%96%87%E4%B8%80%E8%87%B4&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="語文一致 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade50 unknown"><rb>語文一致</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>어문일치</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>를 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>重視</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>중시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>現代假名表記法</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>현대가명표기법</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>과는 달리 <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%B9%B3%E5%AE%89%E6%99%82%E4%BB%A3&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="平安時代 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade62 unknown"><rb>平安時代</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>평안시대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>부터 이어져온 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>表記法</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>표기법</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>基盤</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기반</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하므로 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>語源</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>어원</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>重視</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>중시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한다.
</p>
<h3><span id="같은_發音(발음)의_表記法(표기법)_區別(구별)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".EA.B0.99.EC.9D.80_.E7.99.BC.E9.9F.B3.28.EB.B0.9C.EC.9D.8C.29.EC.9D.98_.E8.A1.A8.E8.A8.98.E6.B3.95.28.ED.91.9C.EA.B8.B0.EB.B2.95.29_.E5.8D.80.E5.88.A5.28.EA.B5.AC.EB.B3.84.29">같은 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>發音</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>발음</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>表記法</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>표기법</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>區別</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>구별</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E6%AD%B7%E5%8F%B2%E7%9A%84%E5%81%87%E5%90%8D%E8%A1%A8%E8%A8%98%E6%B3%95&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="부분 편집: 같은 發音(발음)의 表記法(표기법) 區別(구별)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a href="/index.php?title=%E5%B9%B3%E5%AE%89%E6%99%82%E4%BB%A3&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="平安時代 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade62 unknown"><rb>平安時代</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>평안시대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>에는 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>區別</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>구별</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되었지만 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>現在</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>현재</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는 같은 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>發音</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>발음</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>合</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>합</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>쳐진 글<ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>들을 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>區別</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>구별</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해서 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>表記</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>표기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한다. <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>現代假名表記法</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>현대가명표기법</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에도 は, へ, を의 <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%8A%A9%E8%A9%9E&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="助詞 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade32 unknown"><rb>助詞</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>조사</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a><ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>表記</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>표기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>等</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>등</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>限</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>一部</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일부</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 남아있지만, 여기서는 그 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>事例</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사례</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 훨씬 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>多樣</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>다양</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하며, <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>一般的</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일반적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>인 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>單語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>단어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에서도 많이 나타난다.
</p>
