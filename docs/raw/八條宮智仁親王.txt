<h2><span id=".EC.9D.B4.EB.A6.84"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="이름">이름</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E5%85%AB%E6%A2%9D%E5%AE%AE%E6%99%BA%E4%BB%81%E8%A6%AA%E7%8E%8B&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 이름">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>어린 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>時節</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>시절</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 이름은 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>六宮</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>육궁</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>, <ruby class="hanja grade0"><rb>胡佐麿</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>호좌마</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>, <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>員丸</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>원환</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>, <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>通稱</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>통칭</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>幸丸</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>행환</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>, <ruby class="hanja grade12"><rb>友輔</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>우보</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>. <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>一般的</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일반적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로는 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>八條皇子</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>팔조황자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>(八條の皇子)라고 불렸다.
</p>
