<h2><span id="槪要(개요)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E4%B8%80%E4%BC%8A%E9%82%A3%E7%88%BE%E6%A0%96&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要(개요)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<blockquote>
<p>이렇게보여도 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>古神</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>고신</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>司祭</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사제</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다. <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>某日</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>모일</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>「<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>異常</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>이상</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>冊</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>책</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>」을 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>拾得</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>습득</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한<ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>後</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>후</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로부터 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>觸手</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>촉수</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>操縱</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>조종</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>할수있게 되었다. 그<ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>女</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>여</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에게 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>觸手</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>촉수</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>日常生活</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일상생활</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의<ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>一部</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일부</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이며, <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>特別</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>특별</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>히 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>神經</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>신경</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 쓰지않는다. <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>但</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>단</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>, <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>盛裝</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>성장</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 하고싶은 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>少女心</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>소녀심</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>. 힘을얻은<ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>以來</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>이래</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>古代</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>고대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의목소리로부터 <ruby class="hanja grade0"><rb>呫囁</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>첩섭</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>와<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>天啓等</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>천계등</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을받아, <ruby class="hanja grade10"><rb>無垢</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>무구</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하며 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>至極</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>지극</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>히<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>普通</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>보통</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>인 그<ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>女</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>여</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는 Vtuber<ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>活動</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>활동</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>通</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>통</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해 사람들의SAN<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>値</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>치</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>削減</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>삭감</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하고있는<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>模樣</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>모양</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>.
