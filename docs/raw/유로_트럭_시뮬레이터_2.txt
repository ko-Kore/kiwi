<h2><span id="槪要(개요)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%EC%9C%A0%EB%A1%9C_%ED%8A%B8%EB%9F%AD_%EC%8B%9C%EB%AE%AC%EB%A0%88%EC%9D%B4%ED%84%B0_2&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要(개요)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><b>유로 트럭 시뮬레이터 2</b>는 SCS 소프트웨어의 시뮬레이션 게임으로, <a href="/index.php?title=%EC%9C%A0%EB%A1%9C_%ED%8A%B8%EB%9F%AD_%EC%8B%9C%EB%AE%AC%EB%A0%88%EC%9D%B4%ED%84%B0&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="유로 트럭 시뮬레이터 (없는 문서)">유로 트럭 시뮬레이터</a> 시리즈의 두<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>番</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>번</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>째 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>作品</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>작품</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다. <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>物流會社</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>물류회사</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 차려 <a href="/index.php?title=%EC%9C%A0%EB%9F%BD&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="유럽 (없는 문서)">유럽</a>을 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>背景</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>배경</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 <ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>大型</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대형</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby><a href="/index.php?title=%ED%8A%B8%EB%9F%AD&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="트럭 (없는 문서)">트럭</a>을 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>運轉</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>운전</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하며 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>貨物</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>화물</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>運送</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>운송</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하고, 트럭과 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>車庫</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>차고</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 <ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>購入</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>구입</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하고 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>運轉者</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>운전자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 <ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>雇傭</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>고용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>收益</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>수익</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 낼 수 있다.
</p>
<h2><span id="게임_플레이"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".EA.B2.8C.EC.9E.84_.ED.94.8C.EB.A0.88.EC.9D.B4">게임 플레이</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%EC%9C%A0%EB%A1%9C_%ED%8A%B8%EB%9F%AD_%EC%8B%9C%EB%AE%AC%EB%A0%88%EC%9D%B4%ED%84%B0_2&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 게임 플레이">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span id="始作(시작)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E5.A7.8B.E4.BD.9C.28.EC.8B.9C.EC.9E.91.29"><ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>始作</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>시작</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%EC%9C%A0%EB%A1%9C_%ED%8A%B8%EB%9F%AD_%EC%8B%9C%EB%AE%AC%EB%A0%88%EC%9D%B4%ED%84%B0_2&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="부분 편집: 始作(시작)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>플레이어는 유럽에서 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>運輸會社</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>운수회사</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 차리는 것으로 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>始作</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>시작</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한다. <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>會社名</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>회사명</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>과 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>社長</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사장</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 이름, <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>選好</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>선호</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하는 트럭 브랜드와 첫 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>車庫</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>차고</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>位置</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>위치</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>定</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>정</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하면 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>初期資本</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>초기자본</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 2000<a href="/index.php?title=%EC%9C%A0%EB%A1%9C&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="유로 (없는 문서)">유로</a>를 가지고 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>會社</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>회사</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>始作</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>시작</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하게 된다.
</p>
<h3><span id="貨物(화물)_運送(운송)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E8.B2.A8.E7.89.A9.28.ED.99.94.EB.AC.BC.29_.E9.81.8B.E9.80.81.28.EC.9A.B4.EC.86.A1.29"><ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>貨物</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>화물</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>運送</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>운송</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%EC%9C%A0%EB%A1%9C_%ED%8A%B8%EB%9F%AD_%EC%8B%9C%EB%AE%AC%EB%A0%88%EC%9D%B4%ED%84%B0_2&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="부분 편집: 貨物(화물) 運送(운송)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>처음에는 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>會社</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>회사</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는 있지만 <a href="/index.php?title=%ED%8A%B8%EB%9F%AD&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="트럭 (없는 문서)">트럭</a>이 없어 ‘빠른 일거리’를 받아 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>每</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>매</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>件</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>건</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>마다 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>提供</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>제공</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해주는 트럭을 빌려 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하게 된다. 이 때는 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>訪問</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>방문</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 적이 있는 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>都市</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>도시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 어디서든 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>始作</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>시작</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>할 수 있지만, 첫 트럭을 <ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>購買</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>구매</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>後</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>후</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에는 먼저 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>出發地點</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>출발지점</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>까지 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>移動</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>이동</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해서 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>貨物</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>화물</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 받아야 한다.
<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>運送</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>운송</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>完了</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>완료</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하면 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>結果</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>결과</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 따라 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>運送手當</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>운송수당</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>과 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>經驗値</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>경험치</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>獲得</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>획득</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>할 수 있다. <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>特</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>특</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>히, <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>貨物</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>화물</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>下車</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>하차</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>할 때 세 가지 <ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>駐車</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>주차</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 옵션이 주어지는데, 이 <ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>中</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>중</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 가장 어려운 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>位置</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>위치</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>選擇</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>선택</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하고 <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>完遂</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>완수</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>할 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>境遇</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>경우</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>追加</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>추가</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>經驗値</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>경험치</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 얻을 수 있다.
</p>
<ul><li>빠른 일거리&nbsp;: 트럭을 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>提供</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>제공</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>받아 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>貨物</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>화물</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>運送</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>운송</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한다.</li>
<li><ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>運送</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>운송</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>市場</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>시장</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>&nbsp;: <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>自身</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자신</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>所有</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>소유</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 트럭을 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하여 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>貨物</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>화물</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>運送</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>운송</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한다.</li>
<li><ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>外部</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>외부</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>契約</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>계약</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></li></ul>
<h3><span id="스킬_分配(분배)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".EC.8A.A4.ED.82.AC_.E5.88.86.E9.85.8D.28.EB.B6.84.EB.B0.B0.29">스킬 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>分配</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>분배</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%EC%9C%A0%EB%A1%9C_%ED%8A%B8%EB%9F%AD_%EC%8B%9C%EB%AE%AC%EB%A0%88%EC%9D%B4%ED%84%B0_2&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="부분 편집: 스킬 分配(분배)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>經驗値</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>경험치</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 쌓이면 레벨이 올라가며, 레벨이 오를 때마다 스킬 포인트를 얻고, 이를 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>消費</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>소비</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하여 플레이어의 <a href="/index.php?title=%EC%8A%A4%ED%82%AC&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="스킬 (없는 문서)">스킬</a>을 올릴 수 있다. 스킬을 올리면 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>長距離</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>장거리</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>, <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>高價貨物</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>고가화물</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>, <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>破損注意</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>파손주의</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>貨物</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>화물</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 따위 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>分野別</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>분야별</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 더 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>難易度</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>난이도</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 높은 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>貨物</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>화물</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 받을 수 있으며, 이를 <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>完遂</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>완수</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하면 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>手當</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>수당</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>과 <a href="/index.php?title=%E7%B6%93%E9%A9%97%E5%80%A4&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="經驗値 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade32 unknown"><rb>經驗値</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>경험치</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>를 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>追加</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>추가</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 얻을 수 있다.
</p>
