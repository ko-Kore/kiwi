<h2><span id="Rime_.E4.BD.BF.E7.94.A8.28.EC.82.AC.EC.9A.A9.29"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="Rime_使用(사용)">Rime <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E5%9C%8B%E6%BC%A2%E5%A4%A7%E7%99%BE%E7%A7%91:%EC%9D%B8%ED%81%90%EB%B2%A0%EC%9D%B4%ED%84%B0/%E5%9C%8B%E6%BC%A2%E5%A4%A7%E5%AD%B8%E6%A0%A1/%E5%80%89%E9%A0%A1%E8%BC%B8%E5%85%A5%E6%B3%95_%E5%85%A5%E9%96%80/%EB%A7%A5OS&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: Rime 使用(사용)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a rel="nofollow" class="external autonumber" href="https://rime.im/">[1]</a>
<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用者</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>直接</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>직접</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 키 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>配列</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>배열</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이나 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>辭典</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사전</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>製作</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>제작</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해 쓸 수 있는 <a href="/index.php?title=%EC%98%A4%ED%94%88%EC%86%8C%EC%8A%A4&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="오픈소스 (없는 문서)">오픈소스</a> <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>中國語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>중국어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>入力機</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>입력기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>. <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>基本</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기본</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 <ruby class="hanja grade0"><rb>倉頡</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>창힐</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 들어있는데다 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>入力可能</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>입력가능</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>字數</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자수</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 많고, 자잘한 <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>便宜機能</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>편의기능</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 있어서도 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>充實</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>충실</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하다.
</p><p>링크된 페이지에서 <b><ruby class="hanja grade0"><rb>鼠鬚管</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>서수관</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></b>를 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>選擇</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>선택</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하면 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>設置</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>설치</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 패키지를 받을 수 있다.
