<h2><span id="槪要(개요)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E6%BC%A2%E5%AD%97%E6%96%87%E5%8C%96%E5%9C%88&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要(개요)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>漢字文化圈</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한자문화권</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이란 <a href="/wiki/%E6%BC%A2%E5%AD%97" title="漢字"><ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>漢字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>를 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하거나 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>過去</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>과거</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하였던 <a href="/wiki/%E5%9C%8B%E5%AE%B6" title="國家"><ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>國家</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>국가</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>들로 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>構成</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>구성</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>된 <ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>文化圈</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>문화권</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다.
</p>
<h2><span id="國家別(국가별)_漢字近況(한자근황)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E5.9C.8B.E5.AE.B6.E5.88.A5.28.EA.B5.AD.EA.B0.80.EB.B3.84.29_.E6.BC.A2.E5.AD.97.E8.BF.91.E6.B3.81.28.ED.95.9C.EC.9E.90.EA.B7.BC.ED.99.A9.29"><ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>國家別</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>국가별</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>漢字近況</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한자근황</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E6%BC%A2%E5%AD%97%E6%96%87%E5%8C%96%E5%9C%88&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 國家別(국가별) 漢字近況(한자근황)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/wiki/%ED%8C%8C%EC%9D%BC:Map-Chinese_Characters.png" class="image"><img alt="Map-Chinese Characters.png" src="https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/6c/Map-Chinese_Characters.png/500px-Map-Chinese_Characters.png" decoding="async" width="500" height="322" srcset="https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/6c/Map-Chinese_Characters.png/750px-Map-Chinese_Characters.png 1.5x, https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/6c/Map-Chinese_Characters.png/1000px-Map-Chinese_Characters.png 2x"></a>
</p>
<ul><li><b><ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>濃綠色</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>농록색</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></b>: <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>正體使用國家</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>정체사용국가</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></li>
<li><b><ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>綠色</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>녹색</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></b>: <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>簡體</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>간체</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하나 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>正體</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>정체</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>도 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>混用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>혼용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하는 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>國家</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>국가</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></li>
<li><b><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>軟綠色</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>연록색</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></b>: <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>簡體使用國家</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>간체사용국가</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></li>
<li><b><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>軟豆色</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>연두색</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></b>: <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>漢字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하나 다른 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>文字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>문자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>混用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>혼용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></li>
<li><b><ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>黃色</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>황색</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></b>: <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>漢字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>했던 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>國家</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>국가</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></li></ul>
