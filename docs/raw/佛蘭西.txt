<p><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>佛蘭西</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>불란서</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>(France, 프랑스)는 <a href="/index.php?title=%E8%A5%BF%EC%9C%A0%EB%9F%BD&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="西유럽 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade80 unknown"><rb>西</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>서</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>유럽</a>의 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>國家</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>국가</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다.
</p>
<h2><span id="歷史(역사)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.AD.B7.E5.8F.B2.28.EC.97.AD.EC.82.AC.29"><ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>歷史</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>역사</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E4%BD%9B%E8%98%AD%E8%A5%BF&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 歷史(역사)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>佛蘭西</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>불란서</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에는 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>本</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>본</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>디 갈리아<ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>文化</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>문화</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>受容</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>수용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하여 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>急激</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>급격</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>文化的</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>문화적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja
