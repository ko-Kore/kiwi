<h2><span id="槪要(개요)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E5%AF%AB%E7%9C%9E%E6%A9%9F&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要(개요)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/index.php?title=%E5%AF%AB%E7%9C%9E&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="寫眞 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade42 unknown"><rb>寫眞</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사진</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>을찍는 <a href="/index.php?title=%E6%A9%9F%E6%A2%B0&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="機械 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade32 unknown"><rb>機械</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기계</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>. <ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>暗箱內</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>암상내</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에서 <a href="/index.php?title=%EB%A0%8C%EC%A6%88&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="렌즈 (없는 문서)">렌즈</a>가<ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>集光</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>집광</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하여<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>結像</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>결상</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>被寫體</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>피사체</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>像</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>상</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%AA%92%E9%AB%94&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="媒體 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade32 unknown"><rb>媒體</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>매체</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>에<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>記錄</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기록</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하는 <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%85%89%E5%AD%B8&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="光學 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade62 unknown"><rb>光學</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>광학</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>裝備</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>장비</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>.
</p>
<h2><span id="構造(구조)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.8B.E9.80.A0.28.EA.B5.AC.EC.A1.B0.29"><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>構造</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>구조</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E5%AF%AB%E7%9C%9E%E6%A9%9F&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 構造(구조)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>被寫體</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>피사체</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>反射光</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>반사광</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 <ruby class="hanja grade10"><rb>疊疊</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>첩첩</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의<a href="/index.php?title=%EB%A0%8C%EC%A6%88&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="렌즈 (없는 문서)">렌즈</a>를<ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>通過</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>통과</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하여 <a href="/wiki/%ED%95%84%EB%A6%84" title="필름">필름</a>이나<a href="/index.php?title=%E6%92%AE%E5%83%8F%E7%B4%A0%E5%AD%90&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="撮像素子 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade10 unknown"><rb>撮像素子</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>촬상소자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a><ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>等</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>등</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>媒體</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>매체</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에닿아 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>記錄</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기록</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되는데, 그 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>經路上</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>경로상</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 <a href="/index.php?title=%EC%A1%B0%EB%A6%AC%EA%B0%9C&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="조리개 (없는 문서)">조리개</a>나 <a href="/index.php?title=%EC%85%94%ED%84%B0&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="셔터 (없는 문서)">셔터</a>가 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>位置</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>위치</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하여 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>通過</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>통과</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>할수있는 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>光量</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>광량</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이나 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>通過時間</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>통과시간</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>調節</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>조절</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하여 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>露出</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>노출</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>過不足</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>과부족</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 없도록한다.
</p><p><ruby class="hanja grade10"><rb>撮影用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>촬영용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>렌즈와 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>分離或</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>분리혹</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>統合</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>통합</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>된<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>形態</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>형태</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 <ruby class="hanja grade10"><rb>撮影</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>촬영</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>될<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>映像</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>영상</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 미리보거나 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>同時</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>동시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 <ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>焦點</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>초점</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>도 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>調節可能</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>조절가능</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>裝置</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>장치</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>(뷰파인더)가 붙어있다.
</p>
