<h2><span id="特徵(특징)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E7.89.B9.E5.BE.B5.28.ED.8A.B9.EC.A7.95.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>特徵</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>특징</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E7%94%B3%EC%84%B8%EB%B2%8C%E5%BC%8F&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 特徵(특징)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>

class="hanja grade42"><rb>初聲</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>초성</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>ㅇ만 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>入力</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>입력</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>된 <ruby class="hanja <h3><span id="申(신)광조_原案(원안)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E7.94.B3.28.EC.8B.A0.29.EA.B4.91.EC.A1.B0_.E5.8E.9F.E6.A1.88.28.EC.9B.90.EC.95.88.29"><ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>申</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>신</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>광조 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>原案</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>원안</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E7%94%B3%EC%84%B8%EB%B2%8C%E5%BC%8F&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="부분 편집: 申(신)광조 原案(원안)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>最初</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>최초</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>申</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>신</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>세벌<ruby class="hanja unknown"><rb>年</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>년</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> <a href="/index.php?title=%ED%95%98%EC%9D%B4%ED%85%94&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="하이텔 (없는 문서)">하이텔</a> <ruby grade60"><rb>公開</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>공개</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되었다. <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%85%AC%E7%82%B3%E7%A6%B9_%EC%84%B8%EB%B2%8C%E5%BC%8F&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="公炳禹 </p><p>1995<ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>年</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>년</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>原案</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>원안</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에만 있는 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>特異</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>특이</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>點</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>점</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로, <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>複母音用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>복모음용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> ㅗ와 ㅜ가 두<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>個</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>씩 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>配置</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>배치</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되어 있다는 것이 있는데, 같은 손가락의 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>反復的</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>반복적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>인 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 줄이는 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>效果</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>효과</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 노린 것으로 보인다.
