<h2><span id="槪要(개요)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E8%8A%B3%E6%96%87%E7%A4%BE&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要(개요)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>株式會社芳文社</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>주식회사방문사</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는 <a href="/wiki/%E6%97%A5%E6%9C%AC" title="日本"><ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>日本</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일본</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>의<a href="/index.php?title=%E5%87%BA%E7%89%88%E7%A4%BE&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="出版社 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade32 unknown"><rb>出版社</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>출판사</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>이다.
</p>
<h2><span id="情報(정보)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.83.85.E5.A0.B1.28.EC.A0.95.EB.B3.B4.29"><ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>情報</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>정보</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E8%8A%B3%E6%96%87%E7%A4%BE&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 情報(정보)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>社屋</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사옥</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 <a href="/wiki/%E6%9D%B1%E4%BA%AC%E9%83%BD" title="東京都"><ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>東京都</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>동경도</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a><a href="/index.php?title=%E6%96%87%E4%BA%AC%E5%8D%80&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="文京區 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade60 unknown"><rb>文京區</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>문경구</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>에 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>所在</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>소재</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되있으며, <a href="/index.php?title=%E6%B0%B4%E9%81%93%E6%A9%8B%E9%A9%9B&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="水道橋驛 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade32 unknown"><rb>水道橋驛</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>수도교역</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>과 <a href="/index.php?title=%E6%9D%B1%E4%BA%AC_%EB%8F%94%EC%8B%9C%ED%8B%B0&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="東京 돔시티 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade60 unknown"><rb>東京</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>동경</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 돔시티</a>의 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>中間</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>중간</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에있다. <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>標語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>표어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는 “<ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>漫畵</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>만화</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>殿堂</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>전당</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>”<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>.
</p>
<h2><span id="歷史(역사)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.AD.B7.E5.8F.B2.28.EC.97.AD.EC.82.AC.29"><ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>歷史</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>역사</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E8%8A%B3%E6%96%87%E7%A4%BE&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="부분 편집: 歷史(역사)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>戰後</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>전후</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>1<ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>年</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>년</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이지난 <a href="/index.php?title=1946%E5%B9%B4&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="1946年 (없는 문서)">1946<ruby class="hanja grade80 unknown"><rb>年</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>년</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>, <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>創業者</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>창업자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>인 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>孝壽芳春</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>효수방춘</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>依</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>의</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>設立</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>설립</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>된 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>尙文館</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>상문관</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이<ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>前身</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>전신</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이며, 이를<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>繼承</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>계승</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하는 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>芳文社</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>방문사</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>設立日</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>설립일</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 4<ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>年後</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>년후</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>인 <a href="/index.php?title=1950%E5%B9%B4&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="1950年 (없는 문서)">1950<ruby class="hanja grade80 unknown"><rb>年</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>년</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a><a href="/index.php?title=7%E6%9C%8810%E6%97%A5&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="7月10日 (없는 문서)">7<ruby class="hanja grade80 unknown"><rb>月</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>월</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>10<ruby class="hanja grade80 unknown"><rb>日</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>이다. 이러한<ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>歷史</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>역사</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>相應</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>상응</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하게, <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>創立後</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>창립후</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>現在</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>현재</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에이르는 70<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>餘年間</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>여년간</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>, <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>刊行物</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>간행물</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>內容</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>내용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에도 많은<ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>變化</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>변화</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가있었으나, <a href="/index.php?title=2002%E5%B9%B4&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="2002年 (없는 문서)">2002<ruby class="hanja grade80 unknown"><rb>年</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>년</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>의『<ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>漫畵</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>만화</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>타임키라라』<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>誌</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>지</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>創刊以後</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>창간이후</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>, <a href="/wiki/%E8%90%8C" title="萌"><ruby class="hanja grade10"><rb>萌</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>맹</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a><a unknown"><rb>漫畵</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>만화</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>가 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>主力</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>주력</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이되었다.
</p>
