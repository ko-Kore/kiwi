<h3><span id="日本(일본)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.97.A5.E6.9C.AC.28.EC.9D.BC.EB.B3.B8.29"><ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>日本</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일본</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E7%89%B9%E5%88%A5%E5%8D%80&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="부분 편집: 日本(일본)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade10"><rb>市町村</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>시정촌</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>과 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>同級</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>동급</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의&nbsp;<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>基礎自治團體</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기초자치단체</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다. <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>特別區</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>특별구</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>組織</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>조직</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 “<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>地方自治法</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>지방자치법</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>” <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>下</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>하</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 세워졌고 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>東京都</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>동경도</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에서만 <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>唯一</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>유일</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하게 나타난다.
</p>
