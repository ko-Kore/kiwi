<h2><span id="槪要(개요)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E6%A9%9F%E6%A2%B0%E5%BC%8F_%ED%82%A4%EB%B3%B4%EB%93%9C&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要(개요)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>機械式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기계식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 키보드란 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>各</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>각</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 키마다 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>機械式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기계식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 스위치를 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 <a href="/wiki/%ED%82%A4%EB%B3%B4%EB%93%9C" title="키보드">키보드</a>이다.
</p>
<h2><span id="特徵(특징)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E7.89.B9.E5.BE.B5.28.ED.8A.B9.EC.A7.95.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>特徵</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>특징</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E6%A9%9F%E6%A2%B0%E5%BC%8F_%ED%82%A4%EB%B3%B4%EB%93%9C&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 特徵(특징)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>키 하나하나마다 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>機械式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기계식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>構造</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>구조</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>電流</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>전류</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 흐름을 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>開放</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개방</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>/<ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>遮斷</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>차단</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하는 <a href="/index.php?title=%EC%8A%A4%EC%9C%84%EC%B9%98&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="스위치 (없는 문서)">스위치</a>가 <ruby class="hanja grade10"><rb>搭載</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>탑재</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되어 있으며, <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>各各</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>각각</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 스위치 위에 <a href="/index.php?title=%ED%82%A4%EC%BA%A1&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="키캡 (없는 문서)">키캡</a>이 끼워져 있다. 이 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>機械式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기계식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 스위치의 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>構造</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>구조</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 따라 <ruby class="hanja grade12"><rb>打鍵感</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>타건감</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 차이가 생긴다.
</p><p>이 스위치를 만드는 회사로 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>代表的</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대표적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>인 <a href="/index.php?title=%EC%B2%B4%EB%A6%AC&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="체리 (없는 문서)">체리</a><ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>社</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>自社</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자사</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 스위치에 <a href="/index.php?title=%E7%89%B9%E8%A8%B1&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="特許 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade50 unknown"><rb>特許</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>특허</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>를 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>保有</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>보유</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하고 있었던 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>故</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>고</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 높은 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>價格</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>가격</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 자랑했지만, <a href="/index.php?title=2010%E5%B9%B4%E4%BB%A3&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="2010年代 (없는 문서)">2010<ruby class="hanja grade62 unknown"><rb>年代</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>년대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>에 들어 이 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>特許</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>특허</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>滿了</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>만료</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되어 <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>低廉</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>저렴</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>他社</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>타사</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 스위치를 <ruby class="hanja grade10"><rb>搭載</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>탑재</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>製品</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>제품</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>들이 등장하면서 <a href="/index.php?title=%EA%B2%8C%EC%9D%B4%EB%B0%8D&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="게이밍 (없는 문서)">게이밍</a> <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>裝備</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>장비</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>大勢</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대세</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 되었다.
</p>
<h2><span id="스위치_種類(종류)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".EC.8A.A4.EC.9C.84.EC.B9.98_.E7.A8.AE.E9.A1.9E.28.EC.A2.85.EB.A5.98.29">스위치 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>種類</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>종류</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E6%A9%9F%E6%A2%B0%E5%BC%8F_%ED%82%A4%EB%B3%B4%EB%93%9C&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="부분 편집: 스위치 種類(종류)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/index.php?title=%EC%B2%B4%EB%A6%AC&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="체리 (없는 문서)">체리</a><ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>社</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>機械式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기계식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 스위치의 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>種類</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>종류</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는 누르는 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>感覺</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>감각</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 따라 크게 클릭과 넌클릭, 리니어로 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>區分</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>구분</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되며, <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>他社</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>타사</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>類似製品</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>유사제품</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>들도 이 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>區分</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>구분</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 따른다.
</p>
<h3><span id="클릭"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".ED.81.B4.EB.A6.AD">클릭</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E6%A9%9F%E6%A2%B0%E5%BC%8F_%ED%82%A4%EB%B3%B4%EB%93%9C&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="부분 편집: 클릭">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Click. 키를 누를 때 흔히 클릭<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>感</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>감</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이라 하는 '딸깍' 소리와 걸리는 느낌이 난다. 체리<ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>社</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>題品</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>제품</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>中</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>중</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>靑軸</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>청축</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>該當</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>해당</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>된다.
</p>
<h3><span id="넌클릭"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".EB.84.8C.ED.81.B4.EB.A6.AD">넌클릭</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E6%A9%9F%E6%A2%B0%E5%BC%8F_%ED%82%A4%EB%B3%B4%EB%93%9C&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="부분 편집: 넌클릭">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Non-click. 클릭과 비슷하지만 '딸깍' 소리가 나지 않고 키를 누를 때 걸리는 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>區分感</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>구분감</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>만 난다. 체리<ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>社</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade10"><rb>褐軸</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>갈축</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>該當</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>해당</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>된다.
</p>
<h3><span id="리니어"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".EB.A6.AC.EB.8B.88.EC.96.B4">리니어</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E6%A9%9F%E6%A2%B0%E5%BC%8F_%ED%82%A4%EB%B3%B4%EB%93%9C&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="부분 편집: 리니어">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Linear. 키를 눌러도 소리나 걸리는 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>感覺</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>감각</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>全</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>전</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>혀 없다. 체리<ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>社</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>黑軸</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>흑축</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>과 <ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>赤軸</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>적축</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 이에 <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>該當</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>해당</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>된다.
</p>
