<h2><span id="槪要(개요)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E8%B6%8A%E5%8D%97%E7%A4%BE%E6%9C%83%E4%B8%BB%E7%BE%A9%E5%85%B1%E5%92%8C%E5%9C%8B&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要(개요)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>越南</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>월남</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>或</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>혹</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 베트남은 <ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>東南</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>동남</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>亞細亞</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>아세아</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>位置</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>위치</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>共産主義</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>공산주의</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>國家</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>국가</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다. <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>越南</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>월남</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이란 <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%8D%97%E8%B6%8A&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="南越 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade32 unknown"><rb>南越</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>남월</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>의 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>別稱</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>별칭</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다. <a href="/index.php?title=%E8%B6%8A%E5%8D%97%E4%BA%BA&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="越南人 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade32 unknown"><rb>越南人</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>월남인</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>들과 <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%B0%91%E6%95%B8%E6%B0%91%E6%97%8F&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="少數民族 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade60 unknown"><rb>少數民族</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>소수민족</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>이 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>居住</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>거주</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하며 <a href="/wiki/%E8%B6%8A%E5%8D%97%E8%AA%9E" title="越南語"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>越南語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>월남어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>를 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>公用語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>공용어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한다.
</p>
<h2><span id="歷史(역사)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.AD.B7.E5.8F.B2.28.EC.97.AD.EC.82.AC.29"><ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>歷史</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>역사</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E8%B6%8A%E5%8D%97%E7%A4%BE%E6%9C%83%E4%B8%BB%E7%BE%A9%E5%85%B1%E5%92%8C%E5%9C%8B&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 歷史(역사)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade0"><rb>貉龍君</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>맥룡군</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>과 <ruby class="hanja grade0"><rb>嫗姬</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>구희</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>神話</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>신화</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 의하면 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>紀元前</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기원전</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 2500<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>年頃</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>년경</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 거슬러 올라가나 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>神話</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>신화</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>不過</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>불과</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하다.
