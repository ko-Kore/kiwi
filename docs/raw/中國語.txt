<h2><span id="槪要(개요)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E4%B8%AD%E5%9C%8B%E8%AA%9E&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要(개요)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/index.php?title=%E4%B8%AD%E8%8F%AF%E5%9C%88&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="中華圈 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade20 unknown"><rb>中華圈</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>중화권</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>에서 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>主</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>주</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하는 <a href="/wiki/%E8%A8%80%E8%AA%9E" title="言語"><ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>言語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>언어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>.

<h2><span id="呼稱(호칭)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E5.91.BC.E7.A8.B1.28.ED.98.B8.EC.B9.AD.29"><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>呼稱</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>호칭</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E4%B8%AD%E5%9C%8B%E8%AA%9E&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 呼稱(호칭)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>中國</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>중국</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>·<ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>臺灣</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대만</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에서는 쓰여진 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>中國語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>중국어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 “<b><ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>漢語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></b>”, <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>對話</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대화</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>할 때의 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>中國語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>중국어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 “<b><ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>中文</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>중문</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></b>”라고 부르고 있다. <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>例</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>예</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 들어, “<ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>漢語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 말한다”, “<ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>中文</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>중문</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 쓴다”등입니다. 그러나, <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>口語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>구어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에서는 “<ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>漢語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 말한다”, “<ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>中文</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>중문</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 말한다”, “<ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>中文</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>중문</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 쓴다”, “<ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>漢語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 쓴다”라고 하는 것처럼 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>區別</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>구별</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>없이 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되기도 한다.
</p>

<h2><span id="特徵(특징)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E7.89.B9.E5.BE.B5.28.ED.8A.B9.EC.A7.95.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>特徵</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>특징</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E4%B8%AD%E5%9C%8B%E8%AA%9E&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="부분 편집: 特徵(특징)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>쓰는 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>地域</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>지역</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 매우 넓다보니 <a href="/index.php?title=%E6%96%B9%E8%A8%80&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="方言 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade60 unknown"><rb>方言</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>방언</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>도 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>多樣</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>다양</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하고 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>方言別</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>방언별</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>發音</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>발음</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이나 <a href="/index.php?title=%E8%AA%9E%E5%BD%99&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="語彙 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade10 unknown"><rb>語彙</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>어휘</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>, <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>文法</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>문법</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>差異</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>차이</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>도 매우 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>甚</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>심</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하다. 아예 다른 <a href="/wiki/%E8%A8%80%E8%AA%9E" title="言語"><ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>言語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>언어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>로 보는 게 더 맞을 정도.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/%E6%A8%99%E6%BA%96%E4%B8%AD%E5%9C%8B%E8%AA%9E" title="標準中國語"><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>標準中國語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>표준중국어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>의 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>基盤</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기반</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이되는 <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%AE%98%E8%A9%B1&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="官話 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade42 unknown"><rb>官話</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>관화</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>가 가장 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>有名</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>유명</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하며, <a href="/index.php?title=%E9%A6%99%E6%B8%AF&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="香港 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade42 unknown"><rb>香港</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>향항</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> 및 <a href="/index.php?title=%E6%BE%B3%E9%96%80&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="澳門 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade0 unknown"><rb>澳門</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>오문</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>, <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%BB%A3%E6%9D%B1%E7%9C%81&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="廣東省 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade52 unknown"><rb>廣東省</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>광동성</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>에서 쓰이는 <a href="/index.php?title=%E7%B2%A4%E8%AA%9E&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="粤語 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade0 unknown"><rb>粤語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>월어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>(<ruby class="hanja grade52 unknown"><rb>廣東語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>광동어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>)</a>도 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>有名</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>유명</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하다. <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>以外</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>이외</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에도 <a href="/index.php?title=%E9%96%A9%E8%AA%9E&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="閩語 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade0 unknown"><rb>閩語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>민어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>, <a href="/wiki/%E5%90%B3%E8%AA%9E" title="吳語"><ruby class="hanja grade12"><rb>吳語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>오어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>, <a href="/index.php?title=%E6%B9%98%E8%AA%9E&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="湘語 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade0 unknown"><rb>湘語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>상어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>, <a href="/index.php?title=%E8%B4%9B%E8%AA%9E&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="贛語 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade0 unknown"><rb>贛語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>공어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>, <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%AE%A2%E5%AE%B6%E8%AA%9E&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="客家語 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade52 unknown"><rb>客家語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>객가어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>가 있다.
</p><p><a href="/index.php?title=%E5%B9%B3%E8%AA%9E&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="平語 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade70 unknown"><rb>平語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>평어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>, <a href="/index.php?title=%E6%99%89%E8%AA%9E&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="晉語 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade20 unknown"><rb>晉語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>진어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>, <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%BE%BD%E8%AA%9E&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="徽語 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade12 unknown"><rb>徽語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>휘어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>는 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>各各</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>각각</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade0"><rb>粤語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>월어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>, <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>官話</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>관화</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>, <ruby class="hanja grade12"><rb>吳語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>오어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>方言</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>방언</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 보는 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>傾向</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>경향</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 있었지만, 요즈음 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>學者</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>학자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>들은 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>普通</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>보통</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>個別言語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개별언어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>(<a href="/index.php?title=%E6%96%B9%E8%A8%80&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="方言 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade60 unknown"><rb>方言</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>방언</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>)로 본다.
</p>
