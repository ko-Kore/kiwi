<h2><span id="槪要(개요)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E7%B0%A1%E5%8C%96%E5%AD%97&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要(개요)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/wiki/%E4%B8%AD%E8%8F%AF%E4%BA%BA%E6%B0%91%E5%85%B1%E5%92%8C%E5%9C%8B" title="中華人民共和國"><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>中華人民共和國</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>중화인민공화국</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>에서 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>施行</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>시행</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>漢字簡化方案</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한자간화방안</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>略稱</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>약칭</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>, <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>或</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>혹</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 그에<ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>基因</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기인</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%AD%97%E5%BD%A2&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="字形 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade62 unknown"><rb>字形</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자형</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>을 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>意味</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>의미</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한다. <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>相對語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>상대어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는 <a href="/wiki/%E7%B9%81%E9%AB%94%E5%AD%97" title="繁體字"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>繁體字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>번체자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>, <a href="/wiki/%E6%AD%A3%E9%AB%94%E5%AD%97" title="正體字"><ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>正體字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>정체자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>等</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>등</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>.
</p>
<h2><span id="特徵(특징)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E7.89.B9.E5.BE.B5.28.ED.8A.B9.EC.A7.95.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>特徵</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>특징</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E7%B0%A1%E5%8C%96%E5%AD%97&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 特徵(특징)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>複雜</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>복잡</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 <a href="/index.php?title=%E9%82%8A&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="邊 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade42 unknown"><rb>邊</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>변</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>과 <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%82%8D&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="傍 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade30 unknown"><rb>傍</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>방</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>을 2~4<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>劃</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>획</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>程度</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>정도</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 <ruby class="hanja grade10"><rb>一括</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일괄</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>置換</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>치환</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하거나 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>民間</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>민간</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에서 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>主</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>주</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되던 <a href="/index.php?title=%E7%95%A5%E5%AD%97&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="略字 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade40 unknown"><rb>略字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>약자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>를 그대로 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>採用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>채용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하기도 하고 어떤 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>文字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>문자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는 <a href="/index.php?title=%E8%8D%89%E6%9B%B8&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="草書 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade62 unknown"><rb>草書</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>초서</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>에서 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>借用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>차용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하기도 하는 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>等</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>등</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>方法</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>방법</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>製作</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>제작</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되었다. <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>劃數</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>획수</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 조금이라도 줄이기 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>爲</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>위</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해 <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>旣存</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기존</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>形態</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>형태</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>完全</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>완전</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>히 바꿔놓기도 해서, 처음 볼때는 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>異質感</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>이질감</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 느껴질 수도 있다. <del><ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>本格漢字魔改造計劃</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>본격한자마개조계획</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></del>
</p>
