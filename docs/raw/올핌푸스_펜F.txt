<h2><span id=".E6.8A.80.E8.A1.93.E7.9A.84.E4.BB.95.E6.A8.A3.28.EA.B8.B0.EC.88.A0.EC.A0.81.EC.82.AC.EC.96.91.29"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="技術的仕樣(기술적사양)"><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>技術的仕樣</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기술적사양</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%EC%98%AC%ED%95%8C%ED%91%B8%EC%8A%A4_%ED%8E%9CF&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 技術的仕樣(기술적사양)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>世界最初</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>세계최초</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 하프프레임 SLR이다. <a href="/index.php?title=%E8%B7%9D%E9%9B%A2%E8%A8%88%EC%B9%B4%EB%A9%94%EB%9D%BC&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="距離計카메라 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade32 unknown"><rb>距離計</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>거리계</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>카메라</a>로 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>錯覺</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>착각</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>될만한 아담한 사이즈로 렌즈<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>交換式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>교환식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> SLR로, <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>一般的</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일반적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>인 SLR에서 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되는 위로 접히는 <a href="/index.php?title=%EA%B1%B0%EC%9A%B8&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="거울 (없는 문서)">거울</a>과 <a href="/index.php?title=%ED%8E%9C%ED%83%80%ED%94%84%EB%A6%AC%EC%A6%98&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="펜타프리즘 (없는 문서)">펜타프리즘</a> <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>代身</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대신</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 옆으로 접히는 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>一名</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일명</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> ‘사이드 스윙 미러’와 3<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>個</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 ‘포로프리즘’으로 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>像</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>상</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 뒤집는다. <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>上下</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>상하</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>或</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>혹</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>左右</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>좌우</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 움직이는 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>一般的</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일반적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>인 <a href="/index.php?title=%ED%8F%AC%EC%BB%AC%ED%94%8C%EB%A0%88%EC%9D%B8_%EC%85%94%ED%84%B0&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="포컬플레인 셔터 (없는 문서)">포컬플레인 셔터</a>가 아닌 얇은 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>半圓形</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>반원형</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>膜</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>막</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>回轉</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>회전</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해서 <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>驅動</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>구동</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되는 ‘로터리(<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>回轉式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>회전식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>) 포컬플레인 셔터’를 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하여, <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>支援</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>지원</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하는 셔터<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>速度</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>속도</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>全區間</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>전구간</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에서 <a href="/index.php?title=%ED%94%8C%EB%9E%98%EC%8B%9C&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="플래시 (없는 문서)">플래시</a> <ruby </p><p><ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>最初</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>최초</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 나온 펜F는 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>電氣</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>전기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>·<ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>電子部品</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>전자부품</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>全</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>전</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>혀 들어가지 않은 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>完全</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>완전</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>機械式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기계식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>構造</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>구조</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이며, <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>後</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>후</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에나온 펜FT는 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>內裝</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>내장</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby><a href="/index.php?title=%E9%9C%B2%E5%87%BA%E8%A8%88&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="露出計 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade32 unknown"><rb>露出計</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>노출계</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>만 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>追加</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>추가</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>된 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>同一</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>동일</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>機械式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기계식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>構造</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>구조</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>인데, <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>露出計</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>노출계</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>除外</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>제외</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 모든 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>機能</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기능</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 <a href="/index.php?title=%E9%9B%BB%E6%B1%A0&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="電池 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade32 unknown"><rb>電池</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>전지</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> 없이도 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>動作</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>동작</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한다.
</p>
<h2><span id=".ED.8E.9CF"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="펜F">펜F</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%EC%98%AC%ED%95%8C%ED%91%B8%EC%8A%A4_%ED%8E%9CF&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 펜F">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>一名</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일명</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 오리지널 펜F. 가장 큰 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>外觀上</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>외관상</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>特徵</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>특징</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로는 렌즈의 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>右側</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>우측</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>위에 <a href="/index.php?title=%EC%85%80%ED%94%84_%ED%83%80%EC%9D%B4%EB%A8%B8&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="셀프 타이머 (없는 문서)">셀프 타이머</a>가 없는<ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>代身</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대신</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 큼지막한 <a href="/index.php?title=%EB%B8%94%EB%9E%99%EB%A0%88%ED%84%B0&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="블랙레터 (없는 문서)">블랙레터</a>로 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>金色</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>금색</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> ‘F’로고가 새겨져 있다는 것. 이 ‘F’로고는 이모델과 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>後代</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>후대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>모델들의 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>純正</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>순정</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 렌즈캡에도 그려져 있다.
</p><p>이 모델에만 <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>該當</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>해당</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되는 또다른 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>特徵</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>특징</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로는 와인딩 레버(필름 감는 레버)가 짧게 되어있어 두<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>番</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>번</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>씩 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>作動</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>작동</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>시켜야 다음 프레임이 <ruby class="hanja grade10"><rb>裝塡</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>장전</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>된다는 것이다. <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>以後</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>이후</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>派生</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>파생</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>모델들에서는 길쭉한 원–스트로크로 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>變更</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>변경</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되어 나왔는데, 그에 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>比</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>비</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하면 투–스트로크 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>方式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>방식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>必要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>필요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>瞬間</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>순간</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 재빨리 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>作動</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>작동</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>시킬수 있어 좋다고 한다.
</p><p><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>前述</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>전술</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한대로 <a href="/index.php?title=%E9%9C%B2%E5%87%BA%E8%A8%88&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="露出計 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade32 unknown"><rb>露出計</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>노출계</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>가 <ruby class="hanja grade10"><rb>搭載</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>탑재</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되지 않았으며, <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>必要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>필요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>境遇</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>경우</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 셔터<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>速度</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>속도</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 다이얼에 끼워 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>할 수 있는 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>專用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>전용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>外裝</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>외장</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>露出計</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>노출계</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>販賣</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>판매</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되었다. 이걸 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>爲</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>위</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해 펜F와 펜FV의 셔터<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>速度</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>속도</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 다이얼에는 작은 홈이 하나 파여 있다. <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>露出計</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>노출계</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>內裝</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>내장</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되어 나온 펜FT에는 <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>該當</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>해당</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되지 않는 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>部分</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>부분</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>. 다이얼의 홈조차 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>削除</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>삭제</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되었다.
</p>
