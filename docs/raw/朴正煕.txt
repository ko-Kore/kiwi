<h2><span id="槪要(개요)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E6%9C%B4%E6%AD%A3%E7%85%95&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要(개요)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><b><ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>朴正煕</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>박정희</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></b>(<a href="/index.php?title=1917%E5%B9%B4&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="1917年 (없는 문서)">1917<ruby class="hanja grade80 unknown"><rb>年</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>년</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a><a href="/index.php?title=11%E6%9C%8814%E6%97%A5&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="11月14日 (없는 문서)">11<ruby class="hanja grade80 unknown"><rb>月</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>월</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>14<ruby class="hanja grade80 unknown"><rb>日</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>-<a href="/index.php?title=1979%E5%B9%B4&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="1979年 (없는 문서)">1979<ruby class="hanja grade80 unknown"><rb>年</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>년</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a><a href="/index.php?title=10%E6%9C%8826%E6%97%A5&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="10月26日 (없는 문서)">10<ruby class="hanja grade80 unknown"><rb>月</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>월</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>26<ruby class="hanja grade80 unknown"><rb>日</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>)는 <a href="/wiki/%E5%A4%A7%E9%9F%93%E6%B0%91%E5%9C%8B" title="大韓民國"><ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>大韓民國</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대한민국</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>의 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>第</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>제</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>5-9<ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>代</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby><a href="/index.php?title=%E5%A4%A7%E7%B5%B1%E9%A0%98&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="大統領 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade42 unknown"><rb>大統領</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대통령</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>이다.
</p><p>그의 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>時代</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>시대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>부터 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>約</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>약</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 30<ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>年間</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>년간</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 걸쳐 <ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>大韓民國</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대한민국</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 “<a href="/index.php?title=%E6%BC%A2%E6%B1%9F%EC%9D%98_%E5%A5%87%E8%B7%A1&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="漢江의 奇跡 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade72 unknown"><rb>漢江</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한강</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade32 unknown"><rb>奇跡</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>”으로 불리는 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>高度</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>고도</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>經濟成長</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>경제성장</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 이루며 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>世界最貧國</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>세계최빈국</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>階層</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>계층</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에서 <ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>大韓民國</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대한민국</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 끌어낸 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>功勞</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>공로</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>評價</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>평가</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>받았다. 1979<ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>年</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>년</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>側近</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>측근</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <a href="/index.php?title=%E9%87%91%E8%BC%89%E5%9C%AD&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="金載圭 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade12 unknown"><rb>金載圭</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>김재규</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>에서 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>暗殺</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>암살</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되었다. 
</p><p><ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>日政時代</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일정시대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 그가 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>日本式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일본식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 이름은 <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%89%B5%E6%B0%8F%E6%94%B9%E5%90%8D&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="創氏改名 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade40 unknown"><rb>創氏改名</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>창씨개명</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>運動</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>운동</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 때 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>命名</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>명명</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>된 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>高木正雄</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>고목정웅</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>(다까기 마사오)였다.
</p>
