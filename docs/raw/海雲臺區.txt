<h2><span id="槪要(개요)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E6%B5%B7%E9%9B%B2%E8%87%BA%E5%8D%80&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要(개요)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>海雲臺區</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>해운대구</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는 <a href="/wiki/%E9%87%9C%E5%B1%B1%E5%BB%A3%E5%9F%9F%E5%B8%82" title="釜山廣域市"><ruby class="hanja grade12"><rb>釜山廣域市</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>부산광역시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>의 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>區</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>구</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다.
</p>
<h2><span id="名稱(명칭)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E5.90.8D.E7.A8.B1.28.EB.AA.85.EC.B9.AD.29"><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>名稱</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>명칭</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E6%B5%B7%E9%9B%B2%E8%87%BA%E5%8D%80&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 名稱(명칭)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/index.php?title=%E5%B4%94%E8%87%B4%E9%81%A0&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="崔致遠 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade12 unknown"><rb>崔致遠</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>최치원</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>이 <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%86%AC%E6%9F%8F%EC%84%AC&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="冬柏섬 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade20 unknown"><rb>冬柏</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>동백</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>섬</a> <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>一帶</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>景致</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>경치</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 보고 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>自身</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자신</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <a href="/index.php?title=%E8%99%9F&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="號 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade60 unknown"><rb>號</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>호</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>인 ‘<ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>海雲</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>해운</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>’을 따서 <a href="/wiki/%E6%B5%B7%E9%9B%B2%E8%87%BA" title="海雲臺"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>海雲臺</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>해운대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>라는 이름을 붙였다는 것에서 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>由來</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>유래</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>했다. <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>實際</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>실제</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 <ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>冬柏</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>동백</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>섬에는 ‘<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>海雲臺</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>해운대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>’라 새겨진 바위가 있는데, 이게 <ruby class="hanja grade12"><rb>崔致遠</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>최치원</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 쓴 것이라 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>傳</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>전</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해진다.
</p>
<h2><span id="觀光(관광)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E8.A7.80.E5.85.89.28.EA.B4.80.EA.B4.91.29"><ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>觀光</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>관광</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E6%B5%B7%E9%9B%B2%E8%87%BA%E5%8D%80&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="부분 편집: 觀光(관광)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/index.php?title=%E6%B5%B7%E9%9B%B2%E8%87%BA%E6%B5%B7%E6%B0%B4%E6%B5%B4%E5%A0%B4&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="海雲臺海水浴場 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade32 unknown"><rb>海雲臺海水浴場</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>해운대해수욕장</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>과 그 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>一帶</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%86%AC%E6%9F%8F%EC%84%AC&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="冬柏섬 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade20 unknown"><rb>冬柏</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>동백</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>섬</a>, <a href="/index.php?title=APEC%EB%88%84%EB%A6%AC%EB%A7%88%EB%A3%A8&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="APEC누리마루 (없는 문서)">APEC누리마루</a> <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>等</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>등</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 여름에 찾는 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>觀光地</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>관광지</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>有名</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>유명</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하다. <a href="/index.php?title=%EC%84%BC%ED%85%80%EC%8B%9C%ED%8B%B0&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="센텀시티 (없는 문서)">센텀시티</a>의 <a href="/index.php?title=%E6%96%B0%E4%B8%96%E7%95%8C%EC%84%BC%ED%85%80%EC%8B%9C%ED%8B%B0&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="新世界센텀시티 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade62 unknown"><rb>新世界</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>신세계</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>센텀시티</a>와 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>新世界免稅店</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>신세계면세점</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>도 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>觀光客</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>관광객</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>들이 많이 찾는다.
</p>
