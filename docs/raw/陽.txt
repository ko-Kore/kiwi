<p>뜻을 나타내는 <a href="/index.php?title=%E9%98%9C&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="阜 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade12 unknown"><rb>阜</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>부</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>(<a href="/index.php?title=%E2%BB%96&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="⻖ (없는 문서)">⻖</a>)와 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>音</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>음</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 나타내는 <a href="/index.php?title=%E6%98%9C&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="昜 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade0 unknown"><rb>昜</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>양</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>이 합쳐진 <a href="/wiki/%E5%BD%A2%E8%81%B2%E6%96%87%E5%AD%97" title="形聲文字"><ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>形聲文字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>형성문자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>이다. “양달”, “햇빛”을 뜻하는 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>漢字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다. <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>元來</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>원래</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> “<ruby class="hanja grade0"><rb>昜</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>양</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>”은 “<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>陽</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>양</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>”을 뜻하는 글<ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>였으나 “<ruby class="hanja grade12"><rb>阜</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>부</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>”가 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>追加</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>추가</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되어 쓰이게 되었다.
</p><p><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>語源</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>어원</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 <a href="/index.php?title=%E4%B8%AD%E5%9C%8B%ED%8B%B0%EB%B2%A0%ED%8A%B8%E7%A5%96%E8%AA%9E&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="中國티베트祖語 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade80 unknown"><rb>中國</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>중국</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>티베트<ruby class="hanja grade70 unknown"><rb>祖語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>조어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> “<i>*laŋ</i>”(“빛, 빛나다, 밝다”)로 <a href="/index.php?title=%EB%B2%84%EB%A7%88%E8%AA%9E&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="버마語 (없는 문서)">버마<ruby class="hanja grade70 unknown"><rb>語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> “လင်း”, <a href="/index.php?title=%E6%B3%B0%E5%9C%8B%E8%AA%9E&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="泰國語 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade32 unknown"><rb>泰國語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>태국어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> “ปลั่ง”과 같은 뿌리이다.
</p>
