<h2><span id="槪要(개요)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E6%89%93%E5%AD%97%E6%A9%9F&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要(개요)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>打字機</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>타자기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>(Typewriter)는 <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%AD%97%E6%9D%BF&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="字板 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade50 unknown"><rb>字板</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자판</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>을 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>打字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>타자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하는 대로 글이 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>印刷</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>인쇄</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되는, <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%8E%9F%E7%A8%BF&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="原稿 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade32 unknown"><rb>原稿</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>원고</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>等</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>등</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>作成</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>작성</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하기 위한 <a href="/index.php?title=%E6%A9%9F%E6%A2%B0&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="機械 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade32 unknown"><rb>機械</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기계</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>이다.
</p>
<h2><span id="種類(종류)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E7.A8.AE.E9.A1.9E.28.EC.A2.85.EB.A5.98.29"><ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>種類</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>종류</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E6%89%93%E5%AD%97%E6%A9%9F&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 種類(종류)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span id="作動方式(작동방식)에_따른_分類(분류)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E4.BD.9C.E5.8B.95.E6.96.B9.E5.BC.8F.28.EC.9E.91.EB.8F.99.EB.B0.A9.EC.8B.9D.29.EC.97.90_.EB.94.B0.EB.A5.B8_.E5.88.86.E9.A1.9E.28.EB.B6.84.EB.A5.98.29"><ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>作動方式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>작동방식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 따른 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>分類</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>분류</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E6%89%93%E5%AD%97%E6%A9%9F&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="부분 편집: 作動方式(작동방식)에 따른 分類(분류)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span id="機械式(기계식)_打字機(타자기)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A9.9F.E6.A2.B0.E5.BC.8F.28.EA.B8.B0.EA.B3.84.EC.8B.9D.29_.E6.89.93.E5.AD.97.E6.A9.9F.28.ED.83.80.EC.9E.90.EA.B8.B0.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>機械式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기계식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>打字機</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>타자기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E6%89%93%E5%AD%97%E6%A9%9F&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="부분 편집: 機械式(기계식) 打字機(타자기)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>各</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>각</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 키에 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>活字臺</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>활자대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>直結</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>직결</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되어 키를 누르는 힘이 그대로 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>活字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>활자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 움직여서 종이 위 <a href="/index.php?title=%EB%A8%B9%E7%B4%99&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="먹紙 (없는 문서)">먹<ruby class="hanja grade70 unknown"><rb>紙</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>지</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>를 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>加擊</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>가격</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하여 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>印刷</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>인쇄</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되는 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>方式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>방식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다. <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>一般的</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일반적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>打字機</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>타자기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%9B%BA%E5%AE%9A%E8%A7%80%E5%BF%B5&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="固定觀念 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade50 unknown"><rb>固定觀念</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>고정관념</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>에 <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>該當</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>해당</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되는 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>物件</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>물건</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>.
</p><p>손가락의 힘이 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>直接</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>직접</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>印刷</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>인쇄</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에까지 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>作用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>작용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하기 때문에 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>打字時</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>타자시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 힘 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>操切</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>조절</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>必要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>필요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하다. <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>普通</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>보통</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 생각하는 <a href="/wiki/%ED%82%A4%EB%B3%B4%EB%93%9C" title="키보드">키보드</a>를 ‘누르는’ 것이 아니라 ‘쳐야’ 하며<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>, 힘이 너무 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>弱</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>약</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하면 글<ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 찍히지 않고 너무 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>强</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>강</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하면 글<ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 번지거나 종이가 찍혀서 잘 찢어질 수 있다.
</p>
<h4><span id="電動(전동)_打字機(타자기)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E9.9B.BB.E5.8B.95.28.EC.A0.84.EB.8F.99.29_.E6.89.93.E5.AD.97.E6.A9.9F.28.ED.83.80.EC.9E.90.EA.B8.B0.29"><ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>電動</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>전동</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>打字機</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>타자기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E6%89%93%E5%AD%97%E6%A9%9F&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="부분 편집: 電動(전동) 打字機(타자기)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p><a href="/wiki/%EC%BB%B4%ED%93%A8%ED%84%B0" title="컴퓨터">컴퓨터</a>의 것과 <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>類似</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>유사</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>形態</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>형태</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <a href="/wiki/%ED%82%A4%EB%B3%B4%EB%93%9C" title="키보드">키보드</a>로 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>入力</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>입력</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 받으면 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>電氣</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>전기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>모터가 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>動作</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>동작</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>活字臺</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>활자대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>或</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>혹</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>活字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>활자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>바퀴나 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>活字球</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>활자구</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 움직여 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>印字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>인자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하는 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>方式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>방식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>打字機</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>타자기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다.
</p><p><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>機械式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기계식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>打字機</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>타자기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>比</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>비</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>打字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>타자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>할 때 드는 힘이 덜하고 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>活字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>활자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>바퀴나 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>活字球</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>활자구</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>採用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>채용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>製品</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>제품</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>印字速度</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>인자속도</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>도 빠르고 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>活字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>활자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>도 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>交換</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>교환</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>할수있다. 그러나 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>電氣</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>전기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 없는 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>環境</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>환경</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에서는 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>當然</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>당연</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>히 못 쓴다.
</p>
