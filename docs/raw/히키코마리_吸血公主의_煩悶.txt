<p>『<b>히키코마리 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>吸血公主</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>흡혈공주</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade10"><rb>煩悶</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>번민</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></b>』은 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>小林湖底著作</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>소림호저저작</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 라이트노벨이다。<ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>原作揷畵</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>원작삽화</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는 리이츄가<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>擔當</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>담당</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>。<ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>第</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>제</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>11<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>回</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>회</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>GA<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>文庫大賞優秀賞受賞作</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>문고대상우수상수상작</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다。<a href="/index.php?title=2020%E5%B9%B41%E6%9C%88&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="2020年1月 (없는 문서)">2020<ruby class="hanja grade80 unknown"><rb>年</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>년</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>1<ruby class="hanja grade80 unknown"><rb>月</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>월</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>로부터 GA<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>文庫</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>문고</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에서<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>刊行</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>간행</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되고있다。
</p><p><ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>漫畵版</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>만화판</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 <ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>原作揷畵家</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>원작삽화가</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>인 리이츄가<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>擔當</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>담당</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하고있으며、2021<ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>年</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>년</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>12<ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>月</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>월</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>부터<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>連載中</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>연재중</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>。애니메이션<ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>化</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>화</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 <a href="/index.php?title=2023%E5%B9%B41%E6%9C%88&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="2023年1月 (없는 문서)">2023<ruby class="hanja grade80 unknown"><rb>年</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>년</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>1<ruby class="hanja grade80 unknown"><rb>月</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>월</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>에<ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>發表</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>발표</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되어、<ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>同年</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>동년</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>10<ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>月</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>월</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로부터<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>放送中</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>방송중</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>。
</p>
<h2><span id=".E6.A2.97.E6.A6.82.28.EA.B2.BD.EA.B0.9C.29"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="梗概(경개)"><ruby class="hanja grade0"><rb>梗概</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>경개</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%ED%9E%88%ED%82%A4%EC%BD%94%EB%A7%88%EB%A6%AC_%E5%90%B8%E8%A1%80%E5%85%AC%E4%B8%BB%EC%9D%98_%E7%85%A9%E6%82%B6&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 梗概(경개)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<blockquote>
<p>「……어？　뭐、뭐지？？」<br><br>
<a href="/index.php?title=%ED%9E%88%ED%82%A4%EC%BD%94%EB%AA%A8%EB%A6%AC&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="히키코모리 (없는 문서)">틀어박혀서사는</a><ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>少女</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>소녀</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>「코마리」<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>卽</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>즉</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 테라코마리가 눈을뜨자、웬걸 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>帝國</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>제국</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>將軍</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>장군</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 <ruby class="hanja grade10"><rb>大拔擢</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대발탁</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되어있었다！더군다나 코마리가 거느리는것은、<ruby class="hanja grade10"><rb>下剋上</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>하극상</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>橫行</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>횡행</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하는 피비리고 우락부락한<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>部隊</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>부대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>。<br><br>
<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>名門吸血鬼</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>명문흡혈귀</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>家系</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>가계</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 태어났으면서도、피가 싫기때문에「<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>運動神經</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>운동신경</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>꽝」「키가 작음」「<ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>魔法</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>마법</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을쓸수없음」으로 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>三拍子</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>삼박자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가갖추어진 코마리。우두망찰한 그<ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>女</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>여</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에게、<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>腹心</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>복심</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>（이되줄것이틀림없는）메이드 빌이 말하였다。<br><br>
「맡기어주십시오。<ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>期必</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기필</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>코<ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>部下</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>부하</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>들을 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>錯覺</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>착각</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하게만들어 보이겠습니다。」<br><br>
흥감과<ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>幸運</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>행운</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 기대어 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>快進擊</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>쾌진격</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하는 코마리의모습을 그린 코미컬판타지！<br>
틀여박혀있지만、코마리는「하려면할수있는 아이」⁉<br><br>
