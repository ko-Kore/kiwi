<h2><span id="槪要(개요)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%EC%95%84%EC%9D%B4%ED%8F%B0_11_%ED%94%84%EB%A1%9C&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要(개요)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<blockquote><p><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>電擊</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>전격</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 프로 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>宣言</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>선언</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>.</p></blockquote>
<p>아이폰 11 프로는 <a href="/index.php?title=%EC%95%A0%ED%94%8C&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="애플 (없는 문서)">애플</a>의 <a href="/wiki/%EC%8A%A4%EB%A7%88%ED%8A%B8%ED%8F%B0" title="스마트폰">스마트폰</a>이다. <a href="/wiki/2019%E5%B9%B4" title="2019年">2019<ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>年</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>년</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a><a href="/index.php?title=9%E6%9C%8810%E6%97%A5&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="9月10日 (없는 문서)">9<ruby class="hanja grade80 unknown"><rb>月</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>월</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>10<ruby class="hanja grade80 unknown"><rb>日</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>公開</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>공개</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되었으며, <a href="/wiki/%EC%95%84%EC%9D%B4%ED%8F%B0" title="아이폰">아이폰</a>의 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>第</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>제</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>13<ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>世代</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>세대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>製品</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>제품</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다.
</p>
<h2><span id="特徵(특징)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E7.89.B9.E5.BE.B5.28.ED.8A.B9.EC.A7.95.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>特徵</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>특징</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%EC%95%84%EC%9D%B4%ED%8F%B0_11_%ED%94%84%EB%A1%9C&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 特徵(특징)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/index.php?title=%EC%95%84%EC%9D%B4%ED%8F%B0_XS&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="아이폰 XS (없는 문서)">아이폰 XS</a>의 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>後繼機</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>후계기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다.
</p><p><ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>前面</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>전면</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 <a href="/wiki/%EC%95%84%EC%9D%B4%ED%8F%B0_X" title="아이폰 X">아이폰 X</a>에서 처음으로 선보인 '노치 디자인'을 그대로 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>維持</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>유지</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하고 있으며, <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>後面</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>후면</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>有光</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>유광</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 유리 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>素材</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>소재</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하였고, 카메라 3개를 <ruby class="hanja grade10"><rb>搭載</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>탑재</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>했기 때문에 새로운 카메라 디자인을 선보였다. 이로 인해 많은 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>論難</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>논란</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 일기도 했다.
</p><p><a href="/index.php?title=%E8%89%B2%E7%9B%B8&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="色相 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade52 unknown"><rb>色相</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>색상</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>은 '스페이스 그레이', '실버', '골드', '미드나이트 그린' 네 가지로 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>出市</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>출시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되었다.
</p><p><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>大略的</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대략적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>인 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>仕樣</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사양</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 A13 바이오닉 <a href="/index.php?title=AP&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="AP (없는 문서)">AP</a>, 4GB 램, 64/256/512GB <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>貯藏空間</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>저장공간</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>, 5.8인치의 <a href="/index.php?title=%EB%A0%88%ED%8B%B0%EB%82%98_%EB%94%94%EC%8A%A4%ED%94%8C%EB%A0%88%EC%9D%B4&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="레티나 디스플레이 (없는 문서)">슈퍼 레티나 XDR 디스플레이</a>, <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>前面</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>전면</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 1,200만 <a href="/index.php?title=%E7%95%B5%E7%B4%A0&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="畵素 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade42 unknown"><rb>畵素</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>화소</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>, <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>後面</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>후면</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 1,200만 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>畵素</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>화소</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <a href="/wiki/%EC%B9%B4%EB%A9%94%EB%9D%BC" class="mw-redirect" title="카메라">카메라</a>(<ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>廣角</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>광각</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>, <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>望遠</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>망원</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>, <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>超廣角</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>초광각</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 모두 <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>該當</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>해당</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>됨), 3,046mAh <a href="/index.php?title=%EB%B0%B0%ED%84%B0%EB%A6%AC&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="배터리 (없는 문서)">배터리</a>가 <ruby class="hanja grade10"><rb>搭載</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>탑재</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되어 있다.
</p>
