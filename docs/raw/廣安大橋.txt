<h2><span id="槪要(개요)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E5%BB%A3%E5%AE%89%E5%A4%A7%E6%A9%8B&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要(개요)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>廣安大橋</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>광안대교</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는 <ruby class="hanja grade12"><rb>釜山廣域市</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>부산광역시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <a href="/wiki/%E6%B5%B7%E9%9B%B2%E8%87%BA%E5%8D%80" title="海雲臺區"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>海雲臺區</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>해운대구</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> <ruby class="hanja grade12"><rb>佑洞</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>우동</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>과 <a href="/index.php?title=%E6%B0%B4%E7%87%9F%E5%8D%80&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="水營區 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade40 unknown"><rb>水營區</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>수영구</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%BB%A3%E5%AE%89%E6%B4%9E&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="廣安洞 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade52 unknown"><rb>廣安洞</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>광안동</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>을 잇는 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>橋梁</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>교량</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다.
</p>
<h2><span id="特徵(특징)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E7.89.B9.E5.BE.B5.28.ED.8A.B9.EC.A7.95.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>特徵</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>특징</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E5%BB%A3%E5%AE%89%E5%A4%A7%E6%A9%8B&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 特徵(특징)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>全區間</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>전구간</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 <a href="/index.php?title=%E8%87%AA%E5%8B%95%E8%BB%8A%E5%B0%88%E7%94%A8%E9%81%93%E8%B7%AF&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="自動車專用道路 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade40 unknown"><rb>自動車專用道路</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자동차전용도로</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>로 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>自轉車</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자전거</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>나 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>二輪車</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>이륜차</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>, <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>步行者</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>보행자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>等</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>등</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>通行</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>통행</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>禁止</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>금지</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>된다. <a href="/index.php?title=2028%E5%B9%B4&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="2028年 (없는 문서)">2028<ruby class="hanja grade80 unknown"><rb>年</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>년</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>까지 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>料金</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>요금</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>徵收</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>징수</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하는 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>有料道路</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>유료도로</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이며 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>料金所</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>요금소</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>海雲臺</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>해운대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>쪽에 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>設置</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>설치</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되어 있다.
