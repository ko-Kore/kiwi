<h2><span id="槪要(개요)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E6%96%AC&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要(개요)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/wiki/%E6%9C%83%E6%84%8F%E6%96%87%E5%AD%97" title="會意文字"><ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>會意文字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>회의문자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>이다. “<ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>車</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>차</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>”+“<ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>斤</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>근</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>”에서 나무를 도끼(<ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>斤</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>근</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>)로 자르고 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>車</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>차</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>材料</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>재료</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 얻는 것입니다. “베다”, “끊어지다” <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>等</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>등</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 뜻한다.
</p>
