<h2><span id="槪要(개요)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E5%85%89%E5%B7%9E%E5%BB%A3%E5%9F%9F%E5%B8%82&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要(개요)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>光州廣域市</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>광주광역시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는 <a href="/wiki/%E5%A4%A7%E9%9F%93%E6%B0%91%E5%9C%8B" title="大韓民國"><ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>大韓民國</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대한민국</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>의 <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%BB%A3%E5%9F%9F%E5%B8%82&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="廣域市 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade40 unknown"><rb>廣域市</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>광역시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>로, <a href="/index.php?title=%E6%B9%96%E5%8D%97&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="湖南 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade50 unknown"><rb>湖南</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>호남</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>最大</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>최대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <a href="/index.php?title=%E9%83%BD%E5%B8%82&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="都市 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade50 unknown"><rb>都市</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>도시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>이다.
</p>
<h2><span id="歷史(역사)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.AD.B7.E5.8F.B2.28.EC.97.AD.EC.82.AC.29"><ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>歷史</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>역사</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E5%85%89%E5%B7%9E%E5%BB%A3%E5%9F%9F%E5%B8%82&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 歷史(역사)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>本來</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>본래</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%85%A8%E7%BE%85%E5%8D%97%E9%81%93_%E5%85%89%E5%B7%9E%E5%B8%82&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="全羅南道 光州市 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade42 unknown"><rb>全羅南道</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>전라남도</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade52 unknown"><rb>光州市</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>광주시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>였으나 <a href="/index.php?title=1988%E5%B9%B4&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="1988年 (없는 문서)">1988<ruby class="hanja grade80 unknown"><rb>年</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>년</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%85%89%E5%B7%9E%E7%9B%B4%E8%BD%84%E5%B8%82&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="光州直轄市 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade10 unknown"><rb>光州直轄市</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>광주직할시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>로 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>分離</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>분리</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되었고, <a href="/index.php?title=1995%E5%B9%B4&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="1995年 (없는 문서)">1995<ruby class="hanja grade80 unknown"><rb>年</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>년</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>現在</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>현재</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 이름으로 바뀌었다.
</p>
