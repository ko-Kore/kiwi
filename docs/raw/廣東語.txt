<h2><span id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="槪要(개요)"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E5%BB%A3%E6%9D%B1%E8%AA%9E&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要(개요)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><b><ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>廣東語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>광동어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></b>란 <a href="/wiki/%E4%B8%AD%E5%9C%8B%E8%AA%9E" title="中國語"><ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>中國語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>중국어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>의 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>方言中</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>방언중</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 하나로, <a href="/index.php?title=%E7%B2%A4%E8%AA%9E&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="粤語 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade0 unknown"><rb>粤語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>월어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>의 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>一種</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일종</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다.
</p>
<h2><span id=".E4.BD.BF.E7.94.A8.E5.9C.B0.E5.9F.9F.28.EC.82.AC.EC.9A.A9.EC.A7.80.EC.97.AD.29"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="使用地域(사용지역)"><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>使用地域</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용지역</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E5%BB%A3%E6%9D%B1%E8%AA%9E&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 使用地域(사용지역)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/index.php?title=%E5%BB%A3%E6%9D%B1%E7%9C%81&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="廣東省 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade52 unknown"><rb>廣東省</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>광동성</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>과 <a href="/index.php?title=%E9%A6%99%E6%B8%AF&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="香港 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade42 unknown"><rb>香港</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>향항</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>, <a href="/index.php?title=%E6%BE%B3%E9%96%80&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="澳門 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade0 unknown"><rb>澳門</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>오문</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>에서 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>主</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>주</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한다. 그<ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>中</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>중</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>特</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>특</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>히 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>香港</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>향항</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>과 <ruby class="hanja grade0"><rb>澳門</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>오문</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에서는 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>事實上</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사실상</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>公用語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>공용어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 쓰인다.
</p>

</p>
