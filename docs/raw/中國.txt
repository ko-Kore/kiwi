<h2><span id="槪要(개요)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>&nbsp;</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E4%B8%AD%E5%9C%8B&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要(개요)&nbsp;">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>

<h3><span id="基源(기원)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E5.9F.BA.E6.BA.90.28.EA.B8.B0.EC.9B.90.29"><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>基源</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기원</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E4%B8%AD%E5%9C%8B&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="부분 편집: 基源(기원)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>

<h3><span id="現在(현재)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E7.8F.BE.E5.9C.A8.28.ED.98.84.EC.9E.AC.29"><ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>現在</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>현재</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E4%B8%AD%E5%9C%8B&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="부분 편집: 現在(현재)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
