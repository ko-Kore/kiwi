<h2><span id="日本式(일본식)_漢字(한자)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.97.A5.E6.9C.AC.E5.BC.8F.28.EC.9D.BC.EB.B3.B8.EC.8B.9D.29_.E6.BC.A2.E5.AD.97.28.ED.95.9C.EC.9E.90.29"><ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>日本式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일본식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>漢字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E6%97%A5%E6%9C%AC%E8%AA%9E%EC%9D%98_%E6%BC%A2%E5%AD%97&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 日本式(일본식) 漢字(한자)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>一部</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일부</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>漢字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>日本</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일본</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에서 만들어져 쓰인다. 이런 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>漢字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 <ruby class="hanja grade0"><rb>国字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>국자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>(고쿠지), <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>或</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>혹</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%92%8C%E8%A3%BD%E6%BC%A2%E5%AD%97&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="和製漢字 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade42 unknown"><rb>和製漢字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>화제한자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>(와세이칸지)라고 부른다. <sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>

<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>影響</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>영향</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 받아 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>近代</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>근대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 만들어진 <span lang="ja"><ruby class="hanja <h3><span id="訓讀(훈독)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E8.A8.93.E8.AE.80.28.ED.9B.88.EB.8F.85.29"><ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>訓讀</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>훈독</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E6%97%A5%E6%9C%AC%E8%AA%9E%EC%9D%98_%E6%BC%A2%E5%AD%97&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="부분 편집: 訓讀(훈독)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>訓讀</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>훈독</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>(<span lang="ja"><ruby class="hanja grade0"><rb>訓読</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>훈독</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>み</span>, 군요미)는 뜻을 읽는 것을 말한다.　<span lang="ja"><ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>流</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>류</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>れ<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>星</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>성</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span>(<span lang="ja">ながれぼし</span>)
</p>
<h3><span id="混用(혼용)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.B7.B7.E7.94.A8.28.ED.98.BC.EC.9A.A9.29"><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>混用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>혼용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E6%97%A5%E6%9C%AC%E8%AA%9E%EC%9D%98_%E6%BC%A2%E5%AD%97&amp;action=edit&amp;section=8" title="부분 편집: 混用(혼용)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>音讀</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>음독</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>과 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>訓讀</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>훈독</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 섞여 있는 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>境遇</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>경우</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>도 있다. <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>音讀</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>음독</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>+<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>訓讀</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>훈독</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>인 것을 주바코 요미(<span lang="ja"><ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>重</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>중</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>(ジュウ)<ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>箱</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>상</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>(ばこ)<ruby class="hanja grade0"><rb>読</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>독</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>み</span>),  <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>訓讀</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>훈독</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>+<ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>音讀</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>음독</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>인 것을 유토 요미(<span lang="ja"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>湯</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>탕</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>(ゆ)<ruby class="hanja grade10"><rb>桶</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>통</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>(トウ)<ruby class="hanja grade0"><rb>読</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>독</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>み</span>)라고 한다.
</p><p><ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>音讀</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>음독</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>과 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>訓讀</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>훈독</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 따라 뜻이 달라지는 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>境遇</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>경우</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>도 있다. <ruby class="hanja grade0"><rb>仮名</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>가명</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이라고 썼을 때, <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>假名</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>가명</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이라는 뜻이 되기도 하고(<span lang="ja">カメイ</span>), <a href="/wiki/%ED%9E%88%EB%9D%BC%EA%B0%80%EB%82%98" class="mw-redirect" title="히라가나">히라가나</a>와 <a href="/wiki/%EA%B0%80%ED%83%80%EC%B9%B4%EB%82%98" class="mw-redirect" title="가타카나">가타카나</a>를 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>總稱</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>총칭</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하는 가나(<span lang="ja">カナ</span>)가 되기도 한다.
</p>
<h2><span id="其他(기타)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E5.85.B6.E4.BB.96.28.EA.B8.B0.ED.83.80.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>其他</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기타</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E6%97%A5%E6%9C%AC%E8%AA%9E%EC%9D%98_%E6%BC%A2%E5%AD%97&amp;action=edit&amp;section=9" title="부분 편집: 其他(기타)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>같은 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>漢字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>反復</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>반복</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해서 쓸 때, <span lang="ja">々</span>를 그 자리에 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>代身</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대신</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 써 준다. <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>例</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>예</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 들어 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>日本語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일본어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에서 ‘<span lang="ja">ひとびと</span>’(사람들)에 <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>該當</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>해당</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하는 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>漢字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>表記</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>표기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는 ‘<span lang="ja"><ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>人人</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>인인</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span>’인데, <span lang="ja"><ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>人</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>인</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>々</span>라고 적는다.
히라가나일 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>境遇</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>경우</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <span lang="ja">ゝ</span> 또는 <span lang="ja">ゞ</span>를 쓰고 가타카나일 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>境遇</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>경우</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <span lang="ja">ヽ</span> 또는 <span lang="ja">ヾ</span>를 쓴다. 이 글<ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>入力</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>입력</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하기 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>爲</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>위</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해서는 로마<ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>入力</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>입력</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 onaji를 치고 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>變換</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>변환</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하면 된다.
</p>
