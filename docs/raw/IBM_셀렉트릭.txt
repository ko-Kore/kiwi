<h2><span id=".E7.89.B9.E5.BE.B5.28.ED.8A.B9.EC.A7.95.29"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="特徵(특징)"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>特徵</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>특징</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=IBM_%EC%85%80%EB%A0%89%ED%8A%B8%EB%A6%AD&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 特徵(특징)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vNUEUth7qjc"><ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>當時</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>당시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>廣告</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>광고</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>映像</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>영상</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>
</p><p><ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>當時</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>당시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>電動打字機</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>전동타자기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>들<ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>中</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>중</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에서 아직 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>一般的</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일반적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이었던 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>活字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>활자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>막대 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>代身</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대신</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>球形</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>구형</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 된 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>活字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>활자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한다.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>金屬</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>금속</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로된 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>球體</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>구체</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에는 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>文字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>문자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>들이 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>陽刻</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>양각</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되어 있으며, <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用者</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 <a href="/wiki/%ED%82%A4%EB%B3%B4%EB%93%9C" title="키보드">키보드</a>를 치면 그 키에 <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>該當</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>해당</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하는 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>文字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>문자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>正面</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>정면</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 오도록 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>活字球</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>활자구</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>回轉</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>회전</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>시키고 기울여서 리본 위로 종이를 두드려 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>印字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>인자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한다.
</p>
<h3><span id=".E9.95.B7.E9.BB.9E.28.EC.9E.A5.EC.A0.90.29"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="長點(장점)"><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>長點</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>장점</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=IBM_%EC%85%80%EB%A0%89%ED%8A%B8%EB%A6%AD&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 長點(장점)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>活字臺</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>활자대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>物件</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>물건</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>과 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>比較</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>비교</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하여 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>活字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>활자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 꼬이는 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>現象</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>현상</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>全</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>전</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>혀 없으며, <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>打字速度</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>타자속도</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 더 빠르게 낼 수 있다.
</p><p>다른 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>書體</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>서체</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 쓰고 싶으면 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>活字球</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>활자구</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 빼고 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>願</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>원</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하는 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>書體</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>서체</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 된 것을 다시 끼우면 되는데, <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>打字機</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>타자기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>自體</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자체</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 바꿔야 했던 <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>旣存</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기존</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>方式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>방식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>과는 것과 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>比較</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>비교</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>도 되지 않을 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>程度</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>정도</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>便利</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>편리</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하다.
</p>
