<p><a href="/index.php?title=%EC%95%A0%ED%94%8C&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="애플 (없는 문서)">애플</a>이 <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>販賣</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>판매</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하는 <a href="/wiki/%EC%8A%A4%EB%A7%88%ED%8A%B8%ED%8F%B0" title="스마트폰">스마트폰</a> <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>製品</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>제품</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다.
</p><p><a href="/index.php?title=2007%E5%B9%B4&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="2007年 (없는 문서)">2007<ruby class="hanja grade80 unknown"><rb>年</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>년</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>初代</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>초대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 아이폰이 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>出市</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>출시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>된 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>後</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>후</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 <a href="/index.php?title=%E6%90%BA%E5%B8%B6%E9%9B%BB%E8%A9%B1&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="携帶電話 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade30 unknown"><rb>携帶電話</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>휴대전화</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>市場</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>시장</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>完全</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>완전</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>히 뒤바꿔 놓았다. <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>以後</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>이후</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>, <a href="/index.php?title=%EB%85%B8%ED%82%A4%EC%95%84&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="노키아 (없는 문서)">노키아</a>와 <a href="/index.php?title=%EB%B8%94%EB%9E%99%EB%B2%A0%EB%A6%AC&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="블랙베리 (없는 문서)">블랙베리</a>를 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>沒落</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>몰락</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 길로 접어들게 하였으며, 몇<ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>年</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>연</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>後</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>후</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 스마트폰 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>事業</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사업</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 뛰어든 <a href="/wiki/%E4%B8%89%E6%98%9F_%EA%B0%A4%EB%9F%AD%EC%8B%9C" title="三星 갤럭시"><ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>三星</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>삼성</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 갤럭시</a>와 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>競爭</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>경쟁</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 벌이게 된다.
</p>
