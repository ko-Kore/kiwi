<h2><span id=".EC.9D.B4.EC.96.B4.EC.B9.98.EA.B8.B0.EC.99.80_.EB.AA.A8.EC.95.84.EC.B9.98.EA.B8.B0"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="이어치기와_모아치기">이어치기와 모아치기</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E9%9F%93%E5%9C%8B%E8%AA%9E_%E5%85%A5%E5%8A%9B%E6%96%B9%E5%BC%8F/%EC%84%B8%EB%B2%8C%E5%BC%8F&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 이어치기와 모아치기">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>세벌<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 가장 크게 이어치기<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>系列</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>계열</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>과 모아치기<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>系列</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>계열</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>分類</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>분류</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>된다.
</p><p><b>이어치기</b>는 로마<ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>나 두벌<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>字板</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자판</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 칠때와 같이 키를 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>順序</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>순서</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>대로 하나씩 눌러 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>單語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>단어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>入力</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>입력</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하는 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>方式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>방식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다.
</p><p><b>모아치기</b>는 <a href="/index.php?title=%ED%94%BC%EC%95%84%EB%85%B8&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="피아노 (없는 문서)">피아노</a>로 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>和音</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>화음</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 치듯이 여러 키를 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>同時</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>동시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 눌러 글<ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>, <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>單語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>단어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>, <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>文章</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>문장</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>入力</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>입력</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하는 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>方式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>방식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다. <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>專用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>전용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하드웨어가 있는 <a href="/index.php?title=%E9%80%9F%E8%A8%98&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="速記 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade60 unknown"><rb>速記</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>속기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a><ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>字板</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자판</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>除外</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>제외</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하면 <a href="/index.php?title=%E7%84%A1%E9%99%90%E5%90%8C%E6%99%82%E5%85%A5%E5%8A%9B&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="無限同時入力 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade42 unknown"><rb>無限同時入力</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>무한동시입력</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>을 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>支援</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>지원</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하는 <a href="/wiki/%ED%82%A4%EB%B3%B4%EB%93%9C" title="키보드">키보드</a>가 있어야 제대로 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>할수있다. <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>一般的</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일반적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>인 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>事務用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사무용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 키보드로는 <a href="/index.php?title=%EA%B3%A0%EC%8A%A4%ED%8C%85%E7%8F%BE%E8%B1%A1&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="고스팅現象 (없는 문서)">고스팅<ruby class="hanja grade40 unknown"><rb>現象</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>현상</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a><ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>等</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>등</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>因</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>인</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>不完全</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>불완전</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하게 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해야 하거나 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>自體</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자체</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>不可能</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>불가능</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하다.
