<h2><span id="槪要(개요)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%EB%B2%A1%EC%8A%A4%EC%BD%94&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要(개요)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>벡스코(Busan Exhibition &amp; COnvention Center: BEXCO)는 <a href="/wiki/%E9%87%9C%E5%B1%B1%E5%BB%A3%E5%9F%9F%E5%B8%82" title="釜山廣域市"><ruby class="hanja grade12"><rb>釜山廣域市</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>부산광역시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> <a href="/wiki/%E6%B5%B7%E9%9B%B2%E8%87%BA%E5%8D%80" title="海雲臺區"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>海雲臺區</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>해운대구</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> <a href="/index.php?title=%E4%BD%91%E6%B4%9E&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="佑洞 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade12 unknown"><rb>佑洞</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>우동</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>에 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>位置</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>위치</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>展示</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>전시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>·컨벤션 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>行事場</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>행사장</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다.
</p>
<h2><span id="歷史(역사)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.AD.B7.E5.8F.B2.28.EC.97.AD.EC.82.AC.29"><ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>歷史</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>역사</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%EB%B2%A1%EC%8A%A4%EC%BD%94&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 歷史(역사)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>이 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>位置</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>위치</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에는 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>元來</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>원래</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <a href="/index.php?title=%E6%B0%B4%E7%87%9F%E9%A3%9B%E8%A1%8C%E5%A0%B4&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="水營飛行場 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade40 unknown"><rb>水營飛行場</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>수영비행장</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>이 있었지만, 1987<ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>年</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>년</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>閉鎖</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>폐쇄</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되었고, <a href="/index.php?title=%EC%84%BC%ED%85%80%EC%8B%9C%ED%8B%B0&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="센텀시티 (없는 문서)">센텀시티</a>가 세워지며 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>建設</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>건설</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되었다.
</p><p><a href="/index.php?title=2012%E5%B9%B4&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="2012年 (없는 문서)">2012<ruby class="hanja grade80 unknown"><rb>年</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>년</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a><a href="/index.php?title=6%E6%9C%881%E6%97%A5&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="6月1日 (없는 문서)">6<ruby class="hanja grade80 unknown"><rb>月</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>월</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>1<ruby class="hanja grade80 unknown"><rb>日</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>第</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>제</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>2<ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>展示場</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>전시장</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>과 오디토리움이 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>完工</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>완공</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되어 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>運營中</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>운영중</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다. <a rel="nofollow" class="external autonumber" href="https://www.hankyung.com/economy/article/2012060106371">[1]</a>
</p>
