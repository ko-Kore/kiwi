<p><b><ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>千葉縣</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>천엽현</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></b>은<a href="/wiki/%E6%97%A5%E6%9C%AC" title="日本"><ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>日本</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일본</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a><a href="/index.php?title=%E9%97%9C%E6%9D%B1%E5%9C%B0%E6%96%B9&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="關東地方 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade52 unknown"><rb>關東地方</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>관동지방</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>의<ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>縣</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>현</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다。<a href="/wiki/%E6%9D%B1%E4%BA%AC" title="東京"><ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>東京</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>동경</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>、<a href="/index.php?title=%E5%9F%BC%E7%8E%89&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="埼玉 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade0 unknown"><rb>埼玉</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기옥</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>、<a href="/index.php?title=%E8%8C%A8%E5%9F%8E&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="茨城 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade0 unknown"><rb>茨城</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자성</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>과<ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>陸地</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>육지</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로、<a href="/index.php?title=%E7%A5%9E%E5%A5%88%E5%B7%9D&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="神奈川 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade30 unknown"><rb>神奈川</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>신내천</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>과<ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>海上</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>해상</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로<ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>隣接</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>인접</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한다。<ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>縣廳所在地</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>현청소재지</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는<a href="/index.php?title=%E5%8D%83%E8%91%89%E5%B8%82&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="千葉市 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade50 unknown"><rb>千葉市</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>천엽시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>이다。
