<h2><span id="意味(의미)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.84.8F.E5.91.B3.28.EC.9D.98.EB.AF.B8.29"><ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>意味</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>의미</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E7%99%BE&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 意味(의미)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>百</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>백</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 <a href="/index.php?title=%E6%95%B8%E5%AD%97&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="數字 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade70 unknown"><rb>數字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>숫자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>100을<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>意味</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>의미</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하는 <a href="/wiki/%E6%BC%A2%E5%AD%97" title="漢字"><ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>漢字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>이다. 그 <ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>外</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>외</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에도 여러, 모든, 모두<ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>等</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>등</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>意味</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>의미</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>亦是</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>역시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>있다.
</p>
<h2><span id="字源(자원)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E5.AD.97.E6.BA.90.28.EC.9E.90.EC.9B.90.29"><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>字源</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자원</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E7%99%BE&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 字源(자원)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>一</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>과 <ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>白</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>백</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>合</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>합</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>쳐진 <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%BD%A2%E8%81%B2%E5%AD%97&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="形聲字 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade42 unknown"><rb>形聲字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>형성자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>이다.
</p>
<h2><span id="音訓(음훈)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E9.9F.B3.E8.A8.93.28.EC.9D.8C.ED.9B.88.29"><ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>音訓</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>음훈</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E7%99%BE&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="부분 편집: 音訓(음훈)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>音</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>음</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 백, <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>訓</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>훈</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 일백이다.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span id="部首(부수)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E9.83.A8.E9.A6.96.28.EB.B6.80.EC.88.98.29"><ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>部首</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>부수</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E7%99%BE&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="부분 편집: 部首(부수)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>百</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>백</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의<ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>部首</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>부수</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는 <ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>白</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>백</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다.
</p>

<h2><span id="異體字(이체자)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E7.95.B0.E9.AB.94.E5.AD.97.28.EC.9D.B4.EC.B2.B4.EC.9E.90.29"><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>異體字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>이체자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E7%99%BE&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="부분 편집: 異體字(이체자)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
