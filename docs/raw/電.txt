<h2><span id="槪要(개요)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E9%9B%BB&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要(개요)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>'번개', '전기', '빠르다' 등을 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>意味</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>의미</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하는 <a href="/wiki/%E6%9C%83%E6%84%8F%E6%96%87%E5%AD%97" title="會意文字"><ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>會意文字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>회의문자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>.
</p>
<h2><span id="歷史(역사)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.AD.B7.E5.8F.B2.28.EC.97.AD.EC.82.AC.29"><ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>歷史</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>역사</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E9%9B%BB&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 歷史(역사)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>元來</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>원래</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는 <a href="/index.php?title=%E7%94%B3&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="申 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade42 unknown"><rb>申</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>신</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>이 '번개'를 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>意味</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>의미</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>했지만 <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>只今</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>지금</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 쓰는 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>意味</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>의미</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>假借</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>가차</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되면서 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>申</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>신</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>雨</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>우</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 덧붙여 만들어진 글<ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다.
</p>
