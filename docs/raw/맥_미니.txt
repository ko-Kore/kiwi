<h2><span id="槪要(개요)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%EB%A7%A5_%EB%AF%B8%EB%8B%88&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要(개요)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><b>맥 미니</b>는 <a href="/index.php?title=%EC%95%A0%ED%94%8C&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="애플 (없는 문서)">애플</a>의 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>入門用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>입문용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 데스크탑 맥 컴퓨터이다.
</p>
<h2><span id="特徵(특징)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E7.89.B9.E5.BE.B5.28.ED.8A.B9.EC.A7.95.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>特徵</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>특징</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%EB%A7%A5_%EB%AF%B8%EB%8B%88&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 特徵(특징)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>패키지 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>構成品</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>구성품</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>簡單</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>간단</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하게 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>本體</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>본체</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>와 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>電源</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>전원</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>코드가 끝. <a href="/index.php?title=%EB%AA%A8%EB%8B%88%ED%84%B0&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="모니터 (없는 문서)">모니터</a>와 <a href="/wiki/%ED%82%A4%EB%B3%B4%EB%93%9C" title="키보드">키보드</a> 및 <a href="/index.php?title=%EB%A7%88%EC%9A%B0%EC%8A%A4&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="마우스 (없는 문서)">마우스</a>는 따로 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>求</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>구</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해야 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>할수 있다. 하지만 그런만큼 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>價格</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>가격</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>도 착해서 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>韓貨基準</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한화기준</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 89<ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>萬</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>만</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>원에 <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>販賣</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>판매</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>된다.
</p>
