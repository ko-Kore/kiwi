<h2><span id="槪要(개요)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E9%9F%B3%E6%A8%82%E7%AE%B1%E5%AD%90&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要(개요)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>音樂箱子</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>음악상자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>機械裝置</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기계장치</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>依</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>의</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>自動</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자동</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 <a href="/index.php?title=%E9%9F%B3%E6%A8%82&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="音樂 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade62 unknown"><rb>音樂</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>음악</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>을 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>演奏</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>연주</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하는 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>樂器</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>악기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>中</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>중</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 하나이다.
</p><p><ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>音樂箱子</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>음악상자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는 크게 나뉘어 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>金屬</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>금속</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>조각이 달린 <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%9C%93%E7%AD%92&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="圓筒 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade10 unknown"><rb>圓筒</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>원통</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>을 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하는 <ruby class="hanja grade10"><rb>圓筒</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>원통</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>音樂箱子</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>음악상자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>(cylinder music box)와 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>突起</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>돌기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가있는 <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%9C%93%E7%9B%A4&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="圓盤 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade32 unknown"><rb>圓盤</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>원반</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>을 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하는 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>圓盤</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>원반</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>音樂箱子</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>음악상자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>(disc music box)로 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>分類</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>분류</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>된다.
