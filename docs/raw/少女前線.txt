<h2><span id="槪要(개요)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E5%B0%91%E5%A5%B3%E5%89%8D%E7%B7%9A&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要(개요)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><b><ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>少女前線</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>소녀전선</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></b>은 <ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>中國</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>중국</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 MICA 팀에서 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>開發</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개발</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 <a href="/index.php?title=%EB%AA%A8%EB%B0%94%EC%9D%BC_%EA%B2%8C%EC%9E%84&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="모바일 게임 (없는 문서)">모바일</a> <a href="/index.php?title=%E6%88%B0%E7%95%A5%EA%B2%8C%EC%9E%84&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="戰略게임 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade40 unknown"><rb>戰略</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>전략</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> <a href="/index.php?title=RPG&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="RPG (없는 문서)">RPG</a> 게임이다.
</p>
<h2><span id="名稱(명칭)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E5.90.8D.E7.A8.B1.28.EB.AA.85.EC.B9.AD.29"><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>名稱</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>명칭</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E5%B0%91%E5%A5%B3%E5%89%8D%E7%B7%9A&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 名稱(명칭)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>英文名稱</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>영문명칭</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 ‘Girls' Frontline’이며, <a href="/wiki/%E6%97%A5%E6%9C%AC" title="日本"><ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>日本</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일본</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>에서는 <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%95%86%E6%A8%99%E6%AC%8A&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="商標權 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade40 unknown"><rb>商標權</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>상표권</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a><ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>問題</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>문제</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 ‘ドールズフロントライン’(Dolls Frontline, “<ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>人形前線</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>인형전선</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>”)이라는 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>名稱</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>명칭</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>發賣</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>발매</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되었다.
</p>
