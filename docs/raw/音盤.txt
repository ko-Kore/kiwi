</p>

<h3><span id="定規音盤(정규음반)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E5.AE.9A.E8.A6.8F.E9.9F.B3.E7.9B.A4.28.EC.A0.95.EA.B7.9C.EC.9D.8C.EB.B0.98.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>定規音盤</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>정규음반</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E9%9F%B3%E7%9B%A4&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="부분 편집: 定規音盤(정규음반)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>普通</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>보통</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 10<ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>曲</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>곡</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>內外</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>내외</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 이루어져 있으며 이는 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>大部分</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대부분</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <a href="/index.php?title=CD&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="CD (없는 문서)">CD</a> 하나 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>分量</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>분량</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다.
</p>
<h3><span id="EP_(미니_音盤(음반))"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="EP_.28.EB.AF.B8.EB.8B.88_.E9.9F.B3.E7.9B.A4.28.EC.9D.8C.EB.B0.98.29.29">EP (미니 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>音盤</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>음반</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>)</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E9%9F%B3%E7%9B%A4&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="부분 편집: EP (미니 音盤(음반))">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>普通</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>보통</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 5~6<ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>曲</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>곡</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>內外</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>내외</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 이루어져 있다. <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>勿論</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>물론</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> EP에도 10<ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>曲</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>곡</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>程度</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>정도</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 들어가는 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>境遇</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>경우</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 있는데, 이는 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>切半</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>절반</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 <a href="/index.php?title=%E6%96%B0%E6%9B%B2&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="新曲 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade50 unknown"><rb>新曲</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>신곡</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>고 나머지 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>切半</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>절반</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 그 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>新曲</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>신곡</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <a href="/index.php?title=%E4%BC%B4%E5%A5%8F&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="伴奏 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade30 unknown"><rb>伴奏</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>반주</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>(inst)인 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>境遇</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>경우</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>大部分</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대부분</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다.
</p>
<h3><span id="싱글_音盤(음반)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".EC.8B.B1.EA.B8.80_.E9.9F.B3.E7.9B.A4.28.EC.9D.8C.EB.B0.98.29">싱글 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>音盤</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>음반</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E9%9F%B3%E7%9B%A4&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="부분 편집: 싱글 音盤(음반)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>普通</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>보통</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 1~3<ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>曲</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>곡</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>內外</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>내외</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 이루어져 있다. <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>大部分</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대부분</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>新曲</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>신곡</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 1<ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>曲</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>곡</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>과 그 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>新曲</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>신곡</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>伴奏</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>반주</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>(inst) 2<ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>曲</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>곡</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 이루어져 있다.
</p>
<h3><span id="라이브_音盤(음반)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".EB.9D.BC.EC.9D.B4.EB.B8.8C_.E9.9F.B3.E7.9B.A4.28.EC.9D.8C.EB.B0.98.29">라이브 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>音盤</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>음반</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E9%9F%B3%E7%9B%A4&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="부분 편집: 라이브 音盤(음반)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a href="/index.php?title=%E6%AD%8C%E6%89%8B&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="歌手 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade70 unknown"><rb>歌手</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>가수</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>가 <a href="/index.php?title=%EC%BD%98%EC%84%9C%ED%8A%B8&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="콘서트 (없는 문서)">콘서트</a>를 열고 그 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>實況</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>실황</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>錄音</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>녹음</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하여 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>音盤</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>음반</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 내는 것이다. <a href="/index.php?title=CD&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="CD (없는 문서)">CD</a> 2<ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>枚</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>매</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>以上</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>이상</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 나오는 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>境遇</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>경우</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>大部分</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대부분</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이며, <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>一般的</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일반적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>인 스튜디오 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>音盤</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>음반</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하고 또 다른 느낌을 받을 수 있다.
</p>
<h3><span id="컴필레이션_音盤(음반)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".EC.BB.B4.ED.95.84.EB.A0.88.EC.9D.B4.EC.85.98_.E9.9F.B3.E7.9B.A4.28.EC.9D.8C.EB.B0.98.29">컴필레이션 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>音盤</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>음반</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E9%9F%B3%E7%9B%A4&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="부분 편집: 컴필레이션 音盤(음반)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>베스트 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>音盤</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>음반</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이라고도 한다. 컴필레이션 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>音盤</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>음반</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에는 여러 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>種類</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>종류</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 있는데,
</p>
<ul><li>한 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>歌手</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>가수</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 이제껏 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>發表</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>발표</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>音樂</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>음악</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>中</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>중</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>人氣</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>인기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>있거나 좋은 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>評價</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>평가</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 받는 것을 모아둔 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>音盤</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>음반</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></li>
<li><ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>未發表曲</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>미발표곡</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>들을 모아둔 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>音盤</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>음반</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></li>
<li>같은 <a href="/index.php?title=%E6%89%80%E5%B1%AC%E7%A4%BE&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="所屬社 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade40 unknown"><rb>所屬社</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>소속사</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>歌手</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>가수</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>들의 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>人氣曲</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>인기곡</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 모아둔 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>音盤</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>음반</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></li></ul>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>等等</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>등등</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 있다.
</p>
