<h2><span id="槪要(개요)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%EB%A7%A5%EB%AA%A8%EB%8B%9D&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要(개요)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/index.php?title=%EB%A7%A5%EB%8F%84%EB%82%A0%EB%93%9C&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="맥도날드 (없는 문서)">맥도날드</a>에서 아침<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>時間帶</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>시간대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에만 <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>販賣</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>판매</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하는 메뉴의 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>總稱</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>총칭</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>.
</p><p><ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>午前</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>오전</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>4<ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>時</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>부터 10<ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>時</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>30<ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>分</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>분</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>까지만 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>注文</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>주문</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>할 수 있으며 이 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>時間帶</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>시간대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에는 <a href="/wiki/%EB%B9%85%EB%A7%A5" title="빅맥">빅맥</a><ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>等</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>등</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>一般</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일반</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>메뉴는 <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>販賣</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>판매</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하지 않는다.
</p>
<h2><span id="構成(구성)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.8B.E6.88.90.28.EA.B5.AC.EC.84.B1.29"><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>構成</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>구성</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%EB%A7%A5%EB%AA%A8%EB%8B%9D&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 構成(구성)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
