<h2><span id="槪要(개요)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E4%B8%89%E6%98%9F_%EA%B0%A4%EB%9F%AD%EC%8B%9C&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要(개요)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/index.php?title=%E4%B8%89%E6%98%9F%E9%9B%BB%E5%AD%90&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="三星電子 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade42 unknown"><rb>三星電子</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>삼성전자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>의 <a href="/wiki/%EC%8A%A4%EB%A7%88%ED%8A%B8%ED%8F%B0" title="스마트폰">스마트폰</a> 및 <a href="/wiki/%ED%83%9C%EB%B8%94%EB%A6%BFPC" title="태블릿PC">태블릿PC</a>, <a href="/index.php?title=%EC%9B%A8%EC%96%B4%EB%9F%AC%EB%B8%94%E6%A9%9F%E5%99%A8&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="웨어러블機器 (없는 문서)">웨어러블<ruby class="hanja grade40 unknown"><rb>機器</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a><ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>等</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>등</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>包含</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>포함</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하는 <a href="/index.php?title=%EC%8A%A4%EB%A7%88%ED%8A%B8%E6%A9%9F%E5%99%A8&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="스마트機器 (없는 문서)">스마트<ruby class="hanja grade40 unknown"><rb>機器</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> <a href="/index.php?title=%EC%8B%9C%EB%A6%AC%EC%A6%88&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="시리즈 (없는 문서)">시리즈</a>이다. <a href="/index.php?title=2009%E5%B9%B4&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="2009年 (없는 문서)">2009<ruby class="hanja grade80 unknown"><rb>年</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>년</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>初代</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>초대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 모델 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>出市</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>출시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>始作</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>시작</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>現在</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>현재</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>까지 이어지고 있는 시리즈이다.
</p>
<h2><span id="歷史(역사)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.AD.B7.E5.8F.B2.28.EC.97.AD.EC.82.AC.29"><ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>歷史</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>역사</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E4%B8%89%E6%98%9F_%EA%B0%A4%EB%9F%AD%EC%8B%9C&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 歷史(역사)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>出市</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>출시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>初期</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>초기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에는 <a href="/index.php?title=%EC%95%A0%ED%94%8C&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="애플 (없는 문서)">애플</a> <a href="/wiki/%EC%95%84%EC%9D%B4%ED%8F%B0" title="아이폰">아이폰</a>에 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>對</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 <ruby class="hanja grade10"><rb>剽竊</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>표절</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>論難</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>논란</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>과 함께 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>特許</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>특허</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>紛爭</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>분쟁</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 휘말렸다. 이 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>特許</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>특허</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>攻防</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>공방</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 자그마치 7<ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>年</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>년</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>동안 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>繼續</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>계속</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되다 <a href="/index.php?title=2018%E5%B9%B4&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="2018年 (없는 문서)">2018<ruby class="hanja grade80 unknown"><rb>年</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>년</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>合意</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>합의</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 이뤄져 끝나게 되었다.
</p>
