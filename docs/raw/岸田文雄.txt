<p><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>岸田文雄</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>안전문웅</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>(기시다 후미오, 1957<ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>年</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>년</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>7<ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>月</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>월</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>29<ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>日</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> - )은 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>日本</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일본</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>政治人</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>정치인</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다. <a href="/index.php?title=%E8%87%AA%E7%94%B1%E6%B0%91%E4%B8%BB%E9%BB%A8&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="自由民主黨 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade42 unknown"><rb>自由民主黨</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자유민주당</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>所屬</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>소속</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>衆議院議員</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>중의원의원</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>, <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>第</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>제</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>100·101<ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>代</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%85%A7%E9%96%A3%E7%B8%BD%E7%90%86%E5%A4%A7%E8%87%A3&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="內閣總理大臣 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade32 unknown"><rb>內閣總理大臣</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>내각총리대신</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>이자 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>第</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>제</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>27<ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>代</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>自由民主黨</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자유민주당</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>總裁</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>총재</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다.
