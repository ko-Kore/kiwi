<h2><span id=".E6.A9.9F.E8.83.BD.28.EA.B8.B0.EB.8A.A5.29"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="機能(기능)"><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>機能</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기능</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%EC%B9%B4%EC%B9%B4%EC%98%A4%ED%86%A1&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 機能(기능)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span id=".EC.B1.84.ED.8C.85.E6.A9.9F.E8.83.BD.28.EA.B8.B0.EB.8A.A5.29"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="채팅機能(기능)">채팅<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>機能</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기능</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%EC%B9%B4%EC%B9%B4%EC%98%A4%ED%86%A1&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 채팅機能(기능)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<dl><dt><ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>個人對話</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개인대화</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></dt>
<dd><ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>親舊</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>친구</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>登錄</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>등록</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用者</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>와 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>對話</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대화</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>始作</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>시작</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>할 수 있다.</dd>
<dt>그룹<ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>對話</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대화</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></dt>
<dd><a href="/index.php?title=%E6%8B%9B%E5%BE%85&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="招待 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade40 unknown"><rb>招待</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>초대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>를 받으면 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>受諾</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>수락</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해서 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>入場</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>입장</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하는것이 아니라 다른사람에 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>依</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>의</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>招待</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>초대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 받으면 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>强制</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>강제</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>參加</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>참가</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되는 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>形態</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>형태</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다. <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>基本</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기본</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 프로필을 걸고 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>參與</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>참여</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하게 된다.</dd>
<dt>오픈채팅</dt>
<dd><ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>親舊登錄</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>친구등록</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 되어있지 않은 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用者</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>와도 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>自由</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자유</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>롭게 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>對話</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대화</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>할 수 있는 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>機能</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기능</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>. <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>個人對話</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개인대화</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>와 그룹<ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>對話</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대화</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 나뉘며, <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>對話房別</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대화방별</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>固有</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>고유</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>招待</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>초대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>링크를 만들어 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>共有</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>공유</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>할 수 있다.</dd></dl>
