<h2><span id="槪要(개요)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E5%85%AC%E7%82%B3%E7%A6%B9%E6%89%93%E5%AD%97%E6%A9%9F&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要(개요)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade12"><rb>公炳禹打字機</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>공병우타자기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>眼科醫師</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>안과의사</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%85%AC%E7%82%B3%E7%A6%B9&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="公炳禹 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade12 unknown"><rb>公炳禹</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>공병우</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>에 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>依</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>의</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>開發</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개발</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>된 <a href="/wiki/%E6%89%93%E5%AD%97%E6%A9%9F" title="打字機"><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>打字機</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>타자기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>를 말한다.
</p>
<h2><span id="特徵(특징)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E7.89.B9.E5.BE.B5.28.ED.8A.B9.EC.A7.95.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>特徵</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>특징</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E5%85%AC%E7%82%B3%E7%A6%B9%E6%89%93%E5%AD%97%E6%A9%9F&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 特徵(특징)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span id="雙焦點(쌍초점)_方式(방식)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E9.9B.99.E7.84.A6.E9.BB.9E.28.EC.8C.8D.EC.B4.88.EC.A0.90.29_.E6.96.B9.E5.BC.8F.28.EB.B0.A9.EC.8B.9D.29"><ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>雙焦點</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>쌍초점</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>方式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>방식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E5%85%AC%E7%82%B3%E7%A6%B9%E6%89%93%E5%AD%97%E6%A9%9F&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="부분 편집: 雙焦點(쌍초점) 方式(방식)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>一般的</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일반적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>인 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>英文</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>영문</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>·한글 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>打字機</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>타자기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>와달리 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>活字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>활자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>引導</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>인도</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되는 <ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>焦點</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>초점</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 두<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>個</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>씩 있다. 이는 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>初聲</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>초성</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>과<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>中聲</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>중성</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>打字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>타자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>할때 모두 커서가 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>前進</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>전진</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하므로 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>終聲</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>종성</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>普通位置</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>보통위치</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>보다 왼쪽에 가서 찍혀야 하기 때문이다. 이로써 <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%AE%8B%E5%9F%BA%E5%91%A8%E6%89%93%E5%AD%97%E6%A9%9F&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="宋基周打字機 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade12 unknown"><rb>宋基周打字機</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>송기주타자기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>에<ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>比</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>비</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>空白</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>공백</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 넣을때 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>以外</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>이외</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>一般的</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일반적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>인 <ruby class="hanja 문서)">스페이스바</a>가 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>介入</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개입</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하는 ‘군<ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>動作</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>동작</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>’을 줄였으며, 빠른<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>速度</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>속도</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>打字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>타자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>할때 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>活字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>활자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 꼬일 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>確率</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>확률</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>도 줄게 되었다.
</p>
<h3><span id="적은_쉬프트_使用(사용)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".EC.A0.81.EC.9D.80_.EC.89.AC.ED.94.84.ED.8A.B8_.E4.BD.BF.E7.94.A8.28.EC.82.AC.EC.9A.A9.29">적은 쉬프트 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E5%85%AC%E7%82%B3%E7%A6%B9%E6%89%93%E5%AD%97%E6%A9%9F&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="부분 편집: 적은 쉬프트 使用(사용)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>벌<ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>數</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>수</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가많아 위아랫글쇠에 한글<ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>子母</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자모</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 빼곡히들어간 <ruby class="hanja href="/index.php?title=1969%E5%B9%B4&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="1969年 (없는 문서)">1969<ruby class="hanja grade80 unknown"><rb>年</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>년</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> <ruby grade60"><rb>使用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 눈에띄게 적다. <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>數字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>숫자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>나 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>記號</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기호</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>, <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>頻度</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>빈도</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가적은 받침을 쓸때를 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>韓英打字機</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한영타자기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>도 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>開發</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개발</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>될수있었다.
</p>
<h3><span id="韓英打字機(한영타자기)의_存在(존재)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E9.9F.93.E8.8B.B1.E6.89.93.E5.AD.97.E6.A9.9F.28.ED.95.9C.EC.98.81.ED.83.80.EC.9E.90.EA.B8.B0.29.EC.9D.98_.E5.AD.98.E5.9C.A8.28.EC.A1.B4.EC.9E.AC.29"><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>韓英打字機</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한영타자기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>存在</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>존재</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E5%85%AC%E7%82%B3%E7%A6%B9%E6%89%93%E5%AD%97%E6%A9%9F&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="부분 편집: 韓英打字機(한영타자기)의 存在(존재)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>벌<ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>數</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>수</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 적은 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>特性</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>특성</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>를 모두 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>印字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>인자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>할수있는 모델이 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>開發</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개발</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되어 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>普及</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>보급</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되었다. 한글은 아랫글쇠, <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>英文</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>영문</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 윗글쇠에 들어가 쉬프트를 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>固定</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>고정</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>狀態</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>상태</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>英文</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>영문</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 치는 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>方式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>방식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>. <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>活字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>활자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 <ruby class="hanja
