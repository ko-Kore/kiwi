<h2><span id="背景(배경)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E8.83.8C.E6.99.AF.28.EB.B0.B0.EA.B2.BD.29"><ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>背景</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>배경</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=CJK%E4%BA%92%E6%8F%9B%E7%94%A8%E6%BC%A2%E5%AD%97&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 背景(배경)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>이 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>領域</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>영역</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 생기게 된 것은 <a href="/index.php?title=KS_X_1001&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="KS X 1001 (없는 문서)">KS X 1001</a>에서 같은 글<ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>韓國語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한국어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>讀音</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>독음</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>數</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>수</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>대로 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>重複配定</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>중복배정</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 일에서 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>起因</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기인</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한다. <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>例</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>예</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 들어, <a href="/index.php?title=%E6%A8%82&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="樂 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade62 unknown"><rb>樂</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>악</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>이라는 글<ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 있다 하면 '낙', '락', '악', '요' <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>各各</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>각각</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>對</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 글<ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>(코드포인트)를 하나씩 따로 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>配定</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>배정</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>시킨 것이다. 이 글<ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>들을 <a href="/wiki/%EC%9C%A0%EB%8B%88%EC%BD%94%EB%93%9C" title="유니코드">유니코드</a> <a href="/wiki/CJK%E7%B5%B1%E5%90%88%E6%BC%A2%E5%AD%97" title="CJK統合漢字">CJK<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>統合漢字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>통합한자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>로 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>統合</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>통합</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하면서도, KS<ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>完成型</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>완성형</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 인코딩으로 되돌릴 때를 생각해 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>統合</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>통합</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>된 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>代表音</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대표음</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>(<ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>樂</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>악</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>境遇</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>경우</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에는 '악')을 나타내는 코드 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>以外</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>이외</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에도 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>重複</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>중복</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>된 글<ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>들을 이 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>領域</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>영역</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 실은 것이다.
</p>
<h2><span id="問題點(문제점)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E5.95.8F.E9.A1.8C.E9.BB.9E.28.EB.AC.B8.EC.A0.9C.EC.A0.90.29"><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>問題點</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>문제점</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=CJK%E4%BA%92%E6%8F%9B%E7%94%A8%E6%BC%A2%E5%AD%97&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 問題點(문제점)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>유저<ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>立場</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>입장</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에선 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>全</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>전</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>혀 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>分間</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>분간</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>도 안되는 <a href="/index.php?title=%EB%8B%AE%EC%9D%80%EA%BC%B4%E6%96%87%E5%AD%97&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="닮은꼴文字 (없는 문서)">닮은꼴<ruby class="hanja grade70 unknown"><rb>文字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>문자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>가 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>文書</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>문서</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 곳곳에 숨어있게 되어 여러 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>問題</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>문제</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>發生</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>발생</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한다. <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>檢索</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>검색</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 할때도 '리'로 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>變換</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>변환</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 '<a href="/wiki/%E6%9D%8E" title="李"><ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>李</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>이</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>'는 '이'로 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>變換</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>변환</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해서 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>檢索</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>검색</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해선 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>結果</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>결과</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 걸리지 않는다.
</p><p><a href="/index.php?title=%E6%9B%B8%E9%AB%94&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="書體 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade62 unknown"><rb>書體</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>서체</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>를 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>함에 있어서도 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>問題</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>문제</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 있다. <a href="/wiki/%E9%9F%93%E5%9C%8B%E8%AA%9E" title="韓國語"><ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>中國語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>중국어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> 폰트를 쓰게 되면 <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>互換用漢字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>호환용한자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja <h2><span id="定規化(정규화)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E5.AE.9A.E8.A6.8F.E5.8C.96.28.EC.A0.95.EA.B7.9C.ED.99.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>定規化</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>정규화</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=CJK%E4%BA%92%E6%8F%9B%E7%94%A8%E6%BC%A2%E5%AD%97&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="부분 편집: 定規化(정규화)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/wiki/%EC%9C%A0%EB%8B%88%EC%BD%94%EB%93%9C" title="유니코드">유니코드</a> <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>定規化</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>정규화</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>方式中</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>방식중</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 어느것으로 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>處理</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>처리</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하더라도 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>基本的</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기본적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 모든 <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>互換用漢字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>호환용한자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는 <a href="/wiki/CJK%E7%B5%B1%E5%90%88%E6%BC%A2%E5%AD%97" title="CJK統合漢字">CJK<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>統合漢字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>통합한자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>로 <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>代替</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대체</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>된다. <a href="/index.php?title=%EA%B5%AC%EA%B8%80_%ED%81%AC%EB%A1%AC&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="구글 크롬 (없는 문서)">구글 크롬</a> <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>等</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>등</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>一部</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일부</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>應用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>응용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>프로그램에서 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>基本</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기본</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>該當</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>해당</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>處理</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>처리</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 하고 있으며, 이 위키에서도 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>自動</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자동</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 이루어지고 있다.
</p>
<h2><span id="特異事項(특이사항)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E7.89.B9.E7.95.B0.E4.BA.8B.E9.A0.85.28.ED.8A.B9.EC.9D.B4.EC.82.AC.ED.95.AD.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>特異事項</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>특이사항</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=CJK%E4%BA%92%E6%8F%9B%E7%94%A8%E6%BC%A2%E5%AD%97&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="부분 편집: 特異事項(특이사항)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>이 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>領域</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>영역</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>配定</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>배정</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>된 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>漢字中</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한자중</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>一部</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일부</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>實際</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>실제</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>互換用漢字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>호환용한자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 아니다! <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>該當</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>해당</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 12<ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는 <a href="/wiki/CJK%E7%B5%B1%E5%90%88%E6%BC%A2%E5%AD%97" title="CJK統合漢字">CJK<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>統合漢字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>통합한자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>內</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>내</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>重複</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>중복</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>된 글<ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>存在</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>존재</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하지 않으며, <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>統合漢字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>통합한자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로서 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>處理</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>처리</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>된다.
</p>
