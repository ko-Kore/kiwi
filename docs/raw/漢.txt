<h2><span id=".E5.AD.97.E6.BA.90.28.EC.9E.90.EC.9B.90.29"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="字源(자원)"><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>字源</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자원</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E6%BC%A2&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 字源(자원)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>原來</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>원래</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는 뜻을 나타내는 <a href="/index.php?title=%E6%B0%B5&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="氵 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade0 unknown"><rb>氵</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>수</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>에 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>音</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>음</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 나타내는 <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%A0%87&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="堇 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade0 unknown"><rb>堇</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>근</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>이 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>變形</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>변형</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>된 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>模樣</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>모양</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 합쳐져, <a href="/index.php?title=%E6%8F%9A%E5%AD%90%E6%B1%9F&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="揚子江 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade32 unknown"><rb>揚子江</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>양자강</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>의 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>支流</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>지류</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>인 <a href="/index.php?title=%E6%BC%A2%E6%B0%B4&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="漢水 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade72 unknown"><rb>漢水</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한수</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>를 가리키는 글<ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 쓰였으나, <a href="/index.php?title=%E6%BC%A2%EB%82%98%EB%9D%BC&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="漢나라 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade72 unknown"><rb>漢</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>나라</a>가 이 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>地域</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>지역</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에서 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>繁盛</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>번성</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하여 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>漢</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>나라 사람, 그 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>民族</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>민족</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>인 <a href="/index.php?title=%E6%BC%A2%E6%97%8F&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="漢族 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade60 unknown"><rb>漢族</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한족</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>을 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>代表</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대표</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하는 글<ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 쓰이게 되었다.
</p>
