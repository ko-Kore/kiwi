<p>님의<ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>代</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>(<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>君</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>군</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>が<ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>代</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>)는 <a href="/wiki/%E6%97%A5%E6%9C%AC" title="日本"><ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>日本</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일본</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>의<a href="/wiki/%E5%9C%8B%E6%AD%8C" title="國歌"><ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>國歌</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>국가</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>이다.

<p><ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>本來</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>본래</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는<a href="/index.php?title=%E5%8F%A4%E6%AD%8C&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="古歌 (없는 문서)"><ruby <h3><span id="英譯(영역)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E8.8B.B1.E8.AD.AF.28.EC.98.81.EC.97.AD.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>英譯</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>영역</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%EB%8B%98%EC%9D%98%E4%BB%A3&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="부분 편집: 英譯(영역)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>以下</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>이하</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>歌詞</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>가사</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는 <a href="/wiki/%E8%8B%B1%E5%9C%8B" title="英國"><ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>英國</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>영국</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>의 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>日本硏究家</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일본연구가</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <a href="/index.php?title=%EB%B0%B0%EC%A6%90_%ED%95%A0_%EC%B2%B4%EC%9E%84%EB%B2%8C%EB%A6%B0&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="배즐 할 체임벌린 (없는 문서)">배즐 할 체임벌린</a>의 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>英譯</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>영역</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>引用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>인용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한다.
</p>
<blockquote>
<p>A thousand years of happy life be thine! <br>
Live on, my Lord, till what are pebbles now, <br>
By age united, to great rocks shall grow, <br>
Whose venerable sides the moss doth line.
</p>
</blockquote>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>古歌</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>고가</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>由來</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>유래</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로하는 님의<ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>代</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>歌詞</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>가사</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>適切</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>적절</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하도록 “thine”과 “doth”<ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>等</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>등</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>, <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>古風表現</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>고풍표현</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하는<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>點</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>점</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>과, <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>詩的構文</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>시적구문</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>特徵的</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>특징적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다. 
</p>
