<h2><span id="槪要(개요)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%EC%8A%88%EC%8A%88%EB%B2%84%EA%B1%B0&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要(개요)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<blockquote><p>탱~글한 통새우살 가득~ 슈슈 버거!</p></blockquote>
<p><a href="/wiki/%EB%A7%A5%EB%8F%84%EB%82%A0%EB%93%9C" title="맥도날드">맥도날드</a>에서 <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>販賣</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>판매</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>中</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>중</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>인 햄버거 메뉴이다.
</p>
<h2><span id="名稱(명칭)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E5.90.8D.E7.A8.B1.28.EB.AA.85.EC.B9.AD.29"><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>名稱</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>명칭</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%EC%8A%88%EC%8A%88%EB%B2%84%EA%B1%B0&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 名稱(명칭)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>‘슈슈’는 <b>슈</b>프림 <b>슈</b>림프(Supreme Shrimp)를 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>意味</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>의미</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한다.<a rel="nofollow" class="external autonumber" href="https://www.mcdonalds.co.kr/kor/menu/detail.do?page=1&amp;seq=91&amp;sub_category_seq=1">[1]</a>
</p>
<h2><span id="構成(구성)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.8B.E6.88.90.28.EA.B5.AC.EC.84.B1.29"><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>構成</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>구성</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%EC%8A%88%EC%8A%88%EB%B2%84%EA%B1%B0&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="부분 편집: 構成(구성)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>빵, 소스, 슈림프 패티, 토마토, 양상추, 소스 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>順</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>순</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>構成</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>구성</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되어 있다.
</p>
