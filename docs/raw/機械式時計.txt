<h2><span id=".E4.BD.9C.E5.8B.95.E6.96.B9.E5.BC.8F.28.EC.9E.91.EB.8F.99.EB.B0.A9.EC.8B.9D.29"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="作動方式(작동방식)"><ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>作動方式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>작동방식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E6%A9%9F%E6%A2%B0%E5%BC%8F%E6%99%82%E8%A8%88&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 作動方式(작동방식)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>大體</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대체</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 <a href="/index.php?title=%E8%83%8E%E8%91%89&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="胎葉 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade20 unknown"><rb>胎葉</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>태엽</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>이나 <a href="/index.php?title=%E9%8C%98&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="錘 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade10 unknown"><rb>錘</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>추</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> 같은 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>動力源</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>동력원</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에서 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>發生</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>발생</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>된 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>回轉力</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>회전력</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>脫盡裝置</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>탈진장치</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>通過</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>통과</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>시켜 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>一定</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일정</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>速度</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>속도</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>制限</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>제한</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해서 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>時計</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>시계</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>바늘을 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>作動</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>작동</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>시키는 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>原理</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>원리</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다.
</p>
<h3><span id=".E5.8B.95.E5.8A.9B.E6.BA.90.28.EB.8F.99.EB.A0.A5.EC.9B.90.29"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="動力源(동력원)"><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>動力源</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>동력원</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E6%A9%9F%E6%A2%B0%E5%BC%8F%E6%99%82%E8%A8%88&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="부분 편집: 動力源(동력원)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>바닥 <ruby class="hanja grade0"><rb>据置型</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>거치형</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 <ruby class="hanja grade10"><rb>錘</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>추</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>落下式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>낙하식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>大多數</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대다수</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이며, <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>壁</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>벽</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>걸이<ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>型</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>형</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 <a href="/index.php?title=%E8%83%8E%E8%91%89&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="胎葉 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade20 unknown"><rb>胎葉</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>태엽</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>을 쓰기도 한다. <ruby class="hanja grade10"><rb>錘</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>추</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 감아올리거나 <ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>胎葉</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>태엽</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 감을 때에는 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>專用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>전용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby><a href="/index.php?title=%EC%97%B4%EC%87%A0&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="열쇠 (없는 문서)">열쇠</a>를 꽂아서 몇번씩 돌려준다.
</p><p><a href="/index.php?title=%E6%87%B7%E4%B8%AD%E6%99%82%E8%A8%88&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="懷中時計 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade32 unknown"><rb>懷中時計</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>회중시계</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>나 <a href="/index.php?title=%EC%86%90%EB%AA%A9%E6%99%82%E8%A8%88&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="손목時計 (없는 문서)">손목<ruby class="hanja grade62 unknown"><rb>時計</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>시계</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>는 모두 <ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>胎葉</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>태엽</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한다. <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>側面</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>측면</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 달린 <a href="/index.php?title=%E9%BE%8D%E9%A0%AD&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="龍頭 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade40 unknown"><rb>龍頭</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>용두</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>를 밖으로 뽑지 않은 채로 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>定</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>정</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해진 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>方向</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>방향</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 몇번씩 돌려주면 감아줄 수 있다. 손목<ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>時計中</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>시계중</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에서 차고 움직이기만 해도 뒷<ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>面</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>면</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 달린 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>回轉式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>회전식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade10"><rb>錘</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>추</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 돌아가면서 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>自動</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자동</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 <ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>胎葉</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>태엽</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 감기게 되는 <a href="/index.php?title=%EC%98%A4%ED%86%A0%EB%A7%A4%ED%8B%B1%E6%99%82%E8%A8%88&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="오토매틱時計 (없는 문서)">오토매틱<ruby class="hanja grade62 unknown"><rb>時計</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>시계</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>라는 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>種類</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>종류</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>도 있다.
</p>
<h2><span id=".E7.89.B9.E5.BE.B5.28.ED.8A.B9.EC.A7.95.29"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="特徵(특징)"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>特徵</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>특징</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E6%A9%9F%E6%A2%B0%E5%BC%8F%E6%99%82%E8%A8%88&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="부분 편집: 特徵(특징)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>大部分</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대부분</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <a href="/index.php?title=%E6%B0%B4%E6%99%B6%E6%99%82%E8%A8%88&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="水晶時計 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade12 unknown"><rb>水晶時計</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>수정시계</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>의 <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>秒針</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>초침</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 1<ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>秒</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>초</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 한<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>番</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>번</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 움직이는 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>反面</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>반면</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>機械式時計</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기계식시계</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는 1<ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>秒</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>초</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 몇번이고 움직인다. 손목<ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>時計</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>시계</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>程度</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>정도</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 되면 멈추지 않고 물 흐르듯이 움직이는 것처럼 보이기도 한다. 그 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>理由</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>이유</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는 <a href="/index.php?title=%E8%AA%A4%E5%B7%AE&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="誤差 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade40 unknown"><rb>誤差</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>오차</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>를 줄이기 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>爲</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>위</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해서인데, 한 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>番</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>번</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>誤差</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>오차</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 생기더라도 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>週期</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>주기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 짧기 때문에 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>全體</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>전체</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>誤差</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>오차</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 끼치는 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>影響</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>영향</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 줄어드는 것이다.
</p><p>'<ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>時計</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>시계</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>'라고 하면 떠오르는 <a href="/index.php?title=%E6%93%AC%E8%81%B2%E8%AA%9E&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="擬聲語 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade10 unknown"><rb>擬聲語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>의성어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>인 '째깍째깍'이나 '똑딱똑딱'도 이런 짧은 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>週期</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>주기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>作動</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>작동</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하는 소리를 흉내낸 것이다. <del><ruby class="hanja grade12"><rb>水晶時計</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>수정시계</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>였다면 '칙 칙 칙'일 것이다</del>
