<h2><span id="컴퓨터에서"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".EC.BB.B4.ED.93.A8.ED.84.B0.EC.97.90.EC.84.9C">컴퓨터에서</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E5%85%A5%E5%8A%9B&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="부분 편집: 컴퓨터에서">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
