<html><head></head><body><div class="mw-parser-output"><h2><span id="概要(개요)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A6.82.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade0"><rb>概要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E9%9D%9E%E6%AD%A6%E8%A3%9D%E5%9C%B0%E5%B8%B6_%E4%B8%80%E9%83%A8%E5%8D%80%E5%9F%9F_%E9%96%8B%E6%94%BE%EC%97%90_%E5%B0%8D%ED%95%9C_%E5%9C%8B%E9%9A%9B%E8%81%AF%E5%90%88%E8%BB%8D%EA%B3%BC_%E6%9C%9D%E9%AE%AE%E4%BA%BA%E6%B0%91%E8%BB%8D%E9%96%93_%E5%90%88%E6%84%8F%E6%9B%B8_(2000%E5%B9%B4)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 概要(개요)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>韓半島</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한반도</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>非武裝地帶</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>비무장지대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>西側</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>서측</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>開放</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개방</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>關</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>관</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 <a href="/wiki/%E9%9F%93%E5%9C%8B%E5%81%9C%E6%88%B0%E5%8D%94%E5%AE%9A" title="韓國停戰協定"><ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>韓國停戰協定</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한국정전협정</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>의 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>追加</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>추가</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>合意</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>합의</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다.
</p>
<h2><span id="條項(조항)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A2.9D.E9.A0.85.28.EC.A1.B0.ED.95.AD.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>條項</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>조항</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E9%9D%9E%E6%AD%A6%E8%A3%9D%E5%9C%B0%E5%B8%B6_%E4%B8%80%E9%83%A8%E5%8D%80%E5%9F%9F_%E9%96%8B%E6%94%BE%EC%97%90_%E5%B0%8D%ED%95%9C_%E5%9C%8B%E9%9A%9B%E8%81%AF%E5%90%88%E8%BB%8D%EA%B3%BC_%E6%9C%9D%E9%AE%AE%E4%BA%BA%E6%B0%91%E8%BB%8D%E9%96%93_%E5%90%88%E6%84%8F%E6%9B%B8_(2000%E5%B9%B4)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 條項(조항)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>1. <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>雙方</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>쌍방</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>停戰協定</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>정전협정</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 따라 서울-<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>新義州間</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>신의주간</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>鐵道</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>철도</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>와 <ruby class="hanja grade12"><rb>汶山</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>문산</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby><sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup>-<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>開城間</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개성간</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>道路</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>도로</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>通過</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>통과</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하는 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>軍事分界線</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>군사분계선</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>과 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>非武裝地帶</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>비무장지대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>一部區域</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일부구역</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>開放</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개방</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하여 그 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>區域</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>구역</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 <ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>南</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>남</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>과 <ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>北</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>북</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>管理區域</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>관리구역</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 한다.
</p><p>2. <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>雙方</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>쌍방</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>非武裝地帶</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>비무장지대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>안의 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>一部區域</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일부구역</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>開放</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개방</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>과 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>關聯</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>관련</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>된 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>技術</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기술</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 및 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>實務的</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>실무적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>인 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>問題</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>문제</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>들과 <ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>南</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>남</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>과 <ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>北</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>북</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>管理區域</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>관리구역</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에서 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>提起</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>제기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되는 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>軍事的</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>군사적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>인 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>問題</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>문제</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>들을 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>停戰協定</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>정전협정</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 따라 <ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>南</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>남</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>과 <ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>北</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>북</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>軍隊</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>군대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>들 사이에 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>協議處理</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>협의처리</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 하도록 한다.
</p><p>3. <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>本</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>본</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>合意書</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>합의서</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>板門店</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>판문점</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>將星級會談</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>장성급회담</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에서 <ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>批准</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>비준</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 날로부터 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>效力</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>효력</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>發生</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>발생</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한다.
</p><p>2000<ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>年</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>년</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 11<ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>月</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>월</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 17<ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>日</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>
</p>
