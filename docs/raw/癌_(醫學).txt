<h2><span id="槪要(개요)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E7%99%8C_(%E9%86%AB%E5%AD%B8)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要(개요)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/index.php?title=%E7%99%8C&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="癌 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade20 unknown"><rb>癌</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>암</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>이란 <a href="/index.php?title=%E7%B4%B0%E8%83%9E&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="細胞 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade40 unknown"><rb>細胞</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>세포</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>가　<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>非正常的</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>비정상적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 <ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>增殖</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>증식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하는 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>病</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>병</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 말한다. <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>惡性</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>악성</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby><a href="/wiki/%E8%85%AB%E7%98%8D" title="腫瘍"><ruby class="hanja grade10"><rb>腫瘍</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>종양</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>이라고도 한다.
</p>
<h2><span id="特徵(특징)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E7.89.B9.E5.BE.B5.28.ED.8A.B9.EC.A7.95.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>特徵</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>특징</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E7%99%8C_(%E9%86%AB%E5%AD%B8)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 特徵(특징)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>壽命</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>수명</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 다한 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>細胞</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>세포</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>消滅</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>소멸</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해야 하는데, <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>消滅</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>소멸</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하지 않고 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>非正常的</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>비정상적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 <ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>增殖</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>증식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해서 몸을 망가뜨린다. <a href="/wiki/%E5%A4%A7%E9%9F%93%E6%B0%91%E5%9C%8B" title="大韓民國"><ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>大韓民國</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대한민국</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> <a href="/wiki/%E6%AD%BB%E4%BA%A1" title="死亡"><ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>死亡</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사망</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>原因</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>원인</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 1<ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>位</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>위</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>다.
더불어서 <ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>癌</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>암</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>血液</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>혈액</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>,림프절을 통해 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>轉移</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>전이</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 해서 다른 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>臟器</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>장기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>나 뼈로 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>移動</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>이동</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한다. 4<ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>期</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>(<ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>末期癌</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>말기암</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>)일수록 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>完治率</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>완치솔</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 낮아진다.
</p>
