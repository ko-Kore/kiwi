<p><ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>多國語基本平面</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>다국어기본평면</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>(<span style="font-size: smaller;"><a href="/wiki/%E8%8B%B1%E8%AA%9E" title="英語">英語</a>: </span><span lang="en">Basic Multilingual Plane</span>)은 <a href="/wiki/%EC%9C%A0%EB%8B%88%EC%BD%94%EB%93%9C" title="유니코드">유니코드</a>의 U+0000부터 U+FFFF까지에 <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>該當</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>해당</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하는 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>平面</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>평면</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다. <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>近現代</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>근현대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>文字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>문자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>大部分</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대부분</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 여기에 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>屬</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>속</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하며, <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%AE%8C%E6%88%90%E5%9E%8B&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="完成型 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade20 unknown"><rb>完成型</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>완성형</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> 한글<ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>音節</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>음절</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>과 <a href="/wiki/CJK%E7%B5%B1%E5%90%88%E6%BC%A2%E5%AD%97" title="CJK統合漢字">CJK<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>統合漢字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>통합한자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>가 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>大部分</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대부분</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>領域</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>영역</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 차지한다.
</p>
<h2><span id=".E6.A7.8B.E6.88.90.28.EA.B5.AC.EC.84.B1.29"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="構成(구성)"><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>構成</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>구성</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E5%A4%9A%E5%9C%8B%E8%AA%9E%E5%9F%BA%E6%9C%AC%E5%B9%B3%E9%9D%A2&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 構成(구성)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/wiki/%ED%8C%8C%EC%9D%BC:Roadmap_to_Unicode_BMP_multilingual.svg" class="image"><img alt="Roadmap to Unicode BMP multilingual.svg" src="https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/0/05/Roadmap_to_Unicode_BMP_multilingual.svg/langko-850px-Roadmap_to_Unicode_BMP_multilingual.svg.png" decoding="async" width="850" height="500" srcset="https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/0/05/Roadmap_to_Unicode_BMP_multilingual.svg/langko-1275px-Roadmap_to_Unicode_BMP_multilingual.svg.png 1.5x, https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/0/05/Roadmap_to_Unicode_BMP_multilingual.svg/langko-1700px-Roadmap_to_Unicode_BMP_multilingual.svg.png 2x"></a>
</p><p>그림에서 아래쪽 3<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>行</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>행</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 걸쳐있는 붉은<ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>色</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>색</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> '<a href="/index.php?title=%E6%9D%B1%E4%BA%9E%E7%B4%B0%E4%BA%9E&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="東亞細亞 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade32 unknown"><rb>東亞細亞</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>동아세아</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>文字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>문자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>' <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>領域</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>영역</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 다름아닌 <a href="/wiki/%E7%8F%BE%E4%BB%A3%ED%95%9C%EA%B8%80_11172%E5%AD%97" title="現代한글 11172字"><ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>現代</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>현대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한글 11172<ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>. 그 위의 연한 붉은<ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>色</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>색</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 <a href="/wiki/CJK%E7%B5%B1%E5%90%88%E6%BC%A2%E5%AD%97" title="CJK統合漢字">CJK<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>統合漢字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>통합한자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>들이다.
</p><p>U+D800부터 U+DFFF까지는 <a href="/wiki/UTF-16" title="UTF-16">UTF-16</a>의 서러게이트<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>雙</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>쌍</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>爲</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>위</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>豫約</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>예약</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되어 있다.
</p>
