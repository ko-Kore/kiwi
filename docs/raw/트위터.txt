<h2><span id="槪要(개요)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%ED%8A%B8%EC%9C%84%ED%84%B0&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要(개요)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>트위터(Twitter)는 <a href="/index.php?title=%EC%86%8C%EC%85%9C_%EB%84%A4%ED%8A%B8%EC%9B%8C%ED%81%AC_%EC%84%9C%EB%B9%84%EC%8A%A4&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="소셜 네트워크 서비스 (없는 문서)">소셜 네트워크 서비스</a> <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>兼</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>겸</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <a href="/index.php?title=%EB%A7%88%EC%9D%B4%ED%81%AC%EB%A1%9C%EB%B8%94%EB%A1%9C%EA%B7%B8&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="마이크로블로그 (없는 문서)">마이크로블로그</a> 서비스이다.
</p>
<h2><span id="機能(기능)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A9.9F.E8.83.BD.28.EA.B8.B0.EB.8A.A5.29"><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>機能</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기능</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%ED%8A%B8%EC%9C%84%ED%84%B0&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 機能(기능)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span id="트윗"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".ED.8A.B8.EC.9C.97">트윗</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%ED%8A%B8%EC%9C%84%ED%84%B0&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="부분 편집: 트윗">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>‘트윗’이라 부르는 280<ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>以內</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>이내</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>短文</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>단문</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 <ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>揭示</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>게시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>할 수 있으며, 이는 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>自身</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자신</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>과 팔로워들의 타임라인에 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>表示</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>표시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>된다.
</p>
<h3><span id="팔로우"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".ED.8C.94.EB.A1.9C.EC.9A.B0">팔로우</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%ED%8A%B8%EC%9C%84%ED%84%B0&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="부분 편집: 팔로우">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>願</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>원</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하는 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用者</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 팔로우할 수 있다. 팔로우하는 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用者</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 <ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>揭示</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>게시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 트윗은 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>自身</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자신</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 타임라인에 <ruby class="hanja grade50"><rb>表示</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>표시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>된다.
</p>
<h3><span id="다이렉트_메시지"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".EB.8B.A4.EC.9D.B4.EB.A0.89.ED.8A.B8_.EB.A9.94.EC.8B.9C.EC.A7.80">다이렉트 메시지</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%ED%8A%B8%EC%9C%84%ED%84%B0&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="부분 편집: 다이렉트 메시지">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>許容</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>허용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用者</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>들과 <a href="/index.php?title=%EC%9D%B8%EC%8A%A4%ED%84%B4%ED%8A%B8_%EB%A9%94%EC%8B%A0%EC%A0%80&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="인스턴트 메신저 (없는 문서)">인스턴트 메신저</a>와 같이 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>個人</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개인</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>對話</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대화</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 주고받을 수 있다.
</p>
<h3><span id="플릿"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".ED.94.8C.EB.A6.BF">플릿</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%ED%8A%B8%EC%9C%84%ED%84%B0&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="부분 편집: 플릿">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>文字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>문자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>나 이미지로 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>構成</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>구성</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>된 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>內容</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>내용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 24<ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>時間</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>시간</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>동안만 <ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>揭示</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>게시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>할 수 있다.
</p>
