<h2><span id="槪要(개요)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E8%87%BA&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要(개요)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>臺</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>, <ruby class="hanja grade0"><rb>墩臺</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>돈대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>等</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>등</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 뜻을 나타내는 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>漢字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다. <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>言論</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>언론</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에서 <a href="/wiki/%E8%87%BA%E7%81%A3" class="mw-redirect" title="臺灣"><ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>臺灣</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대만</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>을 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>指稱</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>지칭</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>할 때 쓰인다.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/%E4%B8%AD%E5%9C%8B" title="中國"><ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>中國</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>중국</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>·<a href="/wiki/%E6%97%A5%E6%9C%AC" title="日本"><ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>日本</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일본</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>에서는 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>傳統的</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>전통적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 <a href="/wiki/%E6%BC%A2%E5%AD%97%E6%96%87%E5%8C%96%E5%9C%88" title="漢字文化圈"><ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>漢字文化圈</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한자문화권</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>에서 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>臺</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 빨리 쓸 때 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>發音</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>발음</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 같은 <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%8F%B0&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="台 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade12 unknown"><rb>台</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>태</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>로 쓰는 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>境遇</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>경우</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 많다는 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>點</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>점</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 들어 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>公式的</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>공식적</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>臺</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>廢止</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>폐지</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하고 <ruby class="hanja grade12"><rb>台</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>태</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 <ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>倂合</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>병합</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>시켰다. <a href="/wiki/%E8%87%BA%E7%81%A3" class="mw-redirect" title="臺灣"><ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>臺灣</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대만</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>에서도 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>自國</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자국</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>表記</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>표기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 <ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>臺灣</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대만</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>代身</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대신</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 “<ruby class="hanja grade12"><rb>台灣</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>태만</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>”으로 쓰는 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>境遇</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>경우</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 많다.
</p>
