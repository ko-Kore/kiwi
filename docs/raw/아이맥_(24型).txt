<h2><span id=".E7.89.B9.E5.BE.B5.28.ED.8A.B9.EC.A7.95.29"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="特徵(특징)"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>特徵</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>특징</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%EC%95%84%EC%9D%B4%EB%A7%A5_(24%E5%9E%8B)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 特徵(특징)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span id=".EB.94.94.EC.9E.90.EC.9D.B8"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="디자인">디자인</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%EC%95%84%EC%9D%B4%EB%A7%A5_(24%E5%9E%8B)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 디자인">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a href="/index.php?title=%EC%95%A0%ED%94%8C_M1&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="애플 M1 (없는 문서)">애플 M1</a>칩이 <ruby class="hanja grade10"><rb>搭載</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>탑재</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되어 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>構造</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>구조</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>單純</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>단순</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해져 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>畵面</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>화면</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 밑의 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>空間</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>공간</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>主要部品</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>주요부품</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 모두 들어가게 되어 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>全體</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>전체</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 두께가 11.5mm로 매우 얇아졌다. 가운데로 갈수록 두꺼워지는 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>形態</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>형태</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>였던 <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>旣存</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기존</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>모델과 달리 받침<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>臺</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>除外</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>제외</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 모든 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>部分</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>부분</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 두께가 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>一定</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일정</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하다.
</p><p><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>色相構成</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>색상구성</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 ‘실버’로 하나뿐이었던 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>以前世代</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>이전세대</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 모델과 달리 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>色相</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>색상</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 일곱가지로 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>多樣</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>다양</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하게 나왔다. <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>構成品</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>구성품</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>인 <a href="/index.php?title=%EB%A7%A4%EC%A7%81_%EB%A7%88%EC%9A%B0%EC%8A%A4&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="매직 마우스 (없는 문서)">매직 마우스</a>나 <a href="/index.php?title=%EB%A7%A4%EC%A7%81_%ED%8A%B8%EB%9E%99%ED%8C%A8%EB%93%9C&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="매직 트랙패드 (없는 문서)">매직 트랙패드</a>, <a href="/index.php?title=%EB%A7%A4%EC%A7%81_%ED%82%A4%EB%B3%B4%EB%93%9C&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="매직 키보드 (없는 문서)">매직 키보드</a>도 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>本體</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>본체</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>와 같은 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>色相</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>색상</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로 나온다. <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>畵面</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>화면</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>베젤은 흰<ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>色</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>색</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>으로만 나와서 <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%A5%BD%E4%B8%8D%E5%A5%BD&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="好不好 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade42 unknown"><rb>好不好</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>호부호</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>가 갈린다.
</p>
<h3><span id=".ED.8F.AC.ED.8A.B8"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="포트">포트</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%EC%95%84%EC%9D%B4%EB%A7%A5_(24%E5%9E%8B)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="부분 편집: 포트">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>뒷<ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>面</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>면</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>基本形</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기본형</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 <a href="/index.php?title=%EC%8D%AC%EB%8D%94%EB%B3%BC%ED%8A%B84&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="썬더볼트4 (없는 문서)">썬더볼트4</a><ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>端子</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>단자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 2<ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>口</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>구</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>, <ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>中級型</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>중급형</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>과 <ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>高級型</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>고급형</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>追加</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>추가</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 <a href="/index.php?title=USB_%ED%83%80%EC%9E%85C&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="USB 타입C (없는 문서)">USB 타입C</a> <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>端子</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>단자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 2<ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>口</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>구</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 더 들어간다. <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>左側面</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>좌측면</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에는 3.5mm 이어폰 잭이 있고, <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>有線</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>유선</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>랜 포트는 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>電源</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>전원</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>케이블과 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>統合</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>통합</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되어 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>電源</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>전원</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>어댑터에서 갈라져나온다.
</p>
