<h2><span id=".E7.89.B9.E5.BE.B5.28.ED.8A.B9.EC.A7.95.29"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="特徵(특징)"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>特徵</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>특징</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E6%BC%A2%E5%AD%97%E7%9B%B4%E6%8E%A5%E5%85%A5%E5%8A%9B&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 特徵(특징)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/wiki/%E5%90%8C%E9%9F%B3%E7%95%B0%E7%BE%A9%E8%AA%9E" title="同音異義語"><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>同音異義語</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>동음이의어</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>에 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>依</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>의</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>誤變換</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>오변환</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>問題</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>문제</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>解決</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>해결</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>할수있다. 하지만 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>數千字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>수천자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 넘는 <a href="/wiki/%E6%BC%A2%E5%AD%97" title="漢字"><ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>漢字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a> <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>各各</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>각각</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>該當</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>해당</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하는 코드들을 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>別途</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>별도</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 외우고 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>練習</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>연습</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해야한다.
</p>
<h2><span id=".E7.A8.AE.E9.A1.9E.28.EC.A2.85.EB.A5.98.29"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="種類(종류)"><ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>種類</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>종류</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E6%BC%A2%E5%AD%97%E7%9B%B4%E6%8E%A5%E5%85%A5%E5%8A%9B&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 種類(종류)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span id=".E8.81.AF.E6.83.B3.E5.BC.8F.28.EC.97.B0.EC.83.81.EC.8B.9D.29"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="聯想式(연상식)"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>聯想式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>연상식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E6%BC%A2%E5%AD%97%E7%9B%B4%E6%8E%A5%E5%85%A5%E5%8A%9B&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="부분 편집: 聯想式(연상식)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>키<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>組合</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>조합</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 보고 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>入力</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>입력</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>될 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>文字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>문자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>豫想</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>예상</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>할수있는 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>方式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>방식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>. <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>假名漢字變換</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>가명한자변환</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 나오기 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>前</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>전</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>부터도 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>存在</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>존재</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>했다. <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>主</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>주</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>企業</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>기업</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>依</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>의</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>해 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>開發</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개발</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되어 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>商用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>상용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>소프트웨어로서 <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>販賣</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>판매</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되는 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>境遇</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>경우</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 많다. <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>簡單</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>간단</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>例</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>예</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 <ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>漢</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>←カフ(<a href="/wiki/%E6%BC%A2%E6%96%87" title="漢文"><ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>漢文</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한문</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>), <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>←レタ(레터)과 같이 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>入力</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>입력</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하고자하는 글<ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 이미지를 <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>擴張</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>확장</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하여 코드로 삼는다.
</p>
<h3><span id=".E7.84.A1.E8.81.AF.E6.83.B3.E5.BC.8F.28.EB.AC.B4.EB.A0.A8.EC.83.81.EC.8B.9D.29"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="無聯想式(무련상식)"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>無聯想式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>무련상식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E6%BC%A2%E5%AD%97%E7%9B%B4%E6%8E%A5%E5%85%A5%E5%8A%9B&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="부분 편집: 無聯想式(무련상식)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>키<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>組合</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>조합</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>과 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>入力</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>입력</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>될<ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>文字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>문자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>의 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>關係</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>관계</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>弱</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>약</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>方式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>방식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>. <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>聯想式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>연상식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 쓰더라도 어느<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>程度</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>정도</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>熟達</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>숙달</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>될<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>境遇</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>경우</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>聯想</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>연상</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하는 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>過程</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>과정</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>必要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>필요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>없어진다는 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>等</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>등</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>理由</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>이유</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 키<ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>入力</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>입력</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>과 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>出力文字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>출력문자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>聯關</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>연관</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>짓지않는 <ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>代身</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대신</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>關聯性</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>관련성</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 있는 <ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>漢字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>한자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>를 한<ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>番</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>번</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>에 치기 쉽도록 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>配列</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>배열</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>方式</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>방식</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이다. T-Code나 TUT-Code가 여기에 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>屬</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>속</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한다.
</p>
<h2><span id=".E4.BD.BF.E7.94.A8.E7.92.B0.E5.A2.83.28.EC.82.AC.EC.9A.A9.ED.99.98.EA.B2.BD.29"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="使用環境(사용환경)"><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>使用環境</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용환경</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E6%BC%A2%E5%AD%97%E7%9B%B4%E6%8E%A5%E5%85%A5%E5%8A%9B&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="부분 편집: 使用環境(사용환경)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/wiki/%E6%97%A5%E6%9C%AC" title="日本"><ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>日本</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일본</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>에서도 <a href="/wiki/%E5%81%87%E5%90%8D%E5%85%A5%E5%8A%9B" grade52"><rb>商用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>상용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>소프트웨어라 <a href="/index.php?title=%E9%80%B2%E5%85%A5%E9%9A%9C%E5%A3%81&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="進入障壁 (없는 문서)"><ruby
