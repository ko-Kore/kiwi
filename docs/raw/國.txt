<h2><span id="槪要(개요)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E6.A7.AA.E8.A6.81.28.EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94.29"><ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>槪要</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개요</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E5%9C%8B&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 槪要(개요)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/wiki/%E6%9C%83%E6%84%8F%E6%96%87%E5%AD%97" title="會意文字"><ruby class="hanja grade62"><rb>會意文字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>회의문자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>로 <a href="/index.php?title=%EB%82%98%EB%9D%BC&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="나라 (없는 문서)">나라</a>를 뜻한다.
</p>
<h2><span id="字源(자원)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E5.AD.97.E6.BA.90.28.EC.9E.90.EC.9B.90.29"><ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>字源</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자원</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%E5%9C%8B&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 字源(자원)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>元來</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>원래</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는 <ruby class="hanja grade10"><rb>槍</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>창</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 들고 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>城壁</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>성벽</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 지키는 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>模樣</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>모양</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>을 그린 <a href="/index.php?title=%E6%88%96&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="或 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade40 unknown"><rb>或</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>혹</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>이 ‘나라’의 의미로 쓰였으나, 다른 나라가 쳐들어오는 것이 걱정된다는 <ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>意味</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>의미</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 <ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>假借</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>가차</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되어 ‘<ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>或是</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>혹시</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>’ 따위 의미로 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>使用</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>사용</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>되게 되었고, <a href="/index.php?title=%E5%9B%97&amp;action=edit&amp;redlink=1" class="new" title="囗 (없는 문서)"><ruby class="hanja grade0 unknown"><rb>囗</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>국</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a><ruby class="hanja grade70"><rb>字</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>자</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>로 감싼 <ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>國</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>국</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>이 생겨 쓰이게 되었다.
</p>
