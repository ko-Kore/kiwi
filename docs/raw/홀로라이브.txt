<h2><span id="개요"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".EA.B0.9C.EC.9A.94">개요</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%ED%99%80%EB%A1%9C%EB%9D%BC%EC%9D%B4%EB%B8%8C&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="부분 편집: 개요">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>

</p>
<h2><span id="홀로라이브란?"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".ED.99.80.EB.A1.9C.EB.9D.BC.EC.9D.B4.EB.B8.8C.EB.9E.80.3F">홀로라이브란?</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%ED%99%80%EB%A1%9C%EB%9D%BC%EC%9D%B4%EB%B8%8C&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="부분 편집: 홀로라이브란?">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>버츄얼 유튜버다. <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>女性</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>여성</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 홀로라이브 <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>男性</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>남성</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>은 홀로스타즈라고 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>稱</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>칭</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한다.
</p>
<h2><span id="國家(국가)"></span><span class="mw-headline" id=".E5.9C.8B.E5.AE.B6.28.EA.B5.AD.EA.B0.80.29"><ruby class="hanja grade72"><rb>國家</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>국가</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/index.php?title=%ED%99%80%EB%A1%9C%EB%9D%BC%EC%9D%B4%EB%B8%8C&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="부분 편집: 國家(국가)">편집</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>홀로라이브는 <ruby class="hanja grade60"><rb>日本</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>일본</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>,인도네시아,EN<ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>圈</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>권</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby> 총 3<ruby class="hanja grade42"><rb>個</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>개</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>存在</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>존재</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>한다. <ruby class="hanja grade52"><rb>元來</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>원래</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>는 <ruby class="hanja grade80"><rb>中國</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>중국</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>도 <ruby class="hanja grade40"><rb>存在</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>존재</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>했으나 <ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>桐生</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>동생</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>코코,<ruby class="hanja grade32"><rb>赤井</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>적정</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>하아토<a href="/wiki/%ED%95%98%EB%82%98%EC%9D%98_%E4%B8%AD%E5%9C%8B" title="하나의 中國"><ruby class="hanja grade20"><rb>臺灣言及事件</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>대만언급사건</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby></a>으로 <ruby class="hanja grade30"><rb>契約破棄</rb><rp>(</rp><rt>계약파기</rt><rp>)</rp></ruby>가 됬다.
